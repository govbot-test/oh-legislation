Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 69 2025-2026 Representative Russo A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Upper Arlington High School ice hockey team on winning the 2025 OHSAA State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to salute the Upper Arlington High School ice hockey team as the 2025 Ohio High School Athletic Association State Champion; and WHEREAS, Establishing a reputation for its skill and tenacity, the Upper Arlington High School ice hockey team is an impressive group of athletes whose talent and teamwork are unmatched. At the 2025 State Championship final, the squad bested St. Ignatius 5-2 to win the program’s first state title in nearly fifty years, and this victory will be celebrated for many years to come; and WHEREAS, The members of the Upper Arlington High School ice hockey team rose to the occasion and proved themselves to be a dynamic force in high school sports. Truly, these outstanding athletes can be proud of the accomplishments that were hard-won and deserved throughout the season; and WHEREAS, By remaining faithful to the highest standards of sportsmanship and teamwork, the members of the Upper Arlington High School ice hockey team have primed themselves under the guidance of Coach Craig Hagkull and his team of assistant coaches to reach their full potential. Indeed, the Golden Bears have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in all of their endeavors; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize the Upper Arlington High School ice hockey team on its historic success and honor the athletes and coaches for their tremendous prowess; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Upper Arlington High School ice hockey team.

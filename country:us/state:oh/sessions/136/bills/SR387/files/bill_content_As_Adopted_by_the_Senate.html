A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Rileigh Scarvelli as a 2026 Division II State Track and Field Champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Rileigh Scarvelli on winning the 2026 Division II State Championship in the shot put; and

WHEREAS, A member of the Avon Lake High School girls track and field team, Rileigh Scarvelli has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in her events, and as a result, she placed first in the shot put with a toss of 45 feet, 3 inches at this year's Division II State Meet; and

WHEREAS, Rileigh Scarvelli's accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of high praise; and

WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to Rileigh Scarvelli on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in track and field will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Rileigh Scarvelli as the 2026 Division II State Track and Field Champion in the shot put and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Rileigh Scarvelli.

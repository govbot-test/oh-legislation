Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 326 2025-2026 Senator Gavarone A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Laibe Electric Technology on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Laibe Electric Technology on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to Laibe Electric Technology, for throughout the past one hundred years, it has attained a remarkable record of service. Founded in Toledo in 1926, the company has become a leader in commercial, industrial, and institutional electrical services, providing solutions for clients throughout Northwest Ohio, Southeast Michigan, and beyond to keep them operating safely, reliably, and efficiently; and WHEREAS, The success of Laibe Electric Technology is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and hard‑working employees. Due to their vision and initiative, this respected institution has earned the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied customers, and it is truly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Over the years, Laibe Electric Technology has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area. We are certain that as this noteworthy enterprise maintains its unfaltering dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to prosper and will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Laibe Electric Technology on its One Hundredth Anniversary and salute it as one of Ohio’s finest businesses; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Laibe Electric Technology.

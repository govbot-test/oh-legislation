Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 389 2025-2026 Representative Craig A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team as the 2026 NCSAA Division 5C Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team on winning the 2026 National Christian School Athletic Association Division 5C Championship; and WHEREAS, The Wooster Christian School boys basketball team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of this exceptional squad. In winning this year’s National Christian School Athletic Association Division 5C Tournament, the Eagles have amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of their achievements; and WHEREAS, This triumph of the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional coaching staff, as well as the support of their Eagles community, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletes, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in basketball; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team on its 2026 NCSAA Division 5C Championship title and count its players and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team.

hr450_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 450 2025-2026 Representative Demetriou A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Mill Distributors, Inc. on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Mill Distributors, Inc., on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to Mill Distributors, for throughout the past one hundred years, it has attained a remarkable record of service. Founded in 1926, this family‑owned company offers furniture, fixtures, equipment, window coverings, bed, bath, and table linens, and other products to create inspired commercial environments for a variety of industries, and it was named the 2017 Business of the Year by the Aurora Chamber of Commerce; and WHEREAS, The success of Mill Distributors is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and hard-working employees. Due to their vision and initiative, this respected institution has earned the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied customers, and it is truly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Over the years, Mill Distributors has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area. We are certain that as this noteworthy enterprise maintains its unfaltering dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to prosper and will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Mill Distributors, Inc., on its One Hundredth Anniversary and salute it as one of Ohio's finest businesses; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Mill Distributors, Inc.

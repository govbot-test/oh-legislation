hr431_00_IN As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 431 2025-2026 Representative Miller, J. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Lorain Rotary Club on its One Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Lorain Rotary Club on its Centennial, celebrated May 9, 2026; and WHEREAS, One hundred years is a distinctive milestone on which the Lorain Rotary Club can enthusiastically celebrate its mission, for through its philanthropic, civic, and social activities, it has enhanced the quality of life in our society. Founded in 1926, this iconic club has overcome challenges and persevered by remaining a relevant resource for the community, and it has paid homage to its long and distinguished history through its thoughtful outreach to the Lorain area; and WHEREAS, One of many reasons that the State of Ohio continues to prosper is because of organizations such as the Lorain Rotary Club. For a century, this exemplary group has engaged in an array of worthwhile activities and projects that have met a number of local needs, and we are certain that it will continue to positively impact our state for generations to come; and WHEREAS, The members of the Lorain Rotary Club can always be relied upon to favor a worthy cause, and these exceptional people have made a lasting impact on the community through their admirable resolve and vision. We look with optimism to a society that will thrive as a result of the initiative of all those who have comprised the organization, both past and present; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Lorain Rotary Club on its One Hundredth Anniversary and salute its leadership and members as fine Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Lorain Rotary Club.

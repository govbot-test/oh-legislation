Section 1:
As Reported by the House Transportation Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 541 2025-2026 Representative McClain Cosponsors: Representatives Willis, Daniels, Grim, Lorenz, Newman, Robb Blasdel, Rogers, Troy To enact section 5534.474 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of U.S. Route 42 in Morrow County as the "Deputy Daniel "Weston" Sherrer Memorial Highway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.474 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.474. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the eastbound and westbound lanes of United States route number forty-two, between Young avenue and Sun Fish road, in Morrow county, shall be known as the "Deputy Daniel "Weston" Sherrer Memorial Highway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

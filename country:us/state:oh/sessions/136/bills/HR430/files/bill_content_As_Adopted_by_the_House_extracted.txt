Section 1:
hr430_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 430 2025-2026 Representative Willis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Springfield Fire Rescue Division as the 2026 Ohio Fire Department of the Year. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Springfield Fire Rescue Division on being presented with the 2026 Ohio Fire Department of the Year Award by the Ohio Department of Commerce Division of State Fire Marshal and the Ohio Department of Public Safety Division of EMS; and WHEREAS, This prestigious award is a fitting tribute to the Springfield Fire Rescue Division, for throughout its history, it has achieved a praiseworthy record of service to local residents. The accomplishments of this outstanding department are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on the institution itself but also on its diligent members and on the entire community; and WHEREAS, While many people are content to take a passive role in life, the members of the Springfield Fire Rescue Division have risked their own safety to ensure the well-being of their fellow citizens, and they have earned the gratitude and respect of the community they have so capably served. We are certain that as they maintain their dedication to service, these individuals will continue in the tradition of excellence which has long been the agency's hallmark; and WHEREAS, Countless citizens in the community owe their safety, and some their very lives, to the volunteers of the Springfield Fire Rescue Division, who have displayed outstanding courage and initiative in the performance of their duties. Their enthusiasm and devotion are truly astounding; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the members of the Springfield Fire Rescue Division for their loyal service to the community and salute them as exemplary Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Springfield Fire Rescue Division.

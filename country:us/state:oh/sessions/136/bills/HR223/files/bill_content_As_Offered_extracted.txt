Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 223 2025-2026 Representative Salvo A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Rotary Club of Newcomerstown on its One Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Rotary Club of Newcomerstown on the commemoration of its Centennial, September 29, 2025; and WHEREAS, One hundred years is a distinctive milestone on which the Rotary Club of Newcomerstown can enthusiastically celebrate its mission to enrich its members and the community with philanthropic, civic, and social activities. Founded in 1925, this iconic club has overcome challenges and persevered by remaining a relevant resource for the community, and it has paid homage to its long and distinguished history through its thoughtful outreach to Newcomerstown and the surrounding area; and WHEREAS, One of many reasons that the State of Ohio continues to prosper is because of such organizations as the Rotary Club of Newcomerstown. Among its earliest endeavors, the group held benefits and raised funds for charitable causes, and it went on to contribute to the growth of the area by purchasing land to be used as a community park, supporting the annual Cy Young Bike Tour, the Temperance Tavern Museum expansion, the local Little League program, and sponsoring countless scholarships for area youth; and WHEREAS, The members of the Rotary Club of Newcomerstown can always be relied upon to favor a worthy cause, and these exceptional people have made a lasting impact on the community through their admirable resolve and vision. We look with optimism to a society that will thrive as a result of the initiatives of all those who have comprised the organization, both past and present; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Rotary Club of Newcomerstown on its One Hundredth Anniversary and salute its leadership and members as fine Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Rotary Club of Newcomerstown.

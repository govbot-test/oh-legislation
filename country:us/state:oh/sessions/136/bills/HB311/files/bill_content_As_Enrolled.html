(136th General Assembly)

(House Bill Number 311)

AN ACT

To enact section 5.2322 of the Revised Code to designate the third full week of May as "EMS week in Ohio."

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Ohio:

Section 1. That section 5.2322 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows:

Sec. 5.2322. The third full week of May is designated as "EMS week in Ohio."

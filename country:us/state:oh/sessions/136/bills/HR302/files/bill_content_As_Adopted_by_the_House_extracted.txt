Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 302 2025-2026 Representative Baker A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Madeira High School girls soccer team as the 2025 Division V State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Madeira High School girls soccer team on winning the 2025 Division V State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Madeira High School girls soccer team have established themselves as truly exceptional competitors by combining talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship. With admirable dedication, the Amazons have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in soccer, and as a result, they defeated a tough Chippewa team by the score of 3-0 to win this year’s Division V State Championship, their second consecutive state title and the third in program history; and WHEREAS, The achievements and leadership of the Madeira High School girls soccer team are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the players, their families, their school community, and their head coach, Daniel Brady. Indeed, in leading the team to victory, Coach Brady has helped these athletes to develop great talent by instilling the valuable lessons of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are pleased to pay tribute to the exemplary athletes and the outstanding coach of the Madeira High School girls soccer team for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Madeira High School girls soccer team on its tremendous 2025 season and salute the members and coach as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Madeira High School girls soccer team.

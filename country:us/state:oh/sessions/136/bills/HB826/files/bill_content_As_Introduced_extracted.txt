Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 826 2025-2026 Representative Williams To enact section 2921.311 of the Revised Code to create the offense of obstructing federal immigration enforcement. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 2921.311 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 2921.311. (A) No public official shall purposely prevent, obstruct, or delay federal immigration enforcement. (B) Whoever violates this section is guilty of obstructing federal immigration enforcement, a felony of the fifth degree.

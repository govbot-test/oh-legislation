As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 52 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the National Society Daughters of the American Revolution Elizabeth Sherman Reese Chapter on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the National Society Daughters of the American Revolution Elizabeth Sherman Reese Chapter on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to the National Society Daughters of the American Revolution Elizabeth Sherman Reese Chapter, for throughout the past one hundred twenty-five years, it has achieved a remarkable record of service to Lancaster and the surrounding area. Founded in 1900, the chapter has participated in numerous initiatives that support veterans and foster preservation, education, and patriotism, and the accomplishments of this organization are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the group itself but also on its dedicated members, both past and present, and on the communities it has served; and WHEREAS, Over the years, the National Society Daughters of the American Revolution Elizabeth Sherman Reese Chapter has enhanced the quality of life for many, and through its generous contributions, it has earned the respect and gratitude of the countless citizens it has so capably supported. We are certain that as this worthy chapter maintains its unfaltering commitment to service, it will continue in the tradition of excellence that has been the hallmark of DAR since its establishment; and WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unceasing efforts of organizations such as the National Society Daughters of the American Revolution Elizabeth Sherman Reese Chapter that Ohio has gained a reputation as a state that is responsive to the needs of its citizens and has remained a pleasant place in which to live and work. The chapter is truly deserving of high praise; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the National Society Daughters of the American Revolution Elizabeth Sherman Reese Chapter on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary and extend best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the National Society Daughters of the American Revolution Elizabeth Sherman Reese Chapter.

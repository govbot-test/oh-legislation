A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring The Ohio State University dance team on winning a 2025 UCA & UDA College Cheerleading and Dance Team National title.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to The Ohio State University dance team on earning first place in the Division IA jazz category at the 2025 Universal Cheerleaders Association & Universal Dance Association College Cheerleading and Dance Team National Championship; and

WHEREAS, Establishing a reputation for its skill and tenacity, The Ohio State University dance team is an impressive group of competitors whose talents are unmatched. At this year’s UCA & UDA College Cheerleading and Dance Team National Championship, the Buckeyes won their second consecutive crown in the Division IA jazz category, as well as earned second-place honors in the pom category, and these achievements will be celebrated for many years to come; and

WHEREAS, The members of The Ohio State University dance team rose to the occasion and proved themselves to be capable of meeting and overcoming all challenges they faced. Truly, these outstanding athletes can be proud of their hard-won and well-deserved accomplishments; and

WHEREAS, By remaining faithful to the highest standards of sportsmanship and teamwork, the members of The Ohio State University dance team primed themselves under the guidance of Coach Melissa McGhee to reach their full potential. Indeed, the Buckeyes have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in all of their endeavors; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, heartily recognize The Ohio State University dance team on its national title and praise the strength of this squad and its participating athletes; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to The Ohio State University dance team.

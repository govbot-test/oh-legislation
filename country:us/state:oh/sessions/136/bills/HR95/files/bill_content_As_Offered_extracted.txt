Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 95 2025-2026 Representative Young A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Evan Wierwille on securing a 2025 Division II State Swimming Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Evan Wierwille as a 2025 Division II State Swimming Champion in the 100-yard backstroke para event; and WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication as a freshman member of the Centerville High School swim team, Evan Wierwille has certainly distinguished himself as one of the most talented swimmers in Ohio. At this year’s Division II State meet, he won first place in the 100-yard backstroke para event with a time of 1:09.14 and second place in the 50-yard freestyle para event with a time of 27.32; and WHEREAS, Evan Wierwille has conscientiously applied himself to the quest for excellence as an athlete, training and practicing diligently in order to ensure peak conditioning. Time and again, he has risen to the occasion, maintaining his morale, composure, and good sportsmanship, and he has gained the respect of even his most determined rivals; and WHEREAS, Evan Wierwille has consistently exhibited strength, speed, and stamina in his impressive athletic encounters, and by amply demonstrating his unflagging determination, he has set an example for others to emulate. Indeed, he is an exemplary competitor, and his resolve to achieve state champion status is deserving of esteem; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, honor Evan Wierwille on his outstanding achievement and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Evan Wierwille.

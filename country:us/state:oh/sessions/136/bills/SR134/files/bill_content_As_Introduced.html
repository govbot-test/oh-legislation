A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Hunter Spangler on winning a 2025 Division III State Track and Field Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Hunter Spangler as the 2025 Division III State Track and Field Champion in the shot put; and

WHEREAS, Hunter Spangler’s outstanding ability and spirited drive, as well as his quickness and tenacity, have distinguished him as a superior athlete and an exemplary member of the Liberty Center High School track and field team. At this year’s Division III State Meet, he placed first in the shot put with a throw of 63-1.25 feet, and he has gained the respect and admiration of all those who have witnessed him in action; and

WHEREAS, Few events embody the spirit of individual accomplishment more than shot put. Athletes such as Hunter Spangler exemplify the qualities necessary for excellence in any sport, including discipline, conditioning, and focus. Indeed, throughout long hours of practice and competition, he has consistently demonstrated the talent and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true competitor; and

WHEREAS, Through his participation in sports, Hunter Spangler has learned lessons of perseverance, hard work, and fair and honest competition that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to him throughout his lifetime. Time and again, he has risen to the occasion, maintaining his morale, composure, and good sportsmanship, and he has set a fine example that is sure to inspire future generations; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Hunter Spangler on his outstanding performance at the Division III State Track and Field Championship and offer best wishes for success; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Hunter Spangler.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 229 2025-2026 Senator Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Avon High School boys soccer team on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Avon High School boys soccer team as the 2025 Division II State Champion; and WHEREAS, The members of the Avon High School boys soccer team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly exceptional competitors. The Eagles won the 2025 Division II State Championship with a final score of 1-0 in overtime against Kings High School, and the unwavering commitment of the players and coaches has earned them the esteem of many. They are certainly deserving of high praise for completing the season with an impressive record of 17-3-2; and WHEREAS, Coach Tyler Peairs and Coach Chris Dore have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the Avon High School boys soccer team to victory, and their skills are reflected in the squad’s outstanding accomplishments. Due largely to their tireless efforts, the athletes have distinguished themselves on the field, and they have received respect and admiration from all who know them; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Avon High School boys soccer team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Avon High School boys soccer team as the 2025 Division II State Champion and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Avon High School boys soccer team.

hb452_01_RH As Reported by the House Veterans and Military Development Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 452 2025-2026 Representatives Thomas, C., Lawson-Rowe Cosponsors: Representatives Brennan, Brent, Brewer, Brownlee, Piccolantonio, Grim, Gross, Hall, D., Jarrells, McNally, Miller, J., Rader, Robinson, Russo, Sims, Sweeney, Synenberg, Tims, Upchurch, Williams, Willis To enact section 5.2322 of the Revised Code to designate October 1st as "761st Tank Battalion Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2322 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2322. The first day of October is designated as "761st Tank Battalion Day."

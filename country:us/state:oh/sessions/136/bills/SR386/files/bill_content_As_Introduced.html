Senator Manning


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Jackson Hughes on winning the 2026 Division II State Long Jump Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize Jackson Hughes as the 2026 Division II State Track and Field Champion in the long jump; and

WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication during the 2026 season, Jackson Hughes certainly distinguished himself as one of the most talented athletes in Ohio. At this year's Division II State Meet, he won the long jump event with a leap of 23 feet, 5.5 inches, and he is worthy of acknowledgement; and

WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Jackson Hughes has consistently demonstrated the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. A standout on the Avon High School track and field team, he has inspired his teammates, thrilled his fans, gained the respect of his opponents, and made his coaches, family, and community proud; and

WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. Clearly, Jackson Hughes has learned much about excellence as an athlete, and he has made a significant impact on high school track and field in our state; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to Jackson Hughes on his track and field success and offer best wishes for the years ahead; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Jackson Hughes.

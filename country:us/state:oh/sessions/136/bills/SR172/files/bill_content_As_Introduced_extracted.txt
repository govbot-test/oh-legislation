Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 172 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer Cosponsors: Senators Antonio, Blackshear, Blessing, Brenner, Chavez, Cirino, Craig, Cutrona, DeMora, Gavarone, Hicks-Hudson, Huffman, Ingram, Johnson, Koehler, Landis, Lang, Liston, Manchester, Manning, McColley, O'Brien, Patton, Reineke, Reynolds, Roegner, Romanchuk, Smith, Timken, Weinstein, Wilkin, Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring James C. Lilly for his service to the State of Ohio. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to James C. Lilly on the memorable occasion of his retirement from the Ohio Legislative Service Commission, July 31, 2025; and WHEREAS, Jim Lilly’s sterling reputation and dedication to public service have been a source of pride throughout the whole of his more than thirty-two-year tenure with the Legislative Service Commission. Shepherding the agency through some of its most challenging times as well as witnessing its evolution, he has set himself apart as a consummate professional and an asset to our state’s legislative endeavors, and he has been celebrated for being an earnest supporter of Ohio and its elected officials; and WHEREAS, Jim Lilly has been one of the Ohio Legislative Service Commission’s most capable ambassadors, and by giving graciously of his time, skill, and expertise, he has demonstrated a praiseworthy commitment to the success of the agency as well as to the effectiveness of the Ohio General Assembly; and WHEREAS, Jim Lilly’s expansive professional endeavors have been instrumental to the Ohio Legislative Service Commission, to the Ohio General Assembly, and to the public at-large, but most of all, he has been greatly admired by his colleagues and all those who have had the pleasure of working with him. Throughout his distinguished career, he has embodied the tenets of dependability, sincerity, and altruism that have benefited his peers and his fellow citizens, and his exemplary work ethic has become his enduring legacy; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend a heartfelt salute to James C. Lilly, a fine Ohioan, and offer best wishes for a happy, healthy retirement; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to James C. Lilly.

hr423_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 423 2025-2026 Representative Russo A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Ruby Remati and Keana Hunter as the 2026 U.S. Collegiate Champions in the artistic swimming duet event. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Ruby Remati and Keana Hunter on winning the 2026 U.S. Collegiate Championships duet event in artistic swimming; and WHEREAS, Standout members of The Ohio State University artistic swimming team, Ruby Remati and Keana Hunter are to be heartily commended, for by scoring 264.0933 points, they decisively earned the 2026 U.S. Collegiate Championship in the duet category. Without exception, the duo prepared conscientiously in pursuit of this title, devoting countless hours to training in order to ensure peak conditioning, and clearly, this demanding regimen has paid handsome dividends; and WHEREAS, Athletes such as Ruby Remati and Keana Hunter exemplify the qualities necessary for excellence in any sport, including agility, conditioning, and focus. Throughout long hours of practice and competition, they have consistently demonstrated the talent and motivation that are the hallmarks of true champions; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing a key role in the total education of youth, we are happy to salute Ruby Remati and Keana Hunter on an exemplary season. Indeed, the maturity and experience they have gained through their participation in artistic swimming will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to Ruby Remati and Keana Hunter on securing the 2026 U.S. Collegiate Duet Championship in artistic swimming and offer best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit duly authenticated copies of this Resolution to Ruby Remati and Keana Hunter.

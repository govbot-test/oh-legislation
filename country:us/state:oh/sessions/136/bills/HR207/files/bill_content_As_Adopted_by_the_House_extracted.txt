Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 207 2025-2026 Representative Klopfenstein A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Crane Township on its Bicentennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Crane Township on its Bicentennial, celebrated August 16, 2025; and WHEREAS, Over the past two hundred years, Crane Township has experienced dramatic growth and change, and yet, commendably, it still radiates vitality, optimism, and a charm all its own. Established in 1825 and named in honor of Oliver Crane, the township has adapted to meet each new challenge, and clearly, the community’s outstanding schools, remarkable businesses, and astute civic organizations both embody and thrive upon the values of which our flag is an emblem; and WHEREAS, Even as they have exuberantly embraced the promise of a bright future, the residents of Crane Township have never relinquished their appreciation for the worthiest virtues of the past. Indeed, they have managed to nurture a fresh perspective and a positive outlook that continue to endear this distinctive place to all who know it well; and WHEREAS, Every state depends upon such vibrant, durable communities as Crane Township for its own survival and stability. Although having known adversity and tribulation, it also has been favored with its share of blessings, and in the years to come, the municipality is sure to remain on its admirable course and to continue to transform challenges into opportunities; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Crane Township as it commemorates its Two Hundredth Anniversary and offer our best wishes for its perennial good fortune; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Crane Township.

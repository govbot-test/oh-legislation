Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 539 2025-2026 Representative Lorenz To enact section 5.2324 of the Revised Code to designate February 4 as Thaddeus Kościuszko Day. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2324 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2324. The fourth day of February is designated as Thaddeus Kościuszko Day, in honor of the Polish general, military engineer, and hero of the American revolutionary war, who was born on that day in 1746, and who was described by Thomas Jefferson, "as pure a son of liberty, as I have ever known."

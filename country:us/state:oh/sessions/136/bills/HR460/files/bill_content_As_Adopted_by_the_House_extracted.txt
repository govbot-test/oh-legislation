Section 1:
hr460_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 460 2025-2026 Representative Jarrells A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Frostop on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Frostop on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is, indeed, a fitting tribute to Frostop, for over the past one hundred years, this fine business has achieved a praiseworthy record of service to the communities it calls home. This company has continually demonstrated its exemplary commitment to the highest standards, customer satisfaction, and competitive pricing, and its dedication to the best interests of its clientele has been evident in all its transactions; and WHEREAS, The success of Frostop is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and hard-working employees. Due to their vision and initiative, this respected brand of small batch root beers and creme sodas has earned the gratitude and appreciation of generations of satisfied customers, and they are truly deserving of high praise for being esteemed ambassadors for the company; and WHEREAS, Over the past one hundred years, Frostop has enhanced the quality of life for many through its drive-ins as well as its root beers and cremes. We are certain that as this worthy enterprise maintains its dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to prosper and uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Frostop on its One Hundredth Anniversary and salute it as a pillar of the local economic landscape; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Frostop.

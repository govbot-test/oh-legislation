A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Lake Anna Young Men’s Christian Association on its One Hundredth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Lake Anna Young Men’s Christian Association on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, Celebration of this prestigious milestone is, indeed, a fitting tribute to the Lake Anna YMCA, for throughout the last century, it has achieved a remarkable record of service to the area. The organization’s accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on the association itself but also on its members and volunteers and on the community; and

WHEREAS, Since its founding on February 13, 1924, the Lake Anna YMCA has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area, and its generous contributions have earned it the gratitude and respect of the many citizens it has so capably served. We are certain that as this fine agency maintains its unfaltering dedication to service, it will continue in the tradition of excellence that has become its hallmark; and

WHEREAS, All those associated with the Lake Anna YMCA are to be commended for their foresight, commitment, and selfless donations of time, energy, and ability far beyond what was required or expected. These fine people have set an example of concerned and responsible citizenship that is worthy of emulation; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Lake Anna Young Men’s Christian Association on its Centennial and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Lake Anna Young Men’s Christian Association.

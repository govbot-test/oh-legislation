A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Tyjon Cousins as the 2025 National Little Brother of the Year.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Tyjon Cousins on being named the 2025 National Little Brother of the Year by Big Brothers Big Sisters of America; and

WHEREAS, Tyjon Cousins is, indeed, a remarkable individual, and he has certainly combined dedication and resolve in all his endeavors. He has maintained a positive outlook and developed an enduring relationship with his Big Brother, Norm Boyd, and it is easy to understand why his engaging personality and tenacious resolve have won him the recognition and prestige of being awarded the 2025 National Little Brother of the Year; and

WHEREAS, Known for being an exceptional young man and ambassador for Big Brothers Big Sisters of Greater Cincinnati, Tyjon Cousins has earned the respect and esteem of many for his strong aspirations, his commendable concern, and his unparalleled character. Indeed, he has revealed a capacity for hope, potential, and achievement that promises to equal virtually any challenge, and he is deserving of high praise for his enthusiastic efforts; and

WHEREAS, Tyjon Cousins’ indomitable nature has enabled him to persevere, and with the help of his Big Brother and others who have offered guidance and encouragement, he has made a lasting impression by continually excelling. To his credit, he has shown an inspirational commitment to better himself, and he can be proud of his success and for prevailing in the face of adversity; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Tyjon Cousins as the 2025 National Little Brother of the Year and salute him as one of our state’s finest young citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Tyjon Cousins.

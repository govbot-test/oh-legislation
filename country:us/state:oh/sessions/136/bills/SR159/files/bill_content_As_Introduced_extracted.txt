Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 159 2025-2026 Senator Romanchuk A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Ontario High School girls 4x400-meter relay team on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Ontario High School girls 4x400-meter relay team as the 2025 Division II State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Ontario High School girls 4x400-meter relay team has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for the spirited and highly motivated performance of its members. Throughout the season, Arialyn Bischak, Tessa Crum, Sasha Bulakovski, and Audrey Mahon worked diligently to improve their skills on the track, and as a result, they accomplished their goals, bringing home this year’s Division II State Championship title with a time of 3:52.39, setting a new school record; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined team, the Ontario Warriors have developed into a remarkably efficient group of runners whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their coach, Rachel Miller; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of members and coaches alike. The runners of the Ontario High School girls 4x400‑meter relay team have exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Ontario High School girls 4x400-meter relay team on its 2025 Division II State Championship and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Ontario High School girls 4x400-meter relay team.

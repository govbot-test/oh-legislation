hr464_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 464 2025-2026 Representative Ritter A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Sophia Szolosi as a three-time 2026 Division III State Champion in girls track and field. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Sophia Szolosi on winning the 2026 Division III State Championship in the 800-meter, 1,600‑meter, and 3,200-meter events; and WHEREAS, A senior member of the Athens High School girls track and field team, Sophia Szolosi has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency as a runner, and as a result, she secured three titles in this year's Division III State Championship with impressive times of 2:08.82 in the 800-meter, 4:48.45 in the 1,600-meter, and 10:17.47 in the 3,200-meter events; and WHEREAS, Sophia Szolosi's accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coach, and the whole of the Bulldogs community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Sophia Szolosi on an exemplary season. Indeed, the maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in sports will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Sophia Szolosi on her astounding performance at the 2026 Division III State Championship and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Sophia Szolosi.

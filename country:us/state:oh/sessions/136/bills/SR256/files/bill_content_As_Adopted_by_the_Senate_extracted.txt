Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 256 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Newark Catholic High School girls volleyball team on winning the 2025 Division VI State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Newark Catholic High School girls volleyball team as the 2025 Division VI State Champion; and WHEREAS, The members of the Newark Catholic High School girls volleyball team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly remarkable competitors. This season, the Green Wave secured the 2025 Division VI State Championship, and the unwavering commitment of the players and coaches has earned them the esteem of many. They are certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, The coaches of the Newark Catholic High School girls volleyball team have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the squad to victory, and their skill is reflected in its outstanding accomplishments. Due largely to their tireless efforts, the members have distinguished themselves on the court, and they have received respect and admiration from all who know them; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Newark Catholic High School girls volleyball team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Newark Catholic High School girls volleyball team as the 2025 Division VI State Champion and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Newark Catholic High School girls volleyball team.

As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 58 2025-2026 Representative McClain A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works, Ltd. on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works, Ltd., on the occasion of its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works is, indeed, deserving of high praise, for throughout fifteen decades of operation, this fine company has attained a noteworthy record of accomplishment. Indeed, this enterprise has earned the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied customers, and its success is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its owner, James Patrick, on its hard-working employees, and on the Bucyrus community; and WHEREAS, Since its establishment, Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area, and it has consistently demonstrated an exemplary commitment to the highest standards of workmanship. Indeed, it has gained a sterling reputation for producing a variety of quality products, including kettles, hods, decorative bowls, fire stands, tympani shells, and waste paper baskets, and we have no doubt that as this worthy venture maintains its dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to grow and prosper; and WHEREAS, Over the years, the proprietors of Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works, both past and present, have epitomized America’s entrepreneurial spirit, and it is through the contributions of individuals such as these that our state has gained a reputation as a friendly, productive, and pleasant place in which to live. We are certain that in years to come, all those associated with this company will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been the hallmark of the business; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works, Ltd., on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Bucyrus Copper Kettle Works, Ltd.

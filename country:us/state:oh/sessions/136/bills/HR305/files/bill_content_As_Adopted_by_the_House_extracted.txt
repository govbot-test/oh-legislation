Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 305 2025-2026 Representative McClain A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team as the 2025 National FFA Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team on winning the 2025 National FFA Championship; and WHEREAS, The members of the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team are a dedicated and committed collection of students, and they leave behind a fantastic legacy for future FFA teams by becoming the first Ohio team to win this national competition. Under the leadership of their advisor, Erin Wollett, members Jaxon Ongalibang, Madison Garrabrant, Bristal Cain, Mason Newsome, Victoria Kovacs, Alayna Seavolt, and Ky Barlow have displayed a degree of determination and motivation of which they can be proud, and they have set an example of excellence that is worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, FFA combines the wisdom and leadership of adulthood with the enthusiasm and optimism of youth so that our young people will be better prepared to assume meaningful, rewarding roles in society. It is certainly encouraging to know that the future of Ohio agriculture and agribusiness lies, to a large extent, in the capable hands of conscientious individuals such as the members of the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team; and WHEREAS, All those associated with the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team warrant the highest praise, for they have demonstrated exceptional diligence and enthusiasm in preparing for the rigors of competition, as well as an unwavering dedication to the goals and principles of the National FFA Organization. The maturity and experience these members have gained through their participation in FFA will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of tomorrow, and we are certain that their future holds great promise; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team on its success and salute all those involved with the group as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team.

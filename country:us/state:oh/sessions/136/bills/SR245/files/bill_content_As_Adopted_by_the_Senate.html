A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the American Association of University Women of Toledo on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the American Association of University Women of Toledo on the occasion of its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, For one hundred twenty-five years, the American Association of University Women of Toledo has made significant efforts to build a brighter future for Ohioans by working to remove gender equity barriers and biases. Indeed, this group has aptly distinguished itself through its advocacy for women to receive fair pay and economic opportunity as well as through its connections with community partners, local and state legislators, and university bodies, resulting in effective training, education, and policy examination; and

WHEREAS, The American Association of University Women of Toledo has established a legacy for serving as an important resource, and in doing so, it has helped to build a stronger Ohio. On the occasion of its anniversary, the organization has an opportunity to reaffirm its goals and formulate policies in response to ongoing challenges, and we are confident that AAUW of Toledo will remain steadfast in continuing to value the best interests of people across the state; and

WHEREAS, It is through the unceasing efforts of groups such as the American Association of University Women of Toledo that Ohio has prospered, and we are proud to pay tribute to its initiatives, which have reflected vision, collaboration, and promise. This organization has worked diligently to contribute to our state’s reputation for being responsive to the growing needs of Ohioans, and this fine branch is truly worthy of praise; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize the American Association of University Women of Toledo on one hundred twenty-five years of exemplary service and salute it as an estimable Ohio endeavor; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the American Association of University Women of Toledo.

A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Tom Hamilton on receiving the 2025 National Baseball Hall of Fame and Museum Ford C. Frick Award.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Tom Hamilton on being presented with the 2025 Ford C. Frick Award for excellence in broadcasting by the National Baseball Hall of Fame and Museum; and

WHEREAS, Selection for this prestigious distinction is an honor of which Tom Hamilton can be proud, for he has been lauded for his outstanding commitment to excellence, quality of broadcasting abilities, reverence for the game, popularity with fans, and recognition by his peers. As the radio announcer calling Cleveland Guardians games since 1990, he has become known for his play-by-play and passionate calls of some of the franchise’s most historic moments, and we applaud him on his exemplary record of accomplishment; and

WHEREAS, Tom Hamilton’s tremendous efforts have helped to guarantee a high level of success for all projects with which he has been involved and have earned him the respect and esteem of many. It is through the unsurpassed dedication of conscientious professionals such as he that our state continues to grow and prosper and remains responsive to the needs of its citizens; and

WHEREAS, Demonstrating a sincere concern for the future of the Cleveland area, Tom Hamilton has given back to the community far more than he has gleaned, and his selfless endeavors stand as the hallmark of responsible citizenship. He has amply displayed his unwavering determination, as well as his astute knowledge of the game, and it is only fitting that he be acknowledged for the significant contributions he has made to the sport of baseball and to the quality of life in our state; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Tom Hamilton on his national accolade and salute him as one of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Tom Hamilton.

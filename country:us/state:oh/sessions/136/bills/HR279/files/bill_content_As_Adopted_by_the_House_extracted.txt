Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 279 2025-2026 Representative Russo A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Upper Arlington High School girls soccer team on placing first at the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Upper Arlington High School girls soccer team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Upper Arlington High School girls soccer team has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for the spirited and highly motivated performance of its members. Throughout the season, the athletes worked diligently to improve their skills on the field, and as a result, the Golden Bears accomplished their goals and brought home this year’s state championship; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined team, the Upper Arlington Golden Bears have developed into a remarkably efficient group of players whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their coach, Jared Dombrowski; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of players and coaches alike. The members of the Upper Arlington High School girls soccer team have exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Upper Arlington High School girls soccer team on its 2025 Division I State Championship and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Upper Arlington High School girls soccer team.

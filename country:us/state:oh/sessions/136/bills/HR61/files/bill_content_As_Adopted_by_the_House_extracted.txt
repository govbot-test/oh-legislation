Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 61 2025-2026 Representative Plummer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Vandalia-Butler High School girls bowling team on securing the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Vandalia-Butler High School girls bowling team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Vandalia-Butler High School girls bowling team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year’s Division I State Tournament, members Elizabeth Koewler, Sage Malott, Jolene Powers, Ashlin Conley, Mary Williams, Melayah Burton, Hailei McQuaid, and Kierstyn Priser amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse as they secured the state title; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of the Vandalia-Butler High School girls bowling team’s exceptional head coach, Mark Seelbaugh, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Vandalia-Butler High School girls bowling team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in bowling; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Vandalia-Butler High School girls bowling team on its 2025 Division I State Championship title and count its members and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Vandalia-Butler High School girls bowling team.

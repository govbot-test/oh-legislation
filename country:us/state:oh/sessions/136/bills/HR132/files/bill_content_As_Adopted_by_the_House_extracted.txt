Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 132 2025-2026 Representative Russo A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Kameron Nelson on winning the 2025 NCAA Men’s Gymnastics Vault Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Kameron Nelson as the winner of the gold medal in the vault at the 2025 National Collegiate Athletic Association Men’s Gymnastics Championships; and WHEREAS, A celebrated student-athlete at The Ohio State University, Kameron Nelson is deserving of acknowledgement for his exemplary performance at this year’s NCAA Men’s Gymnastics Championships, where he secured a first-place finish in the vault with an impressive 14.633 stick and a third-place finish on the floor with a 14.533 stick. His athletic prowess is reflected in these achievements, and he can also stand proud as the first Buckeye gymnast to win a national event title since 2019 as well as the first gymnast in the world to land two triple backs in the same floor routine during competition early in his historic senior season; and WHEREAS, Through his participation in sports, Kameron Nelson has expanded on his growth and accomplishment by preparing diligently for such events as the NCAA Men’s Gymnastics Championships, and he has set an example worthy of emulation. Indeed, by implementing his remarkable discipline, perseverance, and hard work on the national level, he has inspired many to challenge themselves to strive for excellence; and WHEREAS, In a society that encourages individuals to reflect on the merits of advancing one’s personal, professional, and recreational endeavors, athletic competition has become a valuable outlet for success. We are happy to congratulate such a fine gymnast as Kameron Nelson and look with optimism to his bright, promising future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend special recognition to Kameron Nelson for his athletic attainment at the 2025 National Collegiate Athletic Association Men’s Gymnastics Championships and salute him as a fine citizen; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Kameron Nelson.

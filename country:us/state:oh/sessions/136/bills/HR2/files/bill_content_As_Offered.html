A R E S O L U T I O N

Relative to the election of the minority leader, the assistant minority leader, the minority whip, and the assistant minority whip for the 136th General Assembly of Ohio.

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO:

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio have been advised that the caucus of Democrat members has chosen C. Allison Russo of House District #7 as minority leader, Dontavius L. Jarrells of House District #1 as assistant minority leader, Dani Isaacsohn of House District #24 as minority whip, and Michele Grim of House District #43 as assistant minority whip; therefore be it

RESOLVED, By the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly that C. Allison Russo be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of minoirty leader, Dontavius L. Jarrells be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of assistant minority leader; that Dani Isaacsohn be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of minority whip; and that Michele Grim be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of assistant minority whip.

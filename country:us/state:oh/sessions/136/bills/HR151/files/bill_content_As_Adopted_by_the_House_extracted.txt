Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 151 2025-2026 Representative Lorenz A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Olentangy Liberty High School girls lacrosse team as the 2025 Division I State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Olentangy Liberty High School girls lacrosse team on placing first at the 2025 Division I State Championship; and WHEREAS, Each member of the Olentangy Liberty High School girls lacrosse team is an exceptional young athlete, combining personal ability and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship. With admirable dedication, the players have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in their sport, and as a result, the Olentangy Liberty Patriots secured this year’s Division I State Championship crown in double overtime by defeating a strong Upper Arlington team by the score of 9-8, earning their second consecutive such title; and WHEREAS, The members of the Olentangy Liberty High School girls lacrosse team are to be commended, for their victories over a host of talented opponents underscore the athletic prowess and indomitable spirit of each player. Indeed, it is no surprise that their maturity and resolve have gained them widespread respect, even among their most daunting rivals; and WHEREAS, Under the expert guidance of their coach, these gifted athletes have diligently applied themselves to the quest for excellence on the field, and time and again, they have risen to the occasion, amply demonstrating their unflagging determination. Participation in lacrosse has taught them a great deal about commitment, self-discipline, and leadership, and clearly, their accomplishments are deserving of recognition; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Olentangy Liberty High School girls lacrosse team on its outstanding success this season and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Olentangy Liberty High School girls lacrosse team.

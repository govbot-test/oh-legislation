Representative White, A.


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Archbishop Carroll High School prepared presentation team as a Technology Student Association TEAMS National Champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Archbishop Carroll High School prepared presentation team on placing first at the Technology Student Association Tests of Engineering Aptitude, Mathematics, and Science National Conference; and

WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young academicians, the members of the Archbishop Carroll High School prepared presentation team have combined talent, enthusiasm, and confidence to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, members Leah Casas, Claire Jordan, Caleb Weitz, and Ella Westgerdes have worked diligently to improve their skills in anticipation of the 2025 Technology Student Association Tests of Engineering Aptitude, Mathematics, and Science National Conference, and as a result, they secured first place and have earned the respect of many; and

WHEREAS, The accomplishments of the Archbishop Carroll High School prepared presentation team are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the students but also on their coaches, Mary Ollier and Laurie Fuhr, on their school, and on their community. We are certain that the maturity and experience these fine young people have gained through their participation in this event will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; and

WHEREAS, Competitive spirit and innovative effort have played major roles in the development of our country, and the drive to excel is best developed when youth are presented with trials that permit them to test their skills, knowledge, and strengths against their peers. With their vitality, intelligence, and positive outlook, the members of Archbishop Carroll High School prepared presentation team have set an example worthy of emulation. We encourage these young individuals to continue to strive for excellence so that they may assume a meaningful and productive role in our complex society; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Archbishop Carroll High School prepared presentation team on its tremendous accomplishment and salute the members and coaches as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Archbishop Carroll High School prepared presentation team.

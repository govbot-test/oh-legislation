As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 68 2025-2026 Representative Stephens A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Makennah Craft on securing the 2025 Girls Wrestling State Championship in the 100-pound weight class. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Makennah Craft as a 2025 OHSAA State Wrestling Champion; and WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication during the 2024‑2025 season, Makennah Craft has certainly distinguished herself as one of the most talented wrestlers in the state. A senior member of the Jackson High School girls wrestling team, she demonstrated her drive and skill by winning the state crown this year, becoming only the fifth girl in state history to win four state titles, and her outstanding 44-0 record this season caps a two-year undefeated streak; and WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Makennah Craft has consistently exhibited the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. Indeed, she has inspired her teammates, thrilled her fans, gained the respect of her opponents, and made her family, her coaches, and her community very proud; and WHEREAS, Participation in wrestling has enabled Makennah Craft to learn much about commitment, self‑discipline, and leadership. Commendably, she has revealed the skill, strength, and stamina that befit a standout athlete, and it is no surprise that she has won so many admirers. The remarkable skill and experience she has shown as a wrestler will be of great benefit as she forges a bright, promising future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Makennah Craft as the 2025 State Champion in girls wrestling in the 100-pound weight class and look with optimism to her continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Makennah Craft.

A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Scouting America Scouts BSA Troop 201 on its One Hundredth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Scouting America Scouts BSA Troop 201 on the celebration of its Centennial, July 31‑August 1, 2026; and

WHEREAS, Scouts BSA Troop 201 has made a marked impact on generations of young people in the Olmsted Falls area through its legacy of leadership, character development, and community service. Since its inception, the troop has supported local efforts and fostered civic responsibility through Eagle Scout projects, and its accomplishments are worthy of emulation; and

WHEREAS, Founded on the highest ideals of citizenship and service, Scouts BSA provides a values-based initiative through which the youth of our country can become conscientious and responsible leaders within their communities. Over the years, Troop 201 has contributed to the acclaim that the Scouting program enjoys as one of our nation's largest, most highly-regarded youth development groups, and it is deserving of praise; and

WHEREAS, All those associated with Scouts BSA Troop 201 are to be applauded for their foresight, dedication, and selfless donations of time, energy, and ability far beyond what was required or expected. Through their involvement with a variety of projects and endeavors, they have earned the respect and admiration of many, and they have set an example of concerned and responsible citizenship; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Scouting America Scouts BSA Troop 201 on the occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Scouting America Scouts BSA Troop 201.

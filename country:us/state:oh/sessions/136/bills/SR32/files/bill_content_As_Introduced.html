A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Amberly Szczerbiak on being named the 2025 Ohio Fairs' Queen.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Amberly Szczerbiak as the 2025 Ohio Fairs’ Queen; and

WHEREAS, A student at Miami University, Amberly Szczerbiak represents other young women throughout the state who have established high personal goals and have striven to achieve their potential, and her selection as this year’s Ohio Fairs’ Queen is a fitting tribute to her for the enthusiasm and initiative she has continually displayed. Richly endowed with keen intelligence and a multitude of skills, she was previously crowned the 2024 Fayette County Fair Queen, and we are certain she will be a remarkable ambassador for the Great State of Ohio; and

WHEREAS, Known and admired throughout her community for her work with the All-N-One and Jeff All Arounders 4-H clubs, the Miami Trace/Great Oaks FFA Chapter, and the Fayette County Junior Fair Board as well as for her volunteerism with the Fayette Regional Humane Society, Amberly Szczerbiak has truly had a positive impact on her beloved Fayette County. Never content to take a passive role in life, she has brought insight and exceptional ability to all of her endeavors, and her amiable style will serve her well in the future; and

WHEREAS, This honor is a reflection of Amberly Szczerbiak’s tremendous record of accomplishment, and she has served as an inspiring example for other youth. Her many strengths and distinctive qualities, combined with her engaging personality, will no doubt carry her far beyond this event and be of great benefit to her throughout her lifetime; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Amberly Szczerbiak as the 2025 Ohio Fairs’ Queen and, in so doing, salute her as one of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Amberly Szczerbiak.

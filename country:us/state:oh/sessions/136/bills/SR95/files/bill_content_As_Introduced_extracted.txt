Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 95 2025-2026 Senator Weinstein A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Brydon Feister as a 2025 Division II State Wrestling Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Brydon Feister on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship in boys wrestling in the 190-pound weight class; and WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Brydon Feister is deserving of recognition for his exemplary accomplishments as a member of the Archbishop Hoban High School boys wrestling team. An exceptional athlete, he defeated a tough Canfield competitor by a score of 6-5 to win first place in the 190-pound weight class at this year’s Division II State Tournament, securing his school’s first such title in forty-four years, and his noteworthy record of personal achievement stands as a testament to his dedication; and WHEREAS, Through his participation in wrestling, Brydon Feister has learned important lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and good sportsmanship that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to him throughout his life. Through his outstanding efforts, he has inspired countless people to excel in various areas of endeavor, and he has set an example worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. With sports contributing to the development of countless youth, we are happy to congratulate such a fine wrestler as Brydon Feister; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Brydon Feister on his Division II State Wrestling Championship and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Brydon Feister.

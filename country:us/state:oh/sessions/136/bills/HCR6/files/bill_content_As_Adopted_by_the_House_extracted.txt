As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. C. R. No. 6 2025-2026 Representatives Miller, J., Holmes Cosponsors: Representatives Dovilla, Brennan, Gross, Synenberg, Thomas, D., Rader, Salvo, Klopfenstein, Odioso, Claggett, Bird, Johnson, Robb Blasdel, Hiner, Schmidt, Russo, Grim, McNally, White, E., Brownlee, Thomas, C., Jarrells, Brewer, Cockley, Abdullahi, Somani, Hall, D., Sims, Lett, Troy, Brent, Callender, Click, Demetriou, Denson, Fischer, Fowler Arthur, Ghanbari, Glassburn, Hall, T., Hoops, Humphrey, John, Jones, Kishman, Lawson-Rowe, Lear, Mathews, A., Mathews, T., McClain, Miller, M., Mohamed, Newman, Oelslager, Peterson, Piccolantonio, Pizzulli, Plummer, Ray, Richardson, Ritter, Roemer, Stephens, Teska, Upchurch, White, A., Williams, Willis, Young A c o n c u r r e n t R E S O L U T I O N To support and encourage the work of the Ohio Commission for the United States Semiquincentennial in celebrating the nation's 250th anniversary. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO (THE SENATE CONCURRING): WHEREAS, The 250th anniversary of the United States of America will be observed nationally in 2026; and WHEREAS, Every state will observe the nation's 250th anniversary in a myriad of ways to suit each state's history and contributions to the national narrative; and WHEREAS, The 134th Ohio General Assembly created the Ohio Commission for the U.S. Semiquincentennial (Section 149.309 of the Revised Code) in 2021 to help prepare Ohio to "plan, encourage, develop, and coordinate the commemoration of the two hundred fiftieth anniversary of the founding of the United States and the impact of Ohioans on the nation's past, present, and future"; and WHEREAS, The Ohio Commission for the U.S. Semiquincentennial has produced a report entitled "Recommendations to the People of Ohio," which includes more than 40 substantive ideas to engage Ohioans and Ohio communities in the nation's upcoming anniversary; and WHEREAS, Ohioans desire to play a leading role in the nation's anniversary not just in 2026, but also in the years leading up to 2026; now therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the 136th General Assembly of the State of Ohio, are committed to supporting the work of the Ohio Commission of the U.S. Semiquincentennial and participating fully and enthusiastically in the nation's 250th anniversary, which culminates on July 4, 2026; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit duly authenticated copies of this resolution to the Governor and news media of Ohio.

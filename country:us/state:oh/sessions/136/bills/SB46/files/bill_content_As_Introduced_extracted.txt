Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 46 2025-2026 Senator Ingram A BILL To amend section 5.2219 of the Revised Code to add screening awareness to Colorectal Cancer Awareness Month. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2219 of the Revised Code be amended to read as follows: Sec. 5.2219. The month of March is designated as "Colorectal Cancer Screening and Awareness Month." Section 2. That existing section 5.2219 of the Revised Code is hereby repealed.

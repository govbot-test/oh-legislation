Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 160 2025-2026 Senator Romanchuk A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Lexington High School boys 4x800-meter relay team on winning the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Lexington High School boys 4x800-meter relay team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Lexington High School boys 4x800-meter relay team has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for the spirited and highly motivated performance of its members. Throughout the season, the athletes worked diligently to improve their skills on the track, and as a result, the Minutemen accomplished their goals, including bringing home this year’s Division I State Championship title with a time of 7:37.65; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined team, the Lexington High School boys 4x800‑meter relay team has developed into a remarkably efficient group of runners whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their coaches; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of the members and coaches alike. The Minutemen have exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Lexington High School boys 4x800-meter relay team on its 2025 Division I State Championship and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Lexington High School boys 4x800-meter relay team.

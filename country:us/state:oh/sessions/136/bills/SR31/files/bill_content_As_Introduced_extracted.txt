Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 31 2025-2026 Senator Wilkin A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Trevon Pendleton as a 2024 All-Ohio Division V Football Coach of the Year. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Trevon Pendleton on being named a 2024 All-Ohio Division V Coach of the Year in football by the Ohio Prep Sports Media Association; and WHEREAS, As the head football coach at Ironton High School, Trevon Pendleton has demonstrated remarkable integrity and dedication. Throughout the 2024 season, he not only encouraged his athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, camaraderie, and spirit, and as a result, he led them to the Division V State Championship title; and WHEREAS, The expertise, guidance, and sound judgment of coaches such as Trevon Pendleton are critical to the ultimate success of any athletic endeavor. Due largely to his tireless efforts, the members of the Ironton High School football team have emerged as winners, both in and out of competition, and he has earned the genuine respect and admiration of many for his fantastic work; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are pleased to pay tribute to Trevon Pendleton for a job well done. His outstanding rapport with his players, as well as with his fellow coaches, has been a vital factor in the team’s accomplishments, and he is certainly deserving of high praise; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Trevon Pendleton on his state accolade and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Trevon Pendleton.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 365 2025-2026 Representatives Humphrey, Abdullahi, Piccolantonio, Jarrells, Lawson-Rowe, Lett, Mohamed, Richardson, Russo, Sigrist, Somani A R E S O L U T I O N In memory of Master Sergeant Tyler Simmons. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio extend our sincere condolences to the family and friends of Master Sergeant Tyler Simmons on the sorrowful occasion of his death; and WHEREAS, Although there are no words to ease the sorrow now felt, surely there is solace in the remembrance of having known such an outstanding individual as Tyler Simmons. A 2015 graduate of Eastmoor Academy High School, he was a member of the 121st Air Refueling Wing based at Rickenbacker Air National Guard Base, and he was serving aboard a KC-135 Stratotanker in support of Operation Epic Fury when he was killed in the line of duty. His valiant efforts were truly admirable, and his absence will be keenly felt; and WHEREAS, In numerous roles, Tyler Simmons always used his talents to the benefit of others, and the laurels of his life stand as a tribute not only to him but also to those he left behind. The great traditions of our country’s service men and women are an important chapter of American history, and his family and friends can be proud of the sacrifices he made to ensure freedom and justice for all people; and WHEREAS, Although the void his death has created can never be filled, Tyler Simmons will be remembered as a spirited individual who contributed immeasurably to the world around him. It is certain that the world is a richer place for his having been in it, and he will be sorely missed; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, mourn the loss and salute the memory of a truly unique individual, Master Sergeant Tyler Simmons; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the family of Master Sergeant Tyler Simmons.

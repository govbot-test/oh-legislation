Representative LaRe


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Pickerington High School Central cheer team on winning a 2025 OASSA State Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Pickerington High School Central cheer team as the 2025 Ohio Association of Secondary School Administrators State Champion in Division 1 game day building; and

WHEREAS, The members of the Pickerington High School Central cheer team are exemplary athletes, and throughout this celebrated season, they have shown themselves to be a truly exceptional squad. The Tigers have been unwavering in their commitment to excellence, and the competitors and coaches are deserving of high praise for earning this year’s OASSA state title in the Division 1 game day building category; and

WHEREAS, The individuals associated with the Pickerington High School Central cheer team have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in securing this outstanding state recognition. Indeed, this victory is truly a result of the tireless efforts collectively put forth by the team members and coaches, and they have certainly distinguished themselves and received respect and admiration from fans and opponents alike; and

WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of personal and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Pickerington High School Central cheer team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend special acknowledgement to the Pickerington High School Central cheer team as the 2025 OASSA State Champion in Division 1 game day building and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Pickerington High School Central cheer team.

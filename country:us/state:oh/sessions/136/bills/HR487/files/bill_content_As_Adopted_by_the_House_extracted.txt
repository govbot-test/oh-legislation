Section 1:
hr487_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 487 2025-2026 Representative Craig A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Maryn Erdman on winning a 2026 Division V State Track and Field Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Maryn Erdman as the 2026 Division V State Track and Field Champion in the 800-meter run; and WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Maryn Erdman is deserving of acknowledgement for her exemplary accomplishments as a junior member of the Smithville High School girls track and field team. This year, she secured the Division V State Championship in the 800-meter run with a time of 2:12.31, and her noteworthy record of athletic achievement stands as a hallmark for others to emulate; and WHEREAS, Maryn Erdman can always be counted on to shine, and her abilities and sportsmanship have been important factors in her success. A tenacious competitor, she has shown tremendous devotion to this demanding activity, and through her outstanding efforts, she has inspired countless people, including her peers. Indeed, she has set an example worthy of applause; and WHEREAS, The unparalleled commitment to excellence that Maryn Erdman has displayed in her endeavors has earned her the respect and esteem of many. To her credit, she has demonstrated the skill, integrity, and dedication that are the hallmarks of a true champion, and she has certainly distinguished herself as one of the most talented high school runners in the State of Ohio; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, honor Maryn Erdman on her state championship and wish her the best in the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Maryn Erdman.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 62 2025-2026 Senator McColley A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Adam Mattin on securing the 2025 Division III State Wrestling Championship at 126 pounds. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Adam Mattin as the 2025 Division III State Wrestling Champion in the 126-pound weight class; and WHEREAS, Adam Mattin’s accomplishments are truly noteworthy, for he has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his mental and physical fortitude as a wrestler. As a member of the Delta High School wrestling team, which placed second at this year’s Division III State meet, he has exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, integrity, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; and WHEREAS, By developing such remarkable prowess in wrestling, Adam Mattin has earned the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. Indeed, his outstanding record of achievement, including his three state titles, is sure to inspire future generations of Panthers; and WHEREAS, Wrestling is a sport which requires exceptional stamina and natural ability, and Adam Mattin is to be commended for his commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining his athletic goals, he has certainly distinguished himself as a true champion; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize Adam Mattin on his state wrestling championship and honor him as a fine Ohioan; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Adam Mattin.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 169 2025-2026 Representatives Abdullahi, Somani Cosponsors: Representatives Cockley, Brennan, Jarrells, Baker, Brent, Denson, Sims, Brownlee, Upchurch, Sweeney, Piccolantonio, Grim, McNally, Russo, Brewer A BILL To enact section 5.42 of the Revised Code to designate March 8 as International Women's Day in Ohio. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.42 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.42. The eighth day of March is designated as "International Women's Day" in Ohio.

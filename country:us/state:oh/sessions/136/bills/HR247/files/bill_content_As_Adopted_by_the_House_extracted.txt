Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 247 2025-2026 Representatives Miller, M., John A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Herlihy Funeral Home on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Herlihy Funeral Home on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this distinguished event is a fitting tribute to Herlihy Funeral Home for its lasting presence in Mansfield and the surrounding community throughout the past century. Established by Joseph Geiger in 1925, this fine venture is now operated by a fourth generation of the owners, and it has served area families with unwavering commitment, respect, and integrity; and WHEREAS, The success of Herlihy Funeral Home is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and hard‑working employees. This respected establishment has helped countless people cope with the challenges of losing a loved one by offering support, comfort, and dignified ceremonies; and WHEREAS, Over the years, Herlihy Funeral Home has assisted individuals and families during bereavement, and in doing so, it has earned a well-deserved reputation for compassion and dependability. We are certain that as this noteworthy enterprise maintains an unfaltering dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to prosper and will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Herlihy Funeral Home on its One Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Herlihy Funeral Home.

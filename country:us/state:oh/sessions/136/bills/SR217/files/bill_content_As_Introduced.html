A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Brice Fuller as the 2025 Division III State Boys Cross Country Champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Brice Fuller on securing the 2025 Division III State Championship in boys cross country; and

WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Brice Fuller is deserving of acknowledgement for his exemplary accomplishments as a sophomore member of the Fairless High School boys cross country team. A remarkable athlete, he won this year’s Division III State Championship with a time of 14:52.76, and he not only established a new state record but was the only runner to break the fifteen‑minute mark during any of the state meet races. Indeed, his notable record of personal achievement stands as a hallmark for others; and

WHEREAS, Throughout the 2025 season, Brice Fuller had prepared conscientiously in pursuit of the state crown, devoting countless hours to training and practice in order to ensure peak conditioning. This demanding regimen has paid handsome dividends, for he certainly has set an example for others to emulate; and

WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach tomorrow’s leaders the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, high school athletics have become a valuable tool. We are proud to salute Brice Fuller’s athletic achievement, and know the skills and dedication he has demonstrated as a cross country runner will aid him in facing challenges; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Brice Fuller as the 2025 Division III State Boys Cross Country Champion and look with optimism to the years ahead; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Brice Fuller.

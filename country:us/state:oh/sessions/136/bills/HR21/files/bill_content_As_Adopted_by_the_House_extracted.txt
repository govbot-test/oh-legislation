Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 21 2025-2026 Representative Lorenz A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Olentangy Berlin High School cheer team as the 2024 OHSAA Division I Spirit State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Olentangy Berlin High School cheer team on winning the 2024 Ohio High School Athletic Association Division I Spirit State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Olentangy Berlin High School cheer team have established themselves as truly exceptional competitors by combining talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship. With admirable dedication, the Bears have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in their sport, and as a result, they won the 2024 OHSAA Division I Spirit State Championship with an impressive score of 90.93; and WHEREAS, The achievements and leadership of the Olentangy Berlin High School cheer team are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the members of the squad, their families, their school community, and their coaches. Indeed, these athletes have developed determination, perseverance, and camaraderie that they have displayed proudly in all of their competitive endeavors; and WHEREAS, With athletics playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are pleased to pay tribute to the Olentangy Berlin High School cheer team for a job well done. The maturity and experience that these exemplary young Ohioans have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Olentangy Berlin High School cheer team on its tremendous 2024 season and salute the members and coaches as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Olentangy Berlin High School cheer team.

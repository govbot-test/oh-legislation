As Reported by the House Health Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 104 2025-2026 Representatives Lawson-Rowe, Deeter Cosponsors: Representatives Brennan, Brent, Brewer, Click, Daniels, Denson, Hiner, Klopfenstein, Lorenz, Mohamed, Mullins, Odioso, Rader, Ritter, Salvo, Sims, Synenberg, Thomas, D., Tims, Upchurch, White, E., Workman, Somani, White, A. A BILL To enact section 5.2540 of the Revised Code to designate February 14 as "Cardiovascular Health Awareness Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2540 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2540. The fourteenth day of February is designated as "Cardiovascular Health Awareness Day."

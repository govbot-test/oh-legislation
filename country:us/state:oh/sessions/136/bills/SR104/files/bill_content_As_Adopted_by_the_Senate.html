A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Ohio Division of State Fire Marshal on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Ohio Division of State Fire Marshal on the One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary of its founding; and

WHEREAS, Recognition of this event is a fitting tribute to the Ohio Division of State Fire Marshal, for since its inception, this praiseworthy organization has achieved an admirable record of service to the whole of our state. Established in 1900 through Senate Bill 51, the agency has been a vital instrument for safety, responding to even the most challenging of circumstances with professionalism, and its commitment to excellence has been clearly visible in all of its endeavors; and

WHEREAS, Over the past one hundred twenty-five years, the Ohio Division of State Fire Marshal has witnessed many changes in the field of emergency services, and it has worked diligently to keep pace with these challenges. Indeed, the agency’s achievements are an outstanding reflection not only on the department itself but also on its numerous professionals over the years and on the entire state; and

WHEREAS, While many people are content to take a passive role in life, the members of the Ohio Division of State Fire Marshal have proven to be some of the finest individuals in this challenging and paramount field, and the enthusiasm and devotion to duty that they have shown have gained them the gratitude and appreciation of the many they have served. We are certain that as they maintain their dedication to service, these individuals will continue in the tradition of excellence which has long been the department’s hallmark; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Ohio Division of State Fire Marshal on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary and salute its members as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Ohio Division of State Fire Marshal.

As Introduced

136th General Assembly

Regular Session H. B. No. 860

2025-2026

Representatives Kishman, Craig

Cosponsor: Representative Salvo


To enact section 5.035 of the Revised Code to adopt the Zebra Swallowtail as the state butterfly of Ohio.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO:

Section 1. That section 5.035 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows:

Sec. 5.035. The butterfly, Eurytides marcellus, commonly known as the zebra swallowtail, is the official butterfly of the state.

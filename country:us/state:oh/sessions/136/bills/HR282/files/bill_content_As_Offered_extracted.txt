As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 282 2025-2026 Representative Grim A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring The University of Toledo women’s cross country team on winning the 2025 Mid-American Conference Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to salute The University of Toledo women’s cross country team as the 2025 Mid-American Conference Champion; and WHEREAS, Establishing a reputation for its skill and tenacity, The University of Toledo women’s cross country team is an impressive group of athletes whose talent and teamwork are unmatched. At the 2025 Mid-American Conference Championship meet, the squad seized its eleventh title with 32 points, becoming the winningest MAC program, and this victory will be celebrated for many years to come; and WHEREAS, The members of The University of Toledo women’s cross country team rose to the occasion and proved themselves to be a dynamic force in college sports. Truly, these outstanding athletes can be proud of the accomplishments that were hard-won and deserved throughout the 2025 season; and WHEREAS, By remaining faithful to the highest standards of sportsmanship and teamwork, the members of The University of Toledo women’s cross country team primed themselves under the guidance of Head Coach Andrea Grove-McDonough to secure this year’s MAC crown. Indeed, the Rockets have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in all of their endeavors; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize The University of Toledo women’s cross country team on its success and honor the athletes and coaches for their tremendous prowess; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to The University of Toledo women’s cross country team.

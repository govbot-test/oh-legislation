Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 310 2025-2026 Senator Antonio A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring WellLink on its One Hundred Tenth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to WellLink as it celebrates its One Hundred Tenth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, This important occasion provides an ideal time to acknowledge the exceptional work done by WellLink. Since its establishment, the organization has worked to better the health, economic, and social well-being of numerous communities across Northeast Ohio, and it has been held in high esteem for its impressive contributions that have encouraged systemic change; and WHEREAS, All those associated with WellLink are to be commended for their foresight, dedication, and selfless donations of time, energy, and ability far beyond what was required or expected. These fine people have earned the respect and admiration of many and have set an example of concerned and responsible citizenship worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, Founded on the highest ideals of service, WellLink has distinguished itself by its commitment to excellence. Throughout its history, it has maintained a reputation for integrity, reliability, and devotion to service, and it is deserving of the highest praise for its tremendous endeavors; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate WellLink on its One Hundred Tenth Anniversary and applaud its staff and volunteers as outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to WellLink.

Raw Text:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 310 2025-2026 Senator Antonio A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring WellLink on its One Hundred Tenth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to WellLink as it celebrates its One Hundred Tenth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, This important occasion provides an ideal time to acknowledge the exceptional work done by WellLink. Since its establishment, the organization has worked to better the health, economic, and social well-being of numerous communities across Northeast Ohio, and it has been held in high esteem for its impressive contributions that have encouraged systemic change; and WHEREAS, All those associated with WellLink are to be commended for their foresight, dedication, and selfless donations of time, energy, and ability far beyond what was required or expected. These fine people have earned the respect and admiration of many and have set an example of concerned and responsible citizenship worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, Founded on the highest ideals of service, WellLink has distinguished itself by its commitment to excellence. Throughout its history, it has maintained a reputation for integrity, reliability, and devotion to service, and it is deserving of the highest praise for its tremendous endeavors; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate WellLink on its One Hundred Tenth Anniversary and applaud its staff and volunteers as outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to WellLink.

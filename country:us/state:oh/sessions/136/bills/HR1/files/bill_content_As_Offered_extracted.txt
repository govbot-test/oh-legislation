As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 1 2025-2026 Representative Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Relative to the election of the assistant speaker pro tempore, the majority floor leader, the assistant majority floor leader, and the majority whips for the 136th General Assembly of Ohio. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO: WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio have been advised that the caucus of Republican members have chosen Phil Plummer of House District #39 as assistant speaker pro tempore, Marilyn John of House District #76 as majority floor leader, Adam C. Bird of House District #63 as assistant majority floor leader, Riordan T. McClain of House District #87 as majority whip, Steve Demetriou of House District #35 as majority whip, Nick Santucci of House District #64 as majority whip, and Josh Williams of House District #44 as majority whip; therefore be it RESOLVED, By the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly that Phil Plummer be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of assistant speaker pro tempore; that Marilyn John be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of majority floor leader; that Adam C. Bird be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of assistant majority floor leader; that Riordan T. McClain be, and upon the adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of majority whip; that Steve Demetriou be, and upon the adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of majority whip; that Nick Santucci be, and upon the adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of majority whip; and that Josh Williams be, and upon the adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of majority whip.

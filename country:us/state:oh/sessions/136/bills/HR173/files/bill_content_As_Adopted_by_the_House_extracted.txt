Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 173 2025-2026 Representative Holmes A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Aidan Mahon as a 2025 Division II State Track and Field Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Aidan Mahon on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship in the boys high jump; and WHEREAS, A junior at Philo High School, Aidan Mahon has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, he has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in track and field, and as a result, he placed first in the boys high jump with a leap of 6 feet, 6 inches at this year’s Division II State Meet, becoming the first state champion in the history of the Franklin Local School District; and WHEREAS, Aidan Mahon’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his coaches, his school, and his community. His unwavering commitment to excellence has earned him the respect and esteem of many, and he is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Aidan Mahon on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience he has gained through his participation in track and field will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Aidan Mahon as the 2025 Division II State Track and Field Champion in the boys high jump and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Aidan Mahon.

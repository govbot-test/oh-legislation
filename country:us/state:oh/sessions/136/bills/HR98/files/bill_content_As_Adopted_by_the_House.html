136th General Assembly

Regular Session H. R. No. 98

2025-2026


Representative Oelslager


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Louisville High School boys basketball team on winning the 2025 Division III State Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Louisville High School boys basketball team as the 2025 Division III State Champion; and

WHEREAS, The members of the Louisville High School boys basketball team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly exceptional competitors. During the 2024-2025 season, the Leopards amassed a 24-5 record, and in final tournament play, they overcame a strong challenge from a tough Aiken team to win the 2025 Division III State Championship crown, a first in school history. The unwavering commitment of the players and coaches has earned them the esteem of many, and they are certainly deserving of high praise; and

WHEREAS, Head Coach Tom Siegfried has demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the Louisville High School boys basketball team to victory, and his skill is reflected in the squad’s outstanding accomplishments. Due largely to his tireless efforts, the members have distinguished themselves on the court, and he has received respect and admiration from all who know him; and

WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Louisville High School boys basketball team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Louisville High School boys basketball team on securing the 2025 Division III State Championship title and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Louisville High School boys basketball team.

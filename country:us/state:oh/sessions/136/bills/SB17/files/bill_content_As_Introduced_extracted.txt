Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 17 2025-2026 Senators Reynolds, Craig A BILL To enact section 5.212 of the Revised Code to designate March 9th as "6888th Central Postal Directory Battalion Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.212 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.212. The ninth day of March is designated as "6888th Central Postal Directory Battalion Day" to honor those who served in the 6888th central postal directory battalion, the only all-Black women's unit to serve overseas during World War II.

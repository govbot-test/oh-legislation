Section 1:
hr499_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 499 2025-2026 Representative Craig A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Brittany Nolletti on winning the 2026 Adidas Middle School National Championship in the pole vault. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Brittany Nolletti as the 2026 Adidas Middle School National Champion in the pole vault; and WHEREAS, A student at Triway Middle School, Brittany Nolletti has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in her sport, and as a result, she won first place in the pole vault with a mark of 10 feet, 11.1 inches, at this year's Adidas Middle School National Championship; and WHEREAS, Brittany Nolletti's accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to Brittany Nolletti on an exemplary season. Indeed, the maturity and experience she has gained in competition will aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Brittany Nolletti on her national championship and look with optimism to her ongoing success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Brittany Nolletti.

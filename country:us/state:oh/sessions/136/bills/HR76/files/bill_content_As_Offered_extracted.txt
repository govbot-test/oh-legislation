Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 76 2025-2026 Representative LaRe A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team on winning the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for the spirited and highly motivated performance of its members. Throughout the season, the athletes worked diligently to improve their skills on the court, and as a result, the Tigers accomplished their goals, bringing home this year’s state championship, their first state crown since 2018 and eighth such title overall; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined team, the Pickerington Central Tigers have developed into a remarkably efficient group of players whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their coach, Johnathan Hedgepeth; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of athletes and coaches alike. The members of the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team have exemplified the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team on its 2025 Division I State Championship and offer best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team.

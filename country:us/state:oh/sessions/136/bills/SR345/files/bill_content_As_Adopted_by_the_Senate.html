A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the University of Mount Union engineering team as a 2025 IEEE Champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the University of Mount Union engineering team on placing first in the student hardware design competition at the 2025 Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers Electromagnetic Compatibility Society Symposium; and

WHEREAS, The University of Mount Union engineering team members Emily Borroni, Jack Davis, Thomas Elliot, Glauco Filho Fontgalland, Dan Foltz, and Logan Gunderman are a remarkable group of young academicians, and they are deserving of high praise for their success at the IEEE Electromagnetic Compatibility Society Symposium. Indeed, they invested considerable time and energy into creating an entry that could locate hidden radio transmitters, and consequently, they placed first in the student hardware design competition; and

WHEREAS, The success of the University of Mount Union engineering team is a fitting tribute to the excellent leadership provided by its advisor, Dr. Glauco Fontgalland, and to the tremendous effort and dedication displayed by each team member. The group's accomplishments are an outstanding reflection of its talented members, and the maturity and experience they have gained through their participation in the IEEE Electromagnetic Compatibility Society Symposium will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; and

WHEREAS, The members of the University of Mount Union engineering team have demonstrated their sincere desire to fully develop their talents so that they might assume meaningful and rewarding roles in our society, and clearly, they have displayed a degree of determination and motivation of which they can be proud. We applaud the team on a job well done, and hope that their exemplary accomplishments will encourage future generations of students to dedicate themselves similarly; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the University of Mount Union engineering team on its exceptional performance and extend best wishes to the members and advisor for continued success in their future endeavors; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the University of Mount Union engineering team.

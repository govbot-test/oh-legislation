Section 1:
hb848_00_IN As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 848 2025-2026 Representatives Jarrells, White, A. Cosponsors: Representatives Upchurch, Brent, Miller, J., Piccolantonio, Lawson-Rowe, Brennan, Somani, Synenberg, Bryant Bailey, Thomas, C., Sims, Sigrist, Rader, Brewer, Lett, Cockley, Abdullahi, Mohamed, Brownlee, Russo, Baker, Grim To enact section 5.2325 of the Revised Code to designate the month of September as "Celebrate HBCU Month." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2325 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2325. The month of September is designated as "Celebrate HBCU Month" to celebrate historically black colleges and universities.

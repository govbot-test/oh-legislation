Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 359 2025-2026 Representative Hoops A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Napoleon High School girls bowling team on securing the 2026 Division II State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Napoleon High School girls bowling team as the 2026 Division II State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Napoleon High School girls bowling team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year’s Division II State Tournament, the Lady Cats amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse as they won the state title against a tough Coldwater team by the scores of 192-132, 192-165, and 189-144; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of the Napoleon High School girls bowling team’s exceptional head coach, Marsha Horner, and her assistants, Randy Schwaiger and Chris Ehlers, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Napoleon High School girls bowling team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in bowling; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Napoleon High School girls bowling team on its 2026 Division II State Championship title and count its members and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Napoleon High School girls bowling team.

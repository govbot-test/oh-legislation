Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 337 2025-2026 Senator Landis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Kayla Moroschan as a 2026 OHSAA State Wrestling Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize Kayla Moroschan on earning the 2026 Ohio High School Athletic Association State Championship in girls wrestling in the 125-pound weight class; and WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication during the 2025‑2026 season, Kayla Moroschan has certainly distinguished herself as one of the most talented wrestlers in the state. A junior member of the Chippewa High School girls wrestling team, she demonstrated her drive and skill by winning her second state title in three years, and her impressive 51-1 season record is praiseworthy; and WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Kayla Moroschan has consistently exhibited the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. Indeed, she has inspired her teammates, thrilled her fans, gained the respect of her opponents, and made her family, her coaches, and her Chipps community very proud; and WHEREAS, Participation in wrestling has enabled Kayla Moroschan to learn much about dedication, self‑discipline, and leadership. Commendably, she has revealed the skill, strength, and stamina that befit a standout athlete, and it is no surprise that she has won so many admirers. The remarkable skill and experience she has shown as a wrestler will be of great benefit as she forges a bright, promising future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Kayla Moroschan as the 2026 Girls Wrestling State Champion in the 125-pound weight class and salute her as a fine Ohio athlete; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Kayla Moroschan.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 325 2025-2026 Senator Gavarone A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring DGL Consulting Engineers on its One Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend DGL Consulting Engineers on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is, indeed, a fitting tribute to DGL Consulting Engineers, for over the past one hundred years, this fine business has attained a remarkable record of service. Since its founding in 1926, the esteemed civil engineering firm has continually demonstrated its exemplary commitment to the highest industry standards, customer satisfaction, and competitive pricing, and its dedication to the best interests of its clientele has been evident in all its transactions; and WHEREAS, The success of DGL Consulting Engineers is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and hard-working employees. Their vision and initiative have earned this respected institution the gratitude and respect of many appreciative customers, and they are truly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Over the years, DGL Consulting Engineers has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding community and has contributed greatly to the area’s economic well-being. We are certain that as this worthy enterprise maintains its dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to prosper and will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate DGL Consulting Engineers on its Centennial and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to DGL Consulting Engineers.

Senator Manning


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Max Hauck on securing first place in the discus and shot put at the 2026 Division II State Track and Field Meet.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Max Hauck as a two-time 2026 Division II State Track and Field Champion; and

WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication as a member of the Avon High School boys track and field team, Max Hauck has certainly distinguished himself as one of the most talented athletes in Ohio. At this year's Division II State Meet, he placed first in the shot put with a throw of 63 feet, 0.5 inches and the discus with a throw of 180 feet, 10 inches, and he is deserving of commendation; and

WHEREAS, Athletes such as Max Hauck exemplify the qualities necessary for excellence in any sport, including discipline, agility, conditioning, and focus. Throughout long hours of practice and competition, he has consistently demonstrated the talent and motivation that are the key to success in any endeavor; and

WHEREAS, Despite sometimes grueling pressure, Max Hauck has consistently maintained his composure and morale, exhibiting strength, speed, and stamina in every challenging encounter. Time and again, he has amply demonstrated his unflagging determination, and he has set an example for others to emulate; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Max Hauck on his outstanding achievements at the 2026 Division II State Track and Field Meet and offer best wishes for the years ahead; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Max Hauck.

hr482_00_IN As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 482 2025-2026 Representative White, A. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Oakwood High School girls track and field team on securing the 2026 Division III State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Oakwood High School girls track and field team as the 2026 Division III State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Oakwood High School girls track and field team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year's Division III State Meet, the members won the state crown, their third such title in program history, with 52 points, and they amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional Oakwood High School girls track and field team coaching staff, including Head Coach Mark Stiver, as well as the support of their community, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Oakwood High School girls track and field team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in track and field; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Oakwood High School girls track and field team on its state championship and count its members and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Oakwood High School girls track and field team.

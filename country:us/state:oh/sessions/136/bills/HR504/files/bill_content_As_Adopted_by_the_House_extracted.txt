Section 1:
hr504_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 504 2025-2026 Representative Hall, D. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Anthony Bontempo as the 2026 Division II State Champion in the high jump. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Anthony Bontempo on winning the 2026 Division II State Championship in the high jump; and WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Anthony Bontempo is deserving of recognition for his exemplary accomplishments as a senior member of the Tallmadge High School boys track and field team. An exceptional athlete, he won first place in the high jump at this year's Division II State Meet with a leap of 6 feet, 8 inches, and his notable record of personal achievement stands as a hallmark for others; and WHEREAS, Through his participation in track and field, Anthony Bontempo has learned important lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and good sportsmanship that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to him throughout his life. Through his outstanding efforts, he has inspired countless people to excel in various areas of endeavor, and he has set an example worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. With sports contributing to the development of countless youth, we are delighted to congratulate such a fine athlete as Anthony Bontempo; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to Anthony Bontempo on winning the 2026 Division II State High Jump Championship and honor him as one of Ohio's finest young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Anthony Bontempo.

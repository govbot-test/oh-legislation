Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 148 2025-2026 Senator Brenner A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Brooke Chapman as a 2025 Division I State Track and Field Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Brooke Chapman on winning the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Championship in the 1,600-meter run; and WHEREAS, A junior at Olentangy Orange High School, Brooke Chapman has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in track and field, and as a result, she placed first in the 1,600-meter run with a time of 4:46.58 at this year’s Division I State Meet; and WHEREAS, Brooke Chapman’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of acknowledgement; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Brooke Chapman on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in track and field will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Brooke Chapman as the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Champion in the 1,600-meter run and offer best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Brooke Chapman.

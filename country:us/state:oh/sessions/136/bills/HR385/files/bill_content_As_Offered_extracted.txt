Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 385 2025-2026 Representative Stephens A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Levi Priddy as a 2026 Division II State Swimming Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Levi Priddy on winning the 2026 Division II State Championship in the 50-yard freestyle; and WHEREAS, A senior at River Valley High School, Levi Priddy has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, he has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in swimming, and as a result, he won first place in the 50-yard freestyle with a time of 20.21 seconds, securing the first state championship in school history, and second place in the 100-yard freestyle with a time of 44.76 seconds at this year’s Division II State Meet; and WHEREAS, Levi Priddy’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his coaches, his school, and his community. His unwavering commitment to excellence has earned him the appreciation and esteem of many, and he is certainly worthy of acknowledgement; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Levi Priddy on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience he has gained through his participation in swimming will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Levi Priddy on his 2026 Division II State Championship and offer best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Levi Priddy.

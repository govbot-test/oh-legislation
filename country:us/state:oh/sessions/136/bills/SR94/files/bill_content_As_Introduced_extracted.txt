Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 94 2025-2026 Senators Weinstein, Roegner A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring The University of Akron men’s basketball team on securing the 2025 Mid-American Conference Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud The University of Akron men’s basketball team as the 2025 Mid-American Conference Champion; and WHEREAS, The University of Akron men’s basketball team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year’s Mid-American Conference Tournament, the Zips came from behind to best Miami University 76-74 to claim the MAC title and earn a spot in the NCAA Tournament, and the members amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of the exceptional University of Akron men’s basketball team coaching staff, including Head Coach John Groce, as well as the support of their school community, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise The University of Akron men’s basketball team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in basketball; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate The University of Akron men’s basketball team on its 2025 Mid‑American Conference Championship title and count its players and coaches among the ranks of outstanding citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to The University of Akron men’s basketball team.

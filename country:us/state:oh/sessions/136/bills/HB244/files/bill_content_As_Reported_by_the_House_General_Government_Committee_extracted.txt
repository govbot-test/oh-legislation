As Reported by the House General Government Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 244 2025-2026 Representatives Grim, Piccolantonio Cosponsors: Representatives Synenberg, Rader, McNally, Brennan, Lett, Upchurch, Brownlee, Baker, Sims, Cockley, Robinson, White, E., Rogers, Somani, Russo, Ray, LaRe, Brent, John A BILL To enact section 5.56 of the Revised Code to designate March as Women's History Month. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.56 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.56. The month of March is designated as "Women's History Month."

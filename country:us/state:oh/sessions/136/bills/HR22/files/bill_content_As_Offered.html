A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Krill Funeral Service on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Krill Funeral Service on its Sesquicentennial; and

WHEREAS, Since its establishment in 1875 by the Krill Family, Krill Funeral Service has earned the respect and appreciation of many area residents, and it has set an example to which other such enterprises can aspire. Over the years, it has demonstrated its outstanding commitment to the highest industry standards without sacrificing concern and compassion for the bereaved, and it has been dedicated to its mission of meeting the needs of its clients, which has built it a reputation as a caring and professional endeavor; and

WHEREAS, The success of Krill Funeral Service can be attributed to the vision and foresight of a host of innovative individuals, including its owner and funeral director, Gary Burr, and all those who have been involved with its operations are to be applauded on their tremendous efforts. Clearly, this venture convincingly illustrates how very much can be accomplished by a group of conscientious people with clear objectives and firm resolve, and it is through the work of citizens such as these that Ohio continues to be a pleasant place in which to live; and

WHEREAS, Krill Funeral Service has witnessed many changes in the industry over the past one hundred fifty years, but it has always maintained its focus on the needs of its customers. Indeed, it has continually displayed the same signature professionalism and commitment to quality, and we are confident that this exemplary venture will thrive for many more decades to come; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to Krill Funeral Service on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary and salute it as one of Ohio’s finest businesses; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Krill Funeral Service.

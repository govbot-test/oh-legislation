hr481_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 481 2025-2026 Representative White, A. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team on placing first at the 2026 Division III State Meet. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team as the 2026 Division III State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for the spirited and highly motivated performance of its members. Throughout the season, Katherine Erwin, Riley Meador, Delaney Cahill, and Kaya Asinjo worked diligently to improve their skills on the track, and as a result, the group accomplished their goals, bringing home this year's state championship with a new school and state record time of 9:07.10; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined lineup, the members of the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team have developed into a remarkably efficient group of runners whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their coach, Mark Stiver; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of athletes and coaches alike. The members of the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team have exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team on its 2026 Division III State Championship and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team.

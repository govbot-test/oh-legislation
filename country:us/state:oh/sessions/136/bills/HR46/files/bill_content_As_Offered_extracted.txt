Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 46 2025-2026 Representative Russo A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Upper Arlington High School girls swimming and diving team on earning the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Upper Arlington High School girls swimming and diving team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; and WHEREAS, The members of the Upper Arlington High School girls swimming and diving team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly exceptional competitors. The Golden Bears compiled an impressive 263.5 points to win this year’s Division I State Championship, their second consecutive and fourteenth overall state crown. The unwavering commitment of the swimmers, divers, and coaches has earned them the esteem of many, and they are certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, The coaching staff, which includes girls swimming coach, John Sands, and girls diving coach, Ryan Wilson, has demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the Upper Arlington High School girls swimming and diving team to victory, and their skill is reflected in the squad’s outstanding accomplishments. Due largely to their tireless efforts, the coaches have distinguished themselves in competition, and they have received respect and admiration from all who know them; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, participation in athletics has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Upper Arlington High School girls swimming and diving team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Upper Arlington High School girls swimming and diving team as the 2025 Division I State Champion and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Upper Arlington High School girls swimming and diving team.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 97 2025-2026 Representative Oelslager A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Abigail Mozden on securing the 2025 Girls Wrestling State Championship in the 135-pound weight class. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Abigail Mozden as a 2025 OHSAA State Wrestling Champion; and WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication during the 2024‑2025 season, Abigail Mozden has certainly distinguished herself as one of the most talented wrestlers in the state. A member of the Alliance High School girls wrestling team, she demonstrated her drive and skill by winning the state crown this year; and WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Abigail Mozden has consistently exhibited the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. Indeed, she has inspired her teammates, thrilled her fans, gained the respect of her opponents, and made her family, her coaches, and her community very proud; and WHEREAS, Participation in wrestling has enabled Abigail Mozden to learn much about commitment, self‑discipline, and leadership. Commendably, she has revealed the skill, strength, and stamina that befit a standout athlete, and it is no surprise that she has won so many admirers. The remarkable skill and experience she has shown as a wrestler will be of great benefit as she forges a bright, promising future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Abigail Mozden as the 2025 State Champion in girls wrestling in the 135-pound weight class and look with optimism to her continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Abigail Mozden.

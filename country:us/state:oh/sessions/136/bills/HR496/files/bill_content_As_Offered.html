Representative Newman


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Kade Schweikhardt on winning a 2026 Division III State Track and Field Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Kade Schweikhardt as a 2026 Division III State Track and Field Champion in the 800-meter run; and

WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Kade Schweikhardt is deserving of acknowledgement for his exemplary accomplishments as a senior member of the Bethel High School boys track and field team. This year, he secured a Division III State Championship in the 800-meter run with a time of 1:51.69, and his noteworthy record of athletic achievement stands as a hallmark for others to emulate; and

WHEREAS, Kade Schweikhardt can always be counted on to shine, and his abilities and sportsmanship have been important factors in his success. A tenacious competitor, he has shown tremendous devotion to this demanding activity, and through his outstanding efforts, he has inspired countless people, including his peers. Indeed, he has set an example worthy of applause; and

WHEREAS, The unparalleled commitment to excellence that Kade Schweikhardt has displayed in his endeavors has earned him the respect and esteem of many. To his credit, he has demonstrated the skill, integrity, and dedication that are the signatures of a true champion, and he has certainly distinguished himself as one of the most talented high school runners in the State of Ohio; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, honor Kade Schweikhardt on his state championship and wish him the best in the years to come; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Kade Schweikhardt.

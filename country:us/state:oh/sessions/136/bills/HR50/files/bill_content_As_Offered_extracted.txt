Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 50 2025-2026 Representative Stephens A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Rock Hill High School cheer team as a 2025 OASSA State Cheer Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Rock Hill High School cheer team on winning first place in the Division 4 game day building category at the 2025 Ohio Association of Secondary School Administrators State Cheer Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Rock Hill High School cheer team have established themselves as truly exceptional competitors by combining talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship. With admirable dedication, the team members have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in their sport, and as a result, they secured first place in the Division 4 game day building category at the 2025 OASSA State Cheer Competition; and WHEREAS, The achievements and leadership of the Rock Hill High School cheer team are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the members of the squad, Trinity Matney, Miranda Howard, Ellie Hunt, Autumn Blankenship, Haleigh Tyson, Savannah Barnes, Kailey Swed, Haylie Aliff, Kayleigh Sisler, Lexi Craft, Kourtney Jenkins, Jada Boggs, Laycee Dalton, Bridgette Stapleton, Kassidy Lyons, Rose Beckett, Taylor Leninger, and Hailey McFann, as well as their families, their school community, and their coach, Terri Stamper. Indeed, these athletes have developed determination, perseverance, and camaraderie that they have displayed proudly in all of their competitive endeavors; and WHEREAS, With athletics playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are pleased to pay tribute to the Rock Hill High School cheer team for a job well done. The maturity and experience that these exemplary young Ohioans have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Rock Hill High School cheer team on its tremendous season and salute the members and coaches as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Rock Hill High School cheer team.

hr435_00_IN As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 435 2025-2026 Representative Ghanbari A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Village of Bradner on its Sesquicentennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Village of Bradner on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary, celebrated July 17-18, 2026; and WHEREAS, The Village of Bradner was formed in 1876, and recognition of its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary is, indeed, a fitting tribute to this fine community. Over the past century and a half, it has become known for its residents' cooperation, integrity, and willingness to work hard, exemplary qualities that are still manifested by its citizens today; and WHEREAS, Throughout Bradner's existence, its generous and civic-minded population has enjoyed a wealth of benefits, and those who currently make the village their home take great pride in their community and its rich heritage. Their efforts have certainly made the area a pleasant and friendly place in which to live and work; and WHEREAS, America is only as strong as her individual communities, which are unique and which make significant contributions to the culture of our state and nation. The Sesquicentennial of the Village of Bradner offers its citizens the opportunity to share their memories and experiences and to reflect on the benefits of life in their close-knit community, for they have built on the best traditions of the past to ensure a future filled with hope and promise; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Village of Bradner on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary and extend best wishes for peace and prosperity in the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Village of Bradner.

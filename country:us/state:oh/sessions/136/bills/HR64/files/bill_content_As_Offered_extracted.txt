Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 64 2025-2026 Representative Mathews, T. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Laney Oliver as a 2025 OHSAA State Wrestling Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Laney Oliver on securing the 2025 Girls Wrestling State Championship in the 190-pound weight class; and WHEREAS, A senior at Findlay High School, Laney Oliver has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional athlete. A strong competitor, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in her weight class, and as a result, she overcame a strong Lakewood opponent to win the state title and finish the season with a remarkable 34-0 record; and WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Laney Oliver has consistently demonstrated the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Laney Oliver’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in wrestling will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Laney Oliver as the 2025 Girls Wrestling State Champion in the 190‑pound weight class and salute her as a fine Ohio athlete; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Laney Oliver.

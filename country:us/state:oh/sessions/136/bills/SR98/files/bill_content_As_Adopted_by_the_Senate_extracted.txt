Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 98 2025-2026 Senator Manchester A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Russia High School boys basketball team on winning the 2025 Division VII State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Russia High School boys basketball team as the 2025 Division VII State Champion; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Russia High School boys basketball team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the players have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in basketball, and as a result, they won this year’s Division VII State Championship, finished the season with a perfect 29-0 record, and secured the one thousandth victory in program history; and WHEREAS, The achievements and leadership of the Russia High School boys basketball team are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the members, their families, their school, their community, and their coaches. In leading this team to victory, the coaching staff has not only helped the athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coaches for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Russia High School boys basketball team on its tremendous season and honor the members and coaches as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Russia High School boys basketball team.

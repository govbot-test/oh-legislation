Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 72 2025-2026 Representatives Abrams, Isaacsohn, Thomas, C., Denson, Baker, Brownlee, Odioso, Schmidt A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Seton High School varsity dance team on winning a 2025 UDA National Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Seton High School varsity dance team as the 2025 UDA National Champion in the Division II small pom category; and WHEREAS, At this year’s UDA National Championships in Orlando, Florida, the members of the Seton High School varsity dance team rose to the occasion and proved themselves to be a dynamic group with their score of 90.4375 to claim the Small Varsity Division II pom title, and in addition, they placed seventh in the hip hop category of their division. Truly, these outstanding athletes can be proud of the accomplishments that were hard-won and well‑deserved; and WHEREAS, With great admiration, we congratulate the athletes who comprise the Seton High School varsity dance team, for they have overcome many obstacles and displayed tremendous prowess. Indeed, we are confident that as they meet the challenges ahead, they will take with them the lessons of comradery, diligence, and discipline; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in this exciting activity, the members of the Seton High School varsity dance team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Seton High School varsity dance team on its national championship title and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Seton High School varsity dance team.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 114 2025-2026 Senator Patton A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the U.S. Marshals Service’s Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force on its 60,000th arrest. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are proud to commend the U.S. Marshals Service’s Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force on the occasion of its 60,000 arrests, March 24, 2025; and WHEREAS, Established in 2003, the U.S. Marshals Service’s Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force is a collaborative law enforcement effort between federal, state, and local agencies that has provided a unified offensive in the pursuit, apprehension, and prosecution of violent adult fugitives across the northern district of the state. Formed in direct support of Project Safe Neighborhoods, a nationwide initiative to reduce gun crime in America, it has expanded to include 125 local police departments and sheriff’s offices, 350 law enforcement personnel, and numerous outposts, and on March 24, 2025, the U.S. Marshals Service’s Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force was credited with securing its 60,000 arrests. This notable milestone has provided further proof of the agency’s dedication to promoting improved law enforcement methods that place the respect for law and order at a premium; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the U.S. Marshals Service’s Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force have made a measurable difference in the State of Ohio and across the country, and they are deserving of the highest praise for their expertise and commitment. Indeed, their invaluable contributions have set a fine example of humanitarian service, and their selfless endeavors to protect the welfare of their fellow Ohioans stand as the hallmark of responsible citizenship; and WHEREAS, The residents of our state owe their safety to the exemplary efforts of law enforcement officers such as the members of the U.S. Marshals Service’s Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force, for these fine professionals have performed their duties with an unwavering focus on excellence as well as estimable courage and initiative. Indeed, their integrity and cooperation have gained them the appreciation and reverence of many; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the U.S. Marshals Service’s Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force on its allegiance to the welfare of the State of Ohio and salute its members as fine Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the U.S. Marshals Service’s Northern Ohio Violent Fugitive Task Force.

Section 1:
As Reported by the House Veterans and Military Development Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 204 2025-2026 Representatives White, E., Miller, M. Cosponsors: Representatives Brewer, Synenberg, Brownlee, McNally, Brennan, Lawson-Rowe, Russo, Sims, Gross, Lear, Willis, Richardson, Newman, Jarrells, Tims, Upchurch, Rader, Hall, D., Lampton, Thomas, C. To enact section 5.212 of the Revised Code to designate March 9th as "6888th Central Postal Directory Battalion Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.212 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.212. The ninth day of March is designated as "6888th Central Postal Directory Battalion Day" to honor those who served in the 6888th central postal directory battalion, the only all-Black women's unit to serve overseas during World War II.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 37 2025-2026 Senator DeMora A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring The Ohio State University spirit team on securing a 2025 UCA & UDA College Cheerleading and Dance Team National crown. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud The Ohio State University spirit team on winning first-place honors in the Division IA spirit program - game day category at the 2025 Universal Cheerleaders Association & Universal Dance Association College Cheerleading and Dance Team National Championship; and WHEREAS, The Ohio State University spirit team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At the 2025 Universal Cheerleaders Association & Universal Dance Association College Cheerleading and Dance Team National Championship, the Buckeyes placed first in the Division IA spirit program - game day category for the second consecutive year, and the members amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of the members of The Ohio State University spirit team, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional coaching staff as well as the support of the Buckeye nation, these fine individuals have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise The Ohio State University spirit team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. We are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in events such as this; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute The Ohio State University spirit team on its 2025 UCA & UDA College Cheerleading and Dance Team National title and extend best wishes for a future filled with promise; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to The Ohio State University spirit team.

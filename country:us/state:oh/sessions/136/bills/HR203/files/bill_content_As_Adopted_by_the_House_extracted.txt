Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 203 2025-2026 Representative Santucci A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church on its One Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary, commemorated June 29, 2025; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this significant milestone is a fitting tribute to Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church, for over the past century, it has attained a praiseworthy record of service. The achievements of this fine house of worship are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on the church itself but also on its devoted pastor, the Reverend Miron Kerul’-Kmec Jr., on its members, and on the Warren community; and WHEREAS, The source of many social, religious, and humanitarian projects and programs, Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church has enhanced the quality of life in the area since its inception in 1925, and its generous contributions have earned it the gratitude and respect of the many citizens it has so capably served. We are certain that in the years to come, the church will maintain the tradition of spiritual enlightenment that has long been its hallmark; and WHEREAS, The members of the Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church have long been actively involved in, and concerned about, the events in their community, and this celebration offers them a unique opportunity to share their memories and to reflect on the benefits of life in their close-knit community. The congregation can, indeed, be proud of their church, for they have built on the best traditions of the past to ensure a peaceful and robust future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, we commend Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church on its One Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for continued growth and prosperity; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Saints Peter and Paul Byzantine Catholic Church.

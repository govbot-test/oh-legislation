A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Rotary Club of Canal Fulton on its One Hundredth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Rotary Club of Canal Fulton on its Centennial, celebrated June 13, 2026; and

WHEREAS, One hundred years is a distinctive milestone on which the Rotary Club of Canal Fulton can enthusiastically celebrate its mission, for through its philanthropic, civic, and social activities, it has enhanced the quality of life in our society. Founded in 1926, this iconic club has overcome challenges and persevered by remaining a relevant resource for the community, and it has paid homage to its long and distinguished history through its thoughtful outreach to the Canal Fulton area; and

WHEREAS, One of many reasons that the State of Ohio continues to prosper is because of organizations such as the Rotary Club of Canal Fulton. For a century, this exemplary group has engaged in an array of worthwhile activities and projects that have met a number of local needs, and we are certain that it will continue to positively impact our state for generations to come; and

WHEREAS, The members of the Rotary Club of Canal Fulton can always be relied upon to favor a worthy cause, and these exceptional people have made a lasting impact on the community through their admirable resolve and vision. We look with optimism to a society that will thrive as a result of the initiative of all those who have comprised the organization, both past and present; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Rotary Club of Canal Fulton on its One Hundredth Anniversary and salute its leadership and members as fine Ohioans; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Rotary Club of Canal Fulton.

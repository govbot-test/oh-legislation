Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 86 2025-2026 Representatives Kishman, Salvo A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Brice Fuller as a 2025 OATCCC Division III State Indoor Track and Field Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Brice Fuller on winning the Ohio Association of Track and Cross Country Coaches Division III State Indoor Track and Field Championship in the 3,200-meter run; and WHEREAS, A freshman at Fairless High School, Brice Fuller has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, he has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in running, and as a result, he placed first in the 3,200-meter run with a time of 9:34.87 at this year’s OATCCC Division III State indoor track and field meet; and WHEREAS, Brice Fuller’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his coaches, his school, and his community. His unwavering commitment to excellence has earned him the respect and esteem of many, and he is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Brice Fuller on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience he has gained through his participation in track and field will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Brice Fuller on his state championship and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Brice Fuller.

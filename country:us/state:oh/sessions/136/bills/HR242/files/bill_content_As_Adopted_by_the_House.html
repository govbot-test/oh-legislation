A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring First National Bank on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to First National Bank on the memorable occasion of its Sesquicentennial; and

WHEREAS, First National Bank was founded in 1875 in Bellevue, and it has since experienced consistent growth and prosperity due to its attention to friendly, reliable, and convenient service. Indeed, this remarkable financial institution has provided countless individuals in the local communities with outstanding fiscal guidance and leadership, and it has gained a well-deserved reputation for professionalism over the years; and

WHEREAS, Throughout the past fifteen decades, First National Bank has witnessed many fluctuations in the economic climate of our state and nation, but it has always remained responsive to the needs and concerns of its customers. This worthy enterprise has enhanced the quality of life in the communities it serves, and we are certain that as the bank begins another decade of service, it will continue in the tradition of excellence that has been its hallmark since its founding; and

WHEREAS, The success of First National Bank is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and hard-working employees. Their vision and initiative have earned the institution the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied customers, and they are truly deserving of high praise; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend First National Bank on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to First National Bank.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 89 2025-2026 Senator Brenner A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Olentangy Orange High School boys basketball team on winning the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Olentangy Orange High School boys basketball team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; and WHEREAS, The members of the Olentangy Orange High School boys basketball team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly exceptional competitors. Triumphing throughout the 2024-25 season, the Pioneers achieved their ultimate goal and won the Division I State Championship, the first such title in program history, and the unwavering commitment of the players and coaches has earned them the esteem of many. They are certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Coach Anthony Calo has demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the Olentangy Orange High School boys basketball team to victory, and his skill is reflected in the squad’s outstanding accomplishments. Due largely to his tireless efforts, the members have distinguished themselves on the court, and he has received respect and admiration from all who know him; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Olentangy Orange High School boys basketball team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Olentangy Orange High School boys basketball team as the 2025 Division I State Champion and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Olentangy Orange High School boys basketball team.

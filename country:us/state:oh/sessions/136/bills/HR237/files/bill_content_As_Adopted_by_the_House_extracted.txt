Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 237 2025-2026 Representative Young A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Centerville High School Revengeful Mushrooms Destination Imagination Team on tying for first place at the 2025 Destination Imagination Global Finals. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Centerville High School Revengeful Mushrooms Destination Imagination team on securing a share of first place in the technical challenge - breaking point secondary level event at the 2025 Destination Imagination Global Finals; and WHEREAS, The members of the Centerville High School Revengeful Mushrooms Destination Imagination team are, indeed, a remarkable group of young academicians, successfully combining creativity and innovation with collaborative effort and determination to establish themselves as truly exemplary students. With commendable dedication, they advanced to this year’s Global Finals in the technical challenge - breaking point event, where they competed against other teams to design and build assembly and destruction equipment to create and dismantle a stack of items while also presenting a story, and due to their tremendous performance, they tied for first place in the secondary level; and WHEREAS, In preparing themselves for this important contest, the Centerville High School Revengeful Mushrooms Destination Imagination team members have had the opportunity to develop and express their individual abilities and to acquire knowledge and information which will be of great benefit to them throughout their lives. Having worked diligently to sharpen their skills, they confronted competition with confidence and enthusiasm and distinguished themselves as outstanding representatives of their school, community, state, and nation; and WHEREAS, The accomplishments of the Centerville High School Revengeful Mushrooms Destination Imagination team members are a justifiable source of pride not only for the students but also for their coaches, their families, their school, and their community. The maturity and experience these young Ohioans have gained through the participation in the DI program will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the years to come; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Centerville High School Revengeful Mushrooms Destination Imagination team for outstanding achievement and wish its members a bright, rewarding future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Centerville High School Revengeful Mushrooms Destination Imagination team.

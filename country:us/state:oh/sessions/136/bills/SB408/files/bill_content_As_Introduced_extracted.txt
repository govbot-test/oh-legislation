Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 408 2025-2026 Senator DeMora To enact section 5.64 of the Revised Code to designate August 20 as "Benjamin Harrison Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.64 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.64. The twentieth day of August is designated as "Benjamin Harrison Day" in recognition of President Benjamin Harrison, who was born on that day in North Bend, Ohio, in 1833.

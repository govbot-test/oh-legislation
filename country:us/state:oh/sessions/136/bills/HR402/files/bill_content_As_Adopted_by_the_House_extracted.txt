Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 402 2025-2026 Representative Grim A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Ottawa Hills High School Quiz Bowl team as the 2026 National Academic Quiz Tournaments Ohio State Small School Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Ottawa Hills High School Quiz Bowl team on winning the 2026 National Academic Quiz Tournaments Ohio State Small School Championship title; and WHEREAS, The members of the Ottawa Hills High School Quiz Bowl team are a remarkable group of young academicians, combining talent and ambitious spirit with exceptional knowledge in a wide range of subjects to earn this year’s National Academic Quiz Tournaments Ohio State Small School crown, their first such title. Throughout long hours of practice and preparation, competitors Meral Humaida, Rohan Padhye, Sahil Parikh, Zacharia Rashid, Jasper Allred, Josef Duley, Pranathi Gundabolu, Sam Kapadia, Audrey Kelm, Rory Norris, Sungjoo Park, Evelyn Parsons, and Emily Timmerman have displayed a degree of determination and motivation of which they can be proud; and WHEREAS, The success of the Ottawa Hills High School Quiz Bowl team is a fitting tribute to the excellent leadership provided by its advisor, Tim Adkins, who has given tirelessly of his time, energy, and expertise to guide these scholars. The maturity and experience the students have gained through their participation in this event will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; and WHEREAS, In preparing themselves for competition, the individuals who comprise the Ottawa Hills High School Quiz Bowl team have had the opportunity to express their unique abilities and to acquire information and experience that will be of great benefit to them throughout their lifetimes. Demonstrating a sincere desire to fully develop their skills, they are cultivating their ability to assume meaningful roles in our society as the leaders of tomorrow; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Ottawa Hills High School Quiz Bowl team on its tremendous performance and salute its members and coaches as fine Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Ottawa Hills High School Quiz Bowl team.

Senator Romanchuk


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Shelby High School girls 4x200-meter relay team as the Emerging Elite winner at the 2026 Nike National Indoor Championships.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Shelby High School girls 4x200-meter relay team on winning the Emerging Elite title at the 2026 Nike National Indoor Championships; and

WHEREAS, The Shelby High School girls 4x200-meter relay team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year’s Nike National Indoor Championships, members Princess Timko, Ashley Montgomery, Madison Henkel, and Karlie Walp secured the Emerging Elite title with a time of 1:45.28, and they amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and

WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional Shelby High School girls 4x200-meter relay team coaching staff, as well as the support of their school community, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and

WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Shelby High School girls 4x200-meter relay team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in track and field; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Shelby High School girls 4x200-meter relay team on its national title and count its members and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Shelby High School girls 4x200-meter relay team.

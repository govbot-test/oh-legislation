Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 165 2025-2026 Senator Landis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Donald’s Donuts on being voted the Best Donut Shop in the United States. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Donald’s Donuts on being named the Best Donut Shop in the United States by the 2025 USA Today Readers’ Choice Awards; and WHEREAS, This is a remarkable honor, and congratulations are in order. Donald’s Donuts has become a beloved part of the Zanesville community, and after a rigorous selection and screening process, it was voted the 2025 Best Donut Shop in the United States. Considering its positive impact on the community, it is no surprise that this fine endeavor is so widely held in high regard; and WHEREAS, The noteworthy success of Donald’s Donuts is attributable largely to the loyal service of its committed staff, as well as to the leadership of its management. Their combined efforts have earned the shop a well-deserved reputation for achievement and have distinguished the business as a worthy enterprise that has contributed greatly to the well-being of the area; and WHEREAS, Since its establishment in 1960, Donald’s Donuts has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area, and it is truly deserving of high praise. We are certain that as this noteworthy endeavor maintains its unwavering dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to grow and prosper and will follow in the tradition of excellence that has become its hallmark; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize Donald’s Donuts for outstanding attainment and look with optimism to its ongoing contributions to the community; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Donald’s Donuts.

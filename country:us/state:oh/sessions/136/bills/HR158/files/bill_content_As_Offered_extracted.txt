Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 158 2025-2026 Representative Hoops A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Hunter Spangler as a 2025 Division III State Track and Field Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Hunter Spangler on winning the 2025 Division III State Championship in the shot put; and WHEREAS, A junior at Liberty Center High School, Hunter Spangler has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, he has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in his event, and as a result, he placed first in the shot put with a toss of 63 feet, 1.25 inches at this year’s Division III State Meet; and WHEREAS, Hunter Spangler’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his coaches, his school, and his community. His unwavering commitment to excellence has earned him the respect and esteem of many, and he is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to Hunter Spangler on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience he has gained through his participation in track and field will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Hunter Spangler as the 2025 Division III State Track and Field Champion in the shot put and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Hunter Spangler.

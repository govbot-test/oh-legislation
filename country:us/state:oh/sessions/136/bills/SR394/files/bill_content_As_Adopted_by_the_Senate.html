Senator Romanchuk


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Shelby High School girls 4x100-meter relay team as the 2026 Division III State Champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Shelby High School girls 4x100-meter relay team on securing the 2026 Division III State Championship; and

WHEREAS, The Shelby High School girls 4x100-meter relay team has achieved a tremendous feat in winning this year's Division III state title with a new school record of 47.76. The squad's strong performance and drive to succeed have led to its outstanding victory, and it has earned the appreciation and admiration of many; and

WHEREAS, The accomplishments and development of the Shelby High School girls 4x100‑meter relay team are fine examples of what can be attained by a group of hard-working young people supported by a dedicated coaching staff. Throughout extensive conditioning and training, team members Princess Timko, Ashley Montgomery, Karlie Walp, and Madison Henkel have developed into dynamic competitors, and they are deserving of acknowledgement; and

WHEREAS, With great admiration, we congratulate the athletes who comprise the Shelby High School girls 4x100-meter relay team, for they have overcome many formidable opponents and displayed tremendous prowess. Indeed, we are confident that as they meet their future challenges, they will take with them the lessons of comradery, diligence, and discipline; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, heartily applaud the Shelby High School girls 4x100-meter relay team as the 2026 Division III State Champion and praise the strength of this program and its participating athletes; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Shelby High School girls 4x100-meter relay team.

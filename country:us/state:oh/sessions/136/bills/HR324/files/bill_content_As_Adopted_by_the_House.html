136th General Assembly

2025-2026


Representative Fischer


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Dr. Robert Reynolds on being inducted into the 2026 National Bird Dog Hall of Fame.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Dr. Robert Reynolds on being selected as an inductee into the 2026 National Bird Dog Hall of Fame; and

WHEREAS, This prestigious honor is a fitting tribute to Robert Reynolds in recognition of his exemplary contributions to breeding and training bird dogs. A local veterinarian who had hunted with bird dogs for many years, he became a top breeder and trainer for hunt tests and field trials, with several of his animals winning national awards and championships, and he also served as a judge of numerous high-profile field trial events. A member of the Canfield Sportsman Club, he has been active with the German Shorthaired Pointer Club of Ohio since 1998, holding various posts and helping to restart the National German Shorthaired Pointer Association of Ohio Championship in 2008, and he served on the NGSPA Board for six years. He is, indeed, worthy of acknowledgement; and

WHEREAS, Robert Reynolds is truly deserving of high praise, for he has combined civic concern and commitment with selfless initiative to become a dynamic leader in the field trial community. Through his unwavering dedication to excellence, he has certainly distinguished himself as a conscientious and hard-working Ohioan, and we applaud him on his fine efforts; and

WHEREAS, Robert Reynolds’ exceptional record of personal and professional achievement stands as a hallmark worthy of emulation, and as a result of his passion for the sport, he has inspired countless individuals to excel in various areas of endeavor. The respect and admiration he has earned throughout his life are clearly evident, and his accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on him but also on his family and his community; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Dr. Robert Reynolds on his induction into the National Bird Dog Hall of Fame and salute him as one of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Dr. Robert Reynolds.

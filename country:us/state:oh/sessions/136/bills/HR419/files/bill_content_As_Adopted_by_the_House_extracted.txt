hr419_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 419 2025-2026 Representative Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Avon High School winter guard as a 2026 OIPA State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Avon High School winter guard on winning first place in the scholastic A class at the 2026 Ohio Indoor Performance Association State Championship; and WHEREAS, The members of the Avon High School winter guard are to be commended for their remarkable fitness and competitive drive, qualities that have figured prominently in the squad's impressive performances. To their credit, these fine young students could always be counted on to execute even the most demanding routines with agility, precision, and exuberance, and consequently, they secured first place in the scholastic A class at this year's Ohio Indoor Performance Association State Championship; and WHEREAS, Under the expert guidance of their mentor, Laurel Mitchell, the members of the Avon High School winter guard have trained diligently in order to ensure peak conditioning, and their exceptional prowess has earned them many admirers. Participation in this activity has enabled them to learn much about creative expression, self-discipline, leadership, and commitment, and clearly, the experience they have acquired is certain to benefit them in the years to come; and WHEREAS, Throughout long hours of practice and competition, the members of the Avon High School winter guard have consistently demonstrated the talent, dedication, confidence, and motivation which are the hallmarks of true champions. The maturity and experience they have gained through their participation in this year's OIPA State Championship will undoubtedly assist them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Avon High School winter guard on its exemplary achievements and extend best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Avon High School winter guard.

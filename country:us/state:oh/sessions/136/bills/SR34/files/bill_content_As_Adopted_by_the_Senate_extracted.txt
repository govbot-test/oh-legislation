Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 34 2025-2026 Senator McColley Cosponsors: Senators Blessing, Brenner, Chavez, Cirino, Cutrona, Gavarone, Huffman, Johnson, Koehler, Landis, Lang, Manchester, Manning, O'Brien, Patton, Reineke, Reynolds, Roegner, Romanchuk, Schaffer, Timken, Wilkin, Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Regan Burke-Onda for outstanding service to the State of Ohio. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Regan Burke-Onda for her loyal service to the Ohio Senate; and WHEREAS, Regan Burke-Onda is a remarkable individual, for she has combined civic concern and dedication with selfless initiative to become a dynamic member of the Ohio Senate staff. Commencing her tenure in 2017, she served in such office posts as a page for Senators Cliff Hite and Frank Hoagland, a legislative aide for Senators Randy Gardner and Frank Hoagland, and a senior legislative aide for Senator Hoagland before becoming the executive assistant for President Matt Huffman and Chief of Staff John Barron in 2020, adding the title of policy advisor to that role in 2022; and WHEREAS, Over the years, Regan Burke-Onda has given generously of her time, energy, and abilities to assist the Ohio Senate and has demonstrated a praiseworthy commitment to the success of state government. She has provided a valuable service by helping to maintain the efficiency and effectiveness of the legislative process, and without the efforts of people such as she, many services that are currently available to the constituents of our state would cease to exist; and WHEREAS, Regan Burke-Onda’s contributions to the Ohio Senate have been greatly appreciated by all those who have had the pleasure of working with her. She has displayed exceptional devotion, responsibility, and enthusiasm in her endeavors, and we extend best wishes for the years to come; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Regan Burke-Onda on her exemplary state service and salute her as one of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Regan Burke-Onda.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 182 2025-2026 Representative Dean Cosponsors: Representatives Johnson, Gross, Williams, Lear A BILL To enact new section 6109.20 and to repeal section 6109.20 of the Revised Code to prohibit a public water system from adding fluoride to its water. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That new section 6109.20 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 6109.20. No public water system shall add fluoride to the water supplied by the system. Section 2. That section 6109.20 of the Revised Code is hereby repealed.

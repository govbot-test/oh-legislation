Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 41 2025-2026 Representative Hoops A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Ella Borstelman on winning the 2025 Division II State Diving Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Ella Borstelman as the 2025 Division II State Champion in girls diving; and WHEREAS, A standout for the Napoleon High School girls swimming and diving team, Ella Borstelman can always be counted on not only to shine individually but also to contribute significantly to the team’s collective effort. Indeed, she made her coaches and family very proud at this year's Division II State Diving Championship, where she posted a score of 431.75 to win the title, and this accomplishment has inspired her teammates, thrilled her fans, and gained the respect of her opponents; and WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, and commitment during the 2024-2025 season, Ella Borstelman has certainly distinguished herself as one of the most skilled divers in Ohio. Through her participation in athletic competition, she has learned much about dedication, self-discipline, and leadership, and it is our hope that her example will serve as an inspiration for others; and WHEREAS, With sports playing such an important part in the development of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to such a remarkable athlete as Ella Borstelman. Thanks to her remarkable talents and her ambition to succeed, she has established a tremendous record of achievement, and it is no surprise that she has won so many admirers; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Ella Borstelman on her state championship meet and look with optimism to her bright, promising future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Ella Borstelman.

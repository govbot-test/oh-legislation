Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 177 2025-2026 Representative McClain A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Dostal and Kirk Insurance and Financial Services on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Dostal and Kirk Insurance and Financial Services as it celebrates the One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary of its founding; and WHEREAS, Since its inception, Dostal and Kirk Insurance and Financial Services has experienced tremendous prosperity thanks to the valuable services it has provided to its clients. Originating with the founding of the Auck Dostal Agency by John Schaber in 1875, the company has experienced a rich and prosperous history marked by mergers, expansions, achievements, and triumphs, and over the course of the past one hundred fifty years, its exemplary commitment to the highest industry standards has never faltered. Indeed, its dedication to the best interests of the customer has been evident in all its transactions; and WHEREAS, Ohio’s economic well-being depends on forward-thinking and innovative entrepreneurs to provide goods and services to the citizens of our state. On the celebration of this event, we are proud to note that it is through the diligent efforts of individuals such as all those associated with the Dostal and Kirk Insurance and Financial Services, who are willing to invest in the future of their community, that the State of Ohio remains a pleasant place in which to live; and WHEREAS, The noteworthy success of Dostal and Kirk Insurance and Financial Services is attributable largely to the loyal service of its qualified and committed staff, as well as to the innovation and foresight of its management. Their combined efforts have earned the venture a well-deserved reputation for excellence and have distinguished the organization as a worthy enterprise that has contributed greatly to the area’s economic well-being; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to Dostal and Kirk Insurance and Financial Services on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary and salute it as one of Ohio’s finest businesses; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Dostal and Kirk Insurance and Financial Services.

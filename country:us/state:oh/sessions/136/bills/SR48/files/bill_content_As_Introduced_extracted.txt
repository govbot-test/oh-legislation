Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 48 2025-2026 Senator Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Cincinnati Hills Christian Academy girls 400-yard freestyle relay team as the 2025 Division II State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Cincinnati Hills Christian Academy girls 400-yard freestyle relay team on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship; and WHEREAS, The members of the Cincinnati Hills Christian Academy girls 400-yard freestyle relay team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly exceptional competitors. At this year's Division II State Meet, Taylor Bacher, Julia Shafer, Posey Sollmann, and Sophia Fink secured first place in the 400-yard freestyle relay event with an impressive time of 3:28.38, and the unwavering commitment of the swimmers has earned them the esteem of many. They are certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Coach Donald Rielag has demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the Cincinnati Hills Christian Academy girls 400-yard freestyle relay team to victory, and his skill is reflected in the squad’s outstanding accomplishments. Due largely to his tireless efforts, the members have distinguished themselves in the pool, and he has received respect and admiration from all who know him; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Cincinnati Hills Christian Academy girls 400-yard freestyle relay team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Cincinnati Hills Christian Academy girls 400-yard freestyle relay team on their remarkable achievement and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Cincinnati Hills Christian Academy girls 400-yard freestyle relay team.

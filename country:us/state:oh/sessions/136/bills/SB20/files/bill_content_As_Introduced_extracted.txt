Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 20 2025-2026 Senators Antonio, Manning Cosponsors: Senators Romanchuk, Wilson, Craig, DeMora, Weinstein A BILL To enact section 5.2540 of the Revised Code to designate the first week of May as "Ohio Stroke Awareness Week." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2540 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2540. The first week of May is designated as "Ohio Stroke Awareness Week."

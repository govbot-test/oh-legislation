Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 199 2025-2026 Representative LaRe A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Grace Alls as the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Champion in the 400-meter dash. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Grace Alls on your exemplary performance at the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Meet; and WHEREAS, Grace Alls’ accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for she has clearly demonstrated her talent and perseverance, as well as her mental and physical fortitude as a runner. At this year’s Division I State Track and Field Meet, she placed first in the 400-meter dash with a time of 54.10 and helped the 4x200-meter relay team place first with a time of 1:37.32, and she has exemplified all of the characteristics which are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, integrity, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; and WHEREAS, By developing such remarkable prowess as a member of the Pickerington High School Central girls track and field team, Grace Alls has earned the appreciation of her fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even her most determined rivals. Her outstanding record of achievement stands as a hallmark worthy of emulation, and she has inspired countless individuals to excel in numerous areas of endeavor; and WHEREAS, Running is an event which requires exceptional stamina and natural ability, and Grace Alls is to be commended for her commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining her athletic goals, she has certainly distinguished herself as a true champion; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Grace Alls on her recent distinction and salute her as a fine Ohioan; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Grace Alls.

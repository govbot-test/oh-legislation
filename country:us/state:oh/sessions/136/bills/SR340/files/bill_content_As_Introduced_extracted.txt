Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 340 2025-2026 Senator Hicks-Hudson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the East Toledo Family Center on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the East Toledo Family Center on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Since its founding in 1901, the East Toledo Family Center has offered innumerable people the tools and support necessary for them to succeed. For more than a century, it has been a pillar of the East Toledo community, and we applaud its important work; and WHEREAS, Under its capable leadership, both past and present, the East Toledo Family Center has experienced continual growth and has served as a lifeline in the community. Over the years, the center has engaged in an array of worthwhile activities and projects that have met a number of local needs, and we are certain it will continue to do so for years to come; and WHEREAS, An occasion such as this is an important milestone that represents dedication and commitment. For one hundred twenty-five years, the people behind the East Toledo Family Center have served their fellow Ohioans, and on this special anniversary, these individuals have accepted the challenge of continuing this mission into the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend special recognition to the East Toledo Family Center on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary and pay tribute to all those joining in the celebration of this joyous event; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the East Toledo Family Center.

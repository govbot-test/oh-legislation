As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 103 2025-2026 Representative LaRe A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Village of Millersport on its Two Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Village of Millersport on its Bicentennial; and WHEREAS, The Village of Millersport was formed in 1825, and recognition of its Two Hundredth Anniversary is, indeed, a fitting tribute to this fine community. Over the past two centuries, it has become known for its residents’ cooperation, integrity, and willingness to work hard, and these exemplary qualities are still manifested by its citizens today; and WHEREAS, Throughout Millersport’s existence, its generous and civic-minded population has enjoyed a wealth of benefits, and those who currently make the village their home take great pride in their community and its rich heritage. Their efforts have certainly made the area a pleasant and friendly place in which to live and work; and WHEREAS, America is only as strong as her individual communities, which are unique and which make significant contributions to the culture of our state and nation. The Bicentennial of the Village of Millersport offers its citizens the opportunity to share their memories and experiences and to reflect on the benefits of life in their close-knit community, for they have built on the best traditions of the past to ensure a future filled with hope and promise; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Village of Millersport on its Two Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for peace and prosperity in the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Village of Millersport.

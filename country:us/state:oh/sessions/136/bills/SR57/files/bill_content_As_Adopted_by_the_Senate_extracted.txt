Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 57 2025-2026 Senator Manchester A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Urbana High School girls bowling team on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Urbana High School girls bowling team as the 2025 Division II State Champion; and WHEREAS, The members of the Urbana High School girls bowling team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly exceptional competitors. To cap a fantastic season, the Hillclimbers bested the Fairport Harding team at the 2025 Division II State Championship, and team members Audrie Williams, Natalie Turner, Jazmyn Scott, Riley Smith, Emily Fisher, and Mallory Stokes have earned the esteem of many for their unwavering commitment; and WHEREAS, Coach Kurt Scott has demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the Urbana High School girls bowling team to victory, and his skill is reflected in the squad’s outstanding accomplishments. Due largely to his tireless efforts, the members have distinguished themselves on the lanes, and he has received respect and admiration from all who know him; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Urbana High School girls bowling team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Urbana High School girls bowling team on its 2025 Division II State Championship and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Urbana High School girls bowling team.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 141 2025-2026 Senator McColley A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Village of Hamler on its Sesquicentennial. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Village of Hamler on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, America is only as strong as her individual communities, which are unique and which make significant contributions to the culture of our state and nation. Since its founding in 1875, the Village of Hamler has experienced dramatic growth and change, and yet, commendably, it has retained the many engaging qualities that have endeared it to generations of inhabitants. Clearly, it has built on the best traditions of the past to ensure a future filled with hope and promise; and WHEREAS, Over the past century and a half, the Village of Hamler has been known for the friendliness of its residents, the professionalism of its officials, and the industriousness of its leaders, and all those who have lived or worked in this beautiful community have just cause to be proud. Indeed, they have exhibited cooperation, integrity, and a willingness to join together to advance the common good, and they have built on the traditions of the past in order to ensure a prosperous future; and WHEREAS, The One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary of the Village of Hamler offers its citizens the opportunity to share their memories and experiences and to reflect on life in their close-knit community. A vigorous commitment to public service and an earnest devotion to lofty ideals have long been second nature to many of the village residents, and vigilance, industriousness, and generosity have come to typify their collective character; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Village of Hamler on its Sesquicentennial and extend best wishes for continued peace and prosperity; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Village of Hamler.

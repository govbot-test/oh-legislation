Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 183 2025-2026 Senator Koehler A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Clark County Special Olympics volleyball team on winning the 2025 State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Clark County Special Olympics volleyball team as the 2025 State Champion; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and spirited squad, the members of the Clark County Special Olympics volleyball team have developed into an accomplished group of athletes whose talent is equaled only by their admirable display of teamwork. Without exception, they had prepared conscientiously in pursuit of the championship, devoting countless hours to training and practice in order to ensure peak conditioning; and WHEREAS, The members of the Clark County Special Olympics volleyball team have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in all of their endeavors. Indeed, they have epitomized the motivation and poise that befit authentic champions, and as a result, they defeated Pickerington to win the state championship at this year’s Ohio Special Olympics Summer Games; and WHEREAS, Each athlete on the Clark County Special Olympics volleyball team has exemplified the qualities necessary for excellence both in and out of sports. Through their participation in Special Olympics, these fine players have learned lessons of hard work and good sportsmanship that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to them throughout their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Clark County Special Olympics volleyball team on its success and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Clark County Special Olympics volleyball team.

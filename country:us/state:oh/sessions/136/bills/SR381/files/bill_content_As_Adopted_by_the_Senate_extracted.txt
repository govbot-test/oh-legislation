sr381_01_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 381 2025-2026 Senator Landis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Carsen Hostetler as the 2026 Division III State Champion in the 300-meter hurdles. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Carsen Hostetler on winning first place in the 300-meter hurdles at the 2026 Division III State Track and Field Meet; and WHEREAS, This accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for Carsen Hostetler has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his mental and physical fortitude as an athlete. At this year's Division III State Track and Field Meet, he secured first place in the 300-meter hurdles with a time of 37.01 and also placed third in the 110-meter hurdles with a time of 14.16, and he is deserving of the highest praise for his efforts; and WHEREAS, In displaying such exceptional prowess as a junior member of the Indian Valley High School boys track and field team, Carsen Hostetler has earned the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. Competition in athletics has enabled him to learn much about dedication and self-discipline, and we take this opportunity to acknowledge his exceptional record of achievement; and WHEREAS, Hurdling is an event that requires exceptional stamina and natural ability, and Carsen Hostetler is to be commended for his commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining his athletic goals, he has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity that are the hallmarks of a true champion, and he has exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, determination, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Carsen Hostetler on his tremendous performance at the 2026 Division III State track and field meet and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Carsen Hostetler.

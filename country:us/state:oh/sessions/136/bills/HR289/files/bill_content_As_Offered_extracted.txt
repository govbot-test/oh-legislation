Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 289 2025-2026 Representative White, A. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Oakwood High School girls cross country team as the 2025 Division III State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Oakwood High School girls cross country team on winning the 2025 Division III State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Oakwood High School girls cross country team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the Jills have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in long-distance running, and as a result, they secured this year’s Division III State Championship crown, the first such title in program history; and WHEREAS, The Jills’ achievements and leadership are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the runners, their families, their school, their community, and their coach, Kurt Sanford. In leading his team to victory, Coach Sanford has not only helped the athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coach for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Oakwood High School girls cross country team on its tremendous season and salute the members and coach as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Oakwood High School girls cross country team.

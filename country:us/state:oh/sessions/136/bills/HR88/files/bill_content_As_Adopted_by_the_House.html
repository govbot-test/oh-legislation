136th General Assembly

2025-2026


Representative Barhorst


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Brogan Tucker on winning a 2025 Division III State Wrestling Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize Brogan Tucker on securing the 2025 Division III State Wrestling Championship in the 157-pound weight class; and

WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication during the 2024-25 season, Brogan Tucker has certainly distinguished himself as one of the most talented wrestlers in the State of Ohio. After winning state championships in each of the past three years, he continued his historic run by claiming the Division III 157-pound weight class this season; and

WHEREAS, Brogan Tucker has conscientiously applied himself to the quest for excellence as an athlete, and time and again, he has risen to the occasion, maintaining his morale, composure, and good sportsmanship. To his credit, he could always be counted on not only to shine individually but also to contribute significantly to his teammates’ collective effort; and

WHEREAS, A student at Graham High School, Brogan Tucker is truly worthy of acknowledgement for the formidable athletic gifts and the competitive spirit that he has so amply revealed throughout this past season. He has inspired his teammates, thrilled his fans, gained the respect of his opponents, and made his coaches and family very proud; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Brogan Tucker on his impressive wrestling accomplishment and offer best wishes for a bright, rewarding future; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Brogan Tucker.

sr416_00_IN As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 416 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Alexis Miller as the 2026 Miss Ohio. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Alexis Miller on being crowned Miss Ohio for 2026; and WHEREAS, An intelligent, poised, and motivated individual, Alexis Miller has developed her knowledge and skills and has striven to meet challenges with enthusiasm and confidence. A remarkable woman, she has displayed an exceptional willingness to work hard to attain superior results, and for her unfaltering devotion to excellence, she has been selected as this year's Miss Ohio; and WHEREAS, Alexis Miller has derived extra energy from her many activities and accomplishments, which include her service project, Take Heart that is sure to have a tremendous impact, and her integrity and steadfast determination are truly admirable. Indeed, her amiable style will carry her far beyond this event and will be of great value to her in the years to come; and WHEREAS, The achievements of Alexis Miller are a justifiable source of pride not only for her family and friends but also for the entire Newark community. She has shown a sincere desire to fully develop her talents so that she might fulfill her goals in the future, and this, coupled with her responsibility and initiative, shows her potential to serve as an outstanding ambassador for our state; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Alexis Miller on her recent accolade and extend best wishes for continued happiness and fulfillment; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Alexis Miller.

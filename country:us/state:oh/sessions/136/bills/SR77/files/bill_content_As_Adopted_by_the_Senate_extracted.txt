Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 77 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Village of Millersport on its Bicentennial. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Village of Millersport on the jubilant occasion of its Two Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, This is, indeed, an auspicious milestone, and celebration is certainly in order. Since its inception two hundred years ago, the Village of Millersport has experienced dramatic growth and change, and yet, commendably, even as its residents have exuberantly embraced the promise of a bright future, they have never relinquished their appreciation for the worthiest virtues of the past. Without sacrificing their sense of tradition in the name of progress, the people of Millersport have managed to nurture a fresh perspective and a positive outlook that endear this distinctive place to all who know it well; and WHEREAS, Two centuries after it was founded, Millersport still radiates vitality, optimism, and a charm all its own, and its success is a testament to the collective pride shared by the village’s astute leadership, hard-working public servants, and civic-minded residents. Clearly, it is a place of many engaging qualities, and its schools, businesses, and organizations both embody and thrive upon the values of which our flag is an emblem; and WHEREAS, Every state depends upon such vibrant, durable communities as Millersport for its own survival and stability. Although it has known adversity and tribulation, the village also has been favored with its share of blessings. In the years to come, Millersport is sure to remain on its admirable course and to continue to transform challenges into opportunities; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend our congratulations to the Village of Millersport on the observance of its Bicentennial and offer our best wishes for its perennial good fortune; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Village of Millersport.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 45 2025-2026 Senator Koehler A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Dave Chappelle as a 2025 Grammy Award winner. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Dave Chappelle on receiving a 2025 Grammy Award in the best comedy album category; and WHEREAS, In being awarded this prestigious honor for The Dreamer, Dave Chappelle has received a true compliment to his exceptional expression and creativity. For the third consecutive year, he has won a Grammy Award in the best comedy album category, his sixth such accolade overall, and he can be justly proud of this accomplishment; and WHEREAS, Dave Chappelle’s high personal standards and unwavering dedication to excellence have won him the appreciation and esteem of many, resulting in much critical acclaim. He clearly has a special comedic gift that brings joy to those with whom he shares his talents; and WHEREAS, As a comedian, Dave Chappelle has striven to hone his skills, developing his craft with timing and enthusiasm, and his art has given him great personal pride and satisfaction throughout the years. He has certainly excelled in his endeavors, and we wish him continued success; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Dave Chappelle on his 2025 Grammy Award and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Dave Chappelle.

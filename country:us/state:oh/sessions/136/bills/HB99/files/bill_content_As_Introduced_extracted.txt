Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 99 2025-2026 Representative Peterson Cosponsors: Representatives Barhorst, Creech, Daniels, Deeter, Fowler Arthur, Gross, Hiner, Johnson, Jones, Klopfenstein, Miller, K., Mullins, Richardson, Robb Blasdel, Williams, Rogers, White, E., King A BILL To enact section 3901.96 of the Revised Code to exclude nonprofit agricultural membership organizations from insurance regulations. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 3901.96 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 3901.96. (A) As used in this section, a "nonprofit agricultural membership organization" means an organization or an affiliate of an organization that meets both of the following: (1) The organization was incorporated in this state on or before December 31, 1919, for the purpose of promoting the interests of farmers in this state. (2) The organization provides healthcare benefit coverage exclusively to members of the organization and the members' families pursuant to contracts between the members and the organization or its affiliate. (B) A nonprofit agricultural membership organization is not subject to Title XXXIX or Chapter 1751. of the Revised Code or the jurisdiction of the superintendent of insurance. Healthcare benefit coverage provided by a nonprofit agricultural membership organization to the organization's members is not insurance and is not a health benefit contract under any law of this state. (C) The risks arising out of healthcare benefit coverage provided by a nonprofit agricultural membership organization may be assumed or reinsured by a company authorized to conduct the business of insurance in this state.

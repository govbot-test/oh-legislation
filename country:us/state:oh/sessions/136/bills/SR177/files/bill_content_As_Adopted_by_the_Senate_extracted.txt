Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 177 2025-2026 Senator Landis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring The Budget on its One Hundred Thirty-fifth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to The Budget on the memorable occasion of its One Hundred Thirty-fifth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, The Budget is, indeed, deserving of high praise, for throughout thirteen and a half decades of operation, this fine newspaper has attained a noteworthy record of service not only to the Sugarcreek area but also to the Amish and Mennonite communities throughout the nation that are served by the publication. Indeed, the paper has gained the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied readers, and its success is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its owners, on its employees, and on the many volunteer scribes who contribute to its success; and WHEREAS, Since its establishment, The Budget has experienced tremendous prosperity, and it has continually demonstrated its exemplary commitment to the highest standards of journalism, impartial reporting, and competitive pricing. Indeed, its dedication to the best interests of its audience has been evident in all its efforts; and WHEREAS, The success of The Budget can be attributed to the vision and foresight of a host of innovative individuals, and all those who have been involved with its operations are to be applauded on their tremendous efforts. Indeed, this venture convincingly demonstrates how very much can be accomplished by a group of conscientious people with clear objectives and firm resolve, and it is through the work of citizens such as these that Ohio continues to be a pleasant place in which to live; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to The Budget on its One Hundred Thirty-fifth Anniversary and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to The Budget.

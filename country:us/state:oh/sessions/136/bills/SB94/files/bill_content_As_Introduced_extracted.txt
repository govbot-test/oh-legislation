Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 94 2025-2026 Senator DeMora A BILL To enact section 3345.88 of the Revised Code regarding broadcasts of intercollegiate athletic competitions. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 3345.88 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 3345.88. (A) As used in this section: (1) "University athletic event" means an intercollegiate athletic competition in which a student-athlete or team of student-athletes affiliated with a state university participate. (2) "State university" has the same meaning as in section 3345.011 of the Revised Code. (B) To the extent permitted by federal law, no state university shall enter into or renew a contract under which the media rights to broadcast a university athletic event are granted exclusively to a streaming service. This division applies only to contracts entered into or renewed on or after the effective date of this section. (C) Each state university shall provide enrolled students, at no cost to the students, with access to observe any university athletic events that are broadcast in accordance with a contract entered into or renewed in accordance with division (B) of this section. Each state university shall determine the manner in which such access is provided to students.

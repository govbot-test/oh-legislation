2025-2026


Senator Blackshear


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team on winning the 2026 Division III State Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to salute the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team as the 2026 Division III State Champion; and

WHEREAS, Establishing a reputation for its skill and tenacity, the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team is an impressive group of athletes whose talent and teamwork are unmatched. At the 2026 Division III State Meet, the squad seized the title, and this victory will be celebrated for many years to come; and

WHEREAS, The members of the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team rose to the occasion and proved themselves to be a dynamic force in high school sports. Truly, team members Katherine Erwin, Riley Meador, Delaney Cahill, and Kaya Asinjo can be proud of the accomplishments that were hard-won and deserved throughout the season; and

WHEREAS, By remaining faithful to the highest standards of sportsmanship and teamwork, the members of the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team primed themselves under the guidance of Coach Mark Stiver to secure this year's state crown. Indeed, the Lumberjacks have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in all of their endeavors; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team on its success and honor the athletes and coaches for their tremendous prowess; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Oakwood High School girls 4x800-meter relay team.

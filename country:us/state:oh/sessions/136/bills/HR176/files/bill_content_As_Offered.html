Representative LaRe


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Jenn Crank on her dog, Bee, being named a 2025 American Kennel Club National Agility Champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Jenn Crank on her dog, Bee, winning the 2025 American Kennel Club National Agility Championship in the regular class sixteen-inch division; and

WHEREAS, The owner of IncrediPAWS, Jenn Crank has striven to train championship dogs, and consequently, her Shetland sheepdog, Bee, won first place at this year’s AKC National Agility Championship in the regular class sixteen-inch division with a time of 29.91 seconds. This outstanding trainer and her student have amply demonstrated their ability to meet challenges with enthusiasm and confidence and have displayed an exceptional willingness to work hard to produce superior results; and

WHEREAS, Jenn Crank’s accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on her but also on her family and on her community. She has exhibited a sincere desire to develop her knowledge of canines so that she might fully showcase a breed’s capabilities, and she is certainly deserving of high praise; and

WHEREAS, Individuals interested in pursuing a career in animal training could find no finer example to emulate than Jenn Crank, for she has followed her goals in a painstaking manner. Indeed, she has proven herself to be innovative and resolute, and her skill, along with Bee’s confidence and spirit, certainly resulted in success at the AKC National Agility Championship; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Jenn Crank for tremendous achievement and extend best wishes to both her and her Shetland sheepdog, Bee, in the years to come; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Jenn Crank.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 327 2025-2026 Senator McColley A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Liberty Center High School cheerleading team on winning the 2026 International Cheer Competition Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Liberty Center High School cheerleading team on securing first place at the 2026 International Cheer Competition in Orlando, Florida; and WHEREAS, An extremely talented group of young athletes, the members of the Liberty Center High School cheerleading team have certainly distinguished themselves as one of the finest squads in the world. Under the capable direction of their coaches, they performed admirably at this year’s International Cheer Competition, earning first place to win the overall title. They are certainly deserving of applause; and WHEREAS, The members of the Liberty Center High School cheerleading team have conscientiously applied themselves to the quest for excellence, and time and again, they have risen to the occasion, maintaining their morale, composure, and good sportsmanship. Clearly, their enthusiastic involvement in competitive cheerleading has enabled them to learn much about dedication, self-discipline, and leadership, and in the process, their exceptional prowess has made their families, friends, and coaches very proud; and WHEREAS, The members of the Liberty Center High School cheerleading team have consistently demonstrated the talent, confidence, and motivation that are the hallmarks of true champions, and they have earned the respect of even their most determined rivals. The maturity and experience which the students have gained through their participation in this sport will undoubtedly assist them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Liberty Center High School cheerleading team for exemplary achievement and salute the members as some of Ohio’s finest young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Liberty Center High School cheerleading team.

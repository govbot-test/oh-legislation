No. 27 2025-2026 Senator Johnson Cosponsors: Senators Romanchuk, Liston, Ingram, Antonio, Blackshear, Brenner, Cirino, Craig, DeMora, Gavarone, Hicks-Hudson, Huffman, Landis, Patton, Reineke, Roegner, Schaffer, Weinstein Representatives Schmidt, Deeter, Barhorst, Brennan, Brent, Brewer, Brownlee, Denson, Grim, Hall, D., Humphrey, Jarrells, Johnson, Lawson-Rowe, Lett, Manning, McNally, Mohamed, Peterson, Piccolantonio, Plummer, Rader, Rogers, Russo, Salvo, Sigrist, Sims, Somani, Synenberg, Thomas, C., Tims, Upchurch, White, A., White, E., Williams A BILL To enact section 5.2540 of the Revised Code to designate March 20th as "Ameloblastoma Awareness Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2540 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2540. The twentieth day of March is designated as "Ameloblastoma Awareness Day."

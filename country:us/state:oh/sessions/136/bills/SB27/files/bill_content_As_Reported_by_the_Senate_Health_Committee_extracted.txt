As Reported by the Senate Health Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 27 2025-2026 Senator Johnson Cosponsors: Senators Romanchuk, Liston, Ingram A BILL To enact section 5.2540 of the Revised Code to designate March 20th as "Ameloblastoma Awareness Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2540 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2540. The twentieth day of March is designated as "Ameloblastoma Awareness Day."

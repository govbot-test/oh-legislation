As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 340 2025-2026 Representative White, E. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Stautzenberger College on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Stautzenberger College on its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this memorable milestone is, indeed, a fitting tribute to Stautzenberger College, for since its inception, it has played a valuable role in preparing countless students for the future. Truly, the institution has made a difference in our society, and we are certain that in the years to come, it will continue to help numerous people become capable and well‑rounded individuals; and WHEREAS, Education is vital to our nation’s successful workforce, and as the need for global learning and understanding increases, so do the demands placed upon our system of education. Over the years, all those associated with Stautzenberger College have consistently and effectively met these challenges, and as they take part in this anniversary celebration, the students, staff, and alumni of this exemplary institution can be justly proud of its accomplishments; and WHEREAS, Those who comprise the administration, faculty, and staff of Stautzenberger College have encouraged a proud journey for their students as they pursue a reasonably priced, alternative education system to meet their occupational goals. Indeed, these fine people have been outstanding examples of personal and professional success and have encouraged their students to grow their talents through the important programming offered at the school; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Stautzenberger College for a century of service and offer best wishes for the years ahead; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Stautzenberger College.

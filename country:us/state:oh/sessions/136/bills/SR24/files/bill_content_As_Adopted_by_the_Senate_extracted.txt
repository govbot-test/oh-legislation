As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 24 2025-2026 Senator DeMora Cosponsors: Senators Antonio, Blackshear, Blessing, Brenner, Chavez, Cirino, Craig, Cutrona, Gavarone, Hicks-Hudson, Huffman, Ingram, Johnson, Koehler, Landis, Lang, Liston, Manchester, Manning, McColley, O'Brien, Patton, Reineke, Reynolds, Roegner, Romanchuk, Schaffer, Smith, Timken, Weinstein, Wilkin, Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring The Ohio State University football team as the 2025 College Football Playoff National Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to The Ohio State University football team on securing the 2025 College Football Playoff National Championship; and WHEREAS, The Ohio State University football team has achieved a tremendous feat in winning the national crown, the culmination of an exemplary season that was marked by becoming the first champions in the history of the twelve-team College Football Playoff format. The Buckeyes’ strong performance, combined with an adept drive to succeed, has led the squad to this outstanding victory, and the group has earned the appreciation and admiration of many; and WHEREAS, The accomplishments and development of The Ohio State University football team are fine examples of what can be attained by a group of hard-working young people supported by a dedicated coaching staff such as the one led by Ryan Day. Throughout extensive conditioning and training, the members have developed into dynamic competitors, and they are deserving of acknowledgement; and WHEREAS, With great admiration, we congratulate the athletes who comprise The Ohio State University football team, for they have overcome many formidable opponents across the regular season and playoff and never failed to display tremendous prowess. Indeed, we are confident that as they meet their future challenges, they will take with them the lessons of comradery, diligence, and discipline; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, heartily applaud The Ohio State University football team as the 2025 College Football Playoff National Champion and praise the strength of this program and its participating athletes; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to The Ohio State University football team.

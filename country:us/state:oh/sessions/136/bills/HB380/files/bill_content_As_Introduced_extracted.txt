Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 380 2025-2026 Representatives McNally, Fischer Cosponsors: Representatives Grim, Synenberg, Brennan, Deeter, Brent To enact section 5.2322 of the Revised Code to designate June as "Migraine and Headache Awareness Month." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2322 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2322. The month of June is designated as "Migraine and Headache Awareness Month."

Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 268 2025-2026 Representative Santucci A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring McDonald High School on the Centennial of its football program. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize McDonald High School on the auspicious occasion of its football program’s One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to McDonald High School’s football program, for throughout the past century, it has established a proud, rich tradition on the gridiron. Indeed, it has accorded countless young people with maturity and experience that have greatly aided them in meeting their future challenges; and WHEREAS, All those associated with McDonald High School’s football program can be justly proud of its success. Known for their competitiveness, the members and coaches of the Blue Devils football teams have compiled more than six hundred wins and three state championships during the history of the program; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of McDonald High School’s football program over the years have learned lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and good sportsmanship that have undoubtedly been of tremendous benefit to them throughout their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate McDonald High School on the One Hundredth Anniversary of its football program and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to McDonald High School.

Section 1:
As Reported by the Senate Armed Services, Veterans Affairs and Public Safety Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 253 2025-2026 Senators Craig, Reynolds Cosponsors: Senators Smith, DeMora, Weinstein, Schaffer, Patton, Johnson A BILL To enact section 5.213 of the Revised Code to designate February 20th as "Black Ohioan Civil War Medal of Honor Recipient Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.213 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.213. The twentieth day of February is designated as "Black Ohioan Civil War Medal of Honor Recipient Day" to honor Black soldiers from Ohio who received the congressional medal of honor for their actions during the American civil war.

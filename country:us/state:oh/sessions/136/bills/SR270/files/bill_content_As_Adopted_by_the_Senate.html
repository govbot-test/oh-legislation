Senators Manning, Gavarone


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Edith Solet on being named the 2026 Ohio Fairs’ Queen.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Edith Solet as the 2026 Ohio Fairs’ Queen; and

WHEREAS, A senior at Elyria Catholic School, Edith Solet represents other young women throughout the state who have established high personal goals and have striven to achieve their potential, and her selection as this year’s Ohio Fairs’ Queen is a fitting tribute to her for the enthusiasm and initiative she has continually displayed. Richly endowed with keen intelligence and a multitude of skills, she was previously crowned the 2025 Erie County Fair Queen, and we are certain she will be a remarkable ambassador for the Great State of Ohio; and

WHEREAS, Known and admired throughout her community for her work with the Erie County Junior Fair and her family’s farm, Edith Solet has truly had a positive impact on her beloved Erie County. Never content to take a passive role in life, she has brought insight and exceptional ability to all of her endeavors, and her amiable style will serve her well in the future; and

WHEREAS, Edith Solet can be justifiably proud of attaining this honor, for her tremendous record of accomplishment serves as an inspiring example for other youth. Her many strengths and distinctive qualities, combined with her engaging personality, will no doubt carry her far beyond this event and be of great benefit to her throughout her lifetime; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Edith Solet as the 2026 Ohio Fairs’ Queen and, in so doing, salute her as one of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Edith Solet.

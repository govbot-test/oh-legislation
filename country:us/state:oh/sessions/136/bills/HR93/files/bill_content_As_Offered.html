Representative Young


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance team as the 2025 OASSA Jazz State Champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance team on winning the Jazz Championship at the 2025 Ohio Association of Secondary School Administrators State Dance Championships; and

WHEREAS, The talented athletes of the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance team are, indeed, to be commended for their remarkable fitness and competitive drive, qualities that have figured prominently in the squad’s impressive performance at this year’s OASSA State Dance Championships, including placing first in the jazz category, second in the pom category, and third in the hip hop category. To their credit, the members could always be counted on to execute even the most demanding routines with agility, precision, exuberance, and grace; and

WHEREAS, Under the expert guidance of their coaches, the members of the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance team have trained tirelessly in order to ensure peak physical and mental conditioning, and their exceptional prowess has earned them many admirers. Participation in this sport has enabled them to learn much about creative expression, discipline, and dedication, and clearly, the experience they have acquired is certain to benefit them in the years to come; and

WHEREAS, Their enthusiastic spirit and love of sports has led all those who comprise the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance team to take part in many activities, and they have served as an inspiration to their peers. Their accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on them but also on their families, their coaches, their school, and their community; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance team for exemplary achievement and offer best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Centerville High School coeds varsity dance team.

Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 323 2025-2026 Representative Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Edith Solet as the 2026 Ohio Fairs’ Queen. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Edith Solet on being named the 2026 Ohio Fairs’ Queen; and WHEREAS, An intelligent, poised, and motivated young woman, Edith Solet has developed her knowledge and skills and has labored diligently in pursuit of her objectives. The 2025 Erie County Fair Queen, she is an exemplary senior at Elyria Catholic School, and without question, she has displayed an exceptional willingness to work hard to attain superior results. Through her unfaltering devotion, she has been selected as this year’s Ohio Fairs’ Queen; and WHEREAS, Edith Solet has derived extra energy from her many activities and accomplishments, such as her participation in various competitions at the Erie County Fair, including showing animals and projects such as cake decorating, leadership skills, health, safety, woodworking, welding, and archery, and her integrity and steadfast determination are truly admirable. Indeed, her amiable style and unquenchable enthusiasm will be of great value to her in the years to come; and WHEREAS, The achievements of Edith Solet are a justifiable source of pride not only for her family and friends but also for the entire Erie County community. She has shown a sincere desire to fully develop her talents so that she might fulfill her goals in the future, and this, coupled with her maturity and initiative, shows her ability to excel in all of her endeavors; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Edith Solet on this special accolade and extend best wishes for continued happiness and fulfillment; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Edith Solet.

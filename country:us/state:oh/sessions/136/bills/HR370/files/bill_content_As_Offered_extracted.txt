As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 370 2025-2026 Representative Stewart A R E S O L U T I O N In memory of Captain Curtis J. Angst. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio offer our sincere condolences to the family and friends of Captain Curtis J. Angst on the sorrowful occasion of his death; and WHEREAS, Although there are no words to ease the sorrow now felt, surely there is solace in the remembrance of having known such an outstanding individual as Curtis Angst. A member of the Ohio Air National Guard, he was serving as a KC-135 pilot with the 166th Air Refueling Squadron, 121st Air Refueling Wing in support of Operation Epic Fury when he perished in the line of duty, and his valiant efforts were truly admirable. His absence will be keenly felt; and WHEREAS, The great traditions of our country’s service men and women are an important chapter of American history, and throughout his service, Curtis Angst always used his talents to the benefit of others. Indeed, the laurels of his life stand as a tribute not only to him but also to those he left behind, and his loved ones can be proud of the sacrifices he made to ensure freedom and justice for all people; and WHEREAS, Although the void his death has created can never be filled, Curtis Angst will be remembered for his generosity, his spontaneous nature, and his ability to uplift those around him. A spirited individual who lit up every room he walked into, he freely shared his love of camping, hiking, and the outdoors with his family and friends, and his selfless nature endeared him to those he loved most, including his wife, Mary, to whom he was eternally devoted. It is certain that the world is a richer place for his having been in it, and he will be sorely missed; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, mourn the loss and salute the memory of Captain Curtis J. Angst; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the family of Captain Curtis J. Angst.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 159 2025-2026 Speaker Huffman, Representative Russo A R E S O L U T I O N Relative to travel allowance. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO: WHEREAS, Section 101.27 of the Revised Code provides that each member receive a travel reimbursement based upon the mileage from and to the member's place of residence, by the most direct highway route of public travel to and from the seat of government; therefore be it RESOLVED, That the Chief Administrative Officer of the House of Representatives is hereby authorized to pay the following member’s travel allowance based upon their round-trip mileage as set opposite their name and district number: Member's Name District Number Round-Trip Mileage Ty Moore 95 194

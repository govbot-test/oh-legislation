As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 165 2025-2026 Representative Lorenz A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Delaware Area Career Center computer animation team on placing first at the 2025 Business Professionals of America National Leadership Conference. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Delaware Area Career Center computer animation team as a 2025 Business Professionals of America National Leadership Conference Champion; and WHEREAS, All those associated with the Delaware Area Career Center computer animation team are deserving of praise for their ability to excel in many pursuits and to meet challenges with enthusiasm and confidence. The members of the team, including Luke Bennett, Antonio Rodriguez, and Natalie Vrancken, devoted countless hours to rigorous training in order to produce a compelling and professional entry, and consequently, they won this year’s 2025 Business Professionals of America National Leadership Conference Championship; and WHEREAS, Competitive spirit and innovative effort have played major roles in the development of our country, and the drive to excel is best developed when youth are presented with trials that permit them to test their skills, knowledge, and strengths against their peers. Indeed, the members of the Delaware Area Career Center computer animation team can take pride in their accomplishments, for out of a field of twenty-five competitors from across the country, they emerged to secure first place; and WHEREAS, With their vitality, intelligence, and positive outlook, the members of the Delaware Area Career Center computer animation team have set an example worthy of emulation. We encourage these young individuals to continue to strive for excellence so that they may assume a meaningful and productive role in our complex society; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Delaware Area Career Center computer animation team on its accomplishment and honor its members and advisors as fine Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Delaware Area Career Center computer animation team.

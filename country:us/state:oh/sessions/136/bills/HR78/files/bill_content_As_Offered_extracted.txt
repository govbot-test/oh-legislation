Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 78 2025-2026 Representative Hoops A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Arianna Kiessling on securing a 2025 Division II State Championship in girls bowling. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Arianna Kiessling as the 2025 Division II State Champion in girls bowling; and WHEREAS, Arianna Kiessling is deserving of this prestigious honor, a tribute to the remarkable athletic gifts and indomitable spirit that she has so amply displayed as a junior member of the Napoleon High School girls bowling team. A tenacious competitor, she decisively secured this year’s 2025 Division II State Championship crown with a total score of 703, and it is fitting that such an achievement be enthusiastically commemorated; and WHEREAS, Arianna Kiessling has conscientiously applied herself to the quest for excellence as an athlete, training and practicing diligently in order to ensure peak conditioning, and her efforts have been an inspiration to her peers and coaches. Indeed, she can always be counted on not only to shine individually but also to lend her support to her fellow team members, and understandably, the talents and skills that she has exhibited have earned her many admirers; and WHEREAS, Time and again, Arianna Kiessling has risen to the occasion, maintaining her morale, composure, and good sportsmanship, and she has gained the respect of even her most determined rivals. Clearly, her enthusiastic involvement in bowling has enabled her to learn much about dedication, self-discipline, and leadership, and in the process, her exceptional prowess has made her family and her friends very proud; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to Arianna Kiessling on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship title and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Arianna Kiessling.

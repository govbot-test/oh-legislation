Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 158 2025-2026 Senator Blackshear A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Logan Wilkins as a 2025 Division I State Champion in track and field. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Logan Wilkins on winning the 110-meter hurdles at the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Meet; and WHEREAS, A student at Fairmont High School, Logan Wilkins has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional athlete. A strong competitor, he has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in track and field, and as a result, he placed first in the 110-meter hurdles at this year’s Division I State Meet; and WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Logan Wilkins has consistently demonstrated the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. His unwavering commitment to excellence has earned him the respect and esteem of many, and he is certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Logan Wilkins’ accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his coaches, his school, and his community. Indeed, the same maturity and experience he has gained through his participation in track and field will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Logan Wilkins on his personal success and salute him as one of Ohio’s finest young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Logan Wilkins.

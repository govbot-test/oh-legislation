As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 231 2025-2026 Representative Pizzulli A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Wheelersburg High School softball team as the 2025 Division V State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Wheelersburg High School softball team on winning the 2025 Division V State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Wheelersburg High School softball team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the Lady Pirates worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in softball, and as a result, they defeated Liberty Union by the score of 10-0 to win this year’s Division V State Championship, their fourth such title in program history, and finished the season with an impressive 28‑0 record; and WHEREAS, The Lady Pirates’ achievements and leadership are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the players, their families, their school, their community, and their coaches. Indeed, these skilled athletes have developed their talents to the fullest and learned the value of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are pleased to pay tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coaches for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Wheelersburg High School softball team on its tremendous season and salute the members and coaches as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Wheelersburg High School softball team.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 298 2025-2026 Representatives Abrams, Isaacsohn, Thomas, C., Brownlee, Odioso, Bryant Bailey, Baker A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Seton High School girls volleyball team on winning the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Seton High School girls volleyball team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Seton High School girls volleyball team has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for the spirited and highly motivated performance of its members. Throughout the season, the athletes worked diligently to improve their skills on the court, and as a result, the Saints accomplished their goals, bringing home this year’s Division I State Championship title; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined team, the Seton High School girls volleyball team has developed into a remarkably efficient group of players whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their coaches and the support of their community; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of players and coaches alike. The members of the Seton High School girls volleyball team have exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Seton High School girls volleyball team on its 2025 Division I State Championship and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Seton High School girls volleyball team.

As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 39 2025-2026 Representative Creech A R E S O L U T I O N Recognizing Ohio Agriculture Week, March 9-15, 2025. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize March 9-15, 2025, as Ohio Agriculture Week; and WHEREAS, Agriculture is the largest industry in the State of Ohio. Encompassing 13 million acres, Ohio’s 76,500 farms, 95 percent of which are family-owned, raise more than 200 types of crops and livestock, providing families with food, fuel, and fiber, and the food and agriculture industry contributes $124 billion to the state economy each year, employing one in eight of our citizens in areas from tending fields to serving in restaurants. In addition, more than 1,000 food processing companies add to the economy; and WHEREAS, In 2011, the Ohio General Assembly designated the second full week in March each year as Ohio Agriculture Week to publicly recognize the vitally important role that agriculture plays both in our state and across the country. This year’s observance is March 9-15, and it is fitting that this event be promoted and celebrated by communities across Ohio; and WHEREAS, At a time of increasing urbanization, when many are forced to rely on the services of a few to survive, farmers have shouldered the responsibility of feeding the world’s population with competence and efficiency. During Ohio Agriculture Week, Ohioans are encouraged to appreciate the year-round efforts of farmers and all those involved in the food and agribusiness industries. It is truly encouraging to note that the future of Ohio agriculture is in such capable hands; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize March 9-15, 2025, as Ohio Agriculture Week; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit duly authenticated copies of this Resolution to the news media of Ohio.

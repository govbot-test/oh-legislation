As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 117 2025-2026 Representative Miller, K. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Harbor Hills Country Club on its One Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Harbor Hills Country Club on the auspicious occasion of its Centennial; and WHEREAS, One hundred years is a distinctive milestone for Harbor Hills Country Club, and this iconic venue and golf course can enthusiastically celebrate a century of social and networking activities. Founded in 1925, it has remained a sought-after destination for its members and visitors, and it has become a fine example for other such endeavors to emulate; and WHEREAS, The State of Ohio continues to grow and prosper because of such establishments as Harbor Hills Country Club, and this exemplary facility has certainly paid homage to its long and distinguished history in Hebron. Indeed, this venture has benefited the surrounding area and its residents by offering beautifully conditioned fairways and greens on a nine-hole semi-private golf course with a private banquet hall; and WHEREAS, The people who support Harbor Hills Country Club can always be relied upon to provide guests with memorable, invaluable experiences, and these exceptional individuals have made a lasting impact on the community. We look with optimism to a future that will thrive due, in part, to their ongoing, esteemed efforts; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Harbor Hills Country Club on its One Hundredth Anniversary and salute its leadership and members as fine Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Harbor Hills Country Club.

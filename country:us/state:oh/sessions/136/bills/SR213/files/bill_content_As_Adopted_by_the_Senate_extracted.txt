Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 213 2025-2026 Senator Blessing A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Miami Fort Power Plant on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Miami Fort Power Plant on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary, celebrated December 11, 2025; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to the Miami Fort Power Plant, for throughout the past one hundred years, this fine facility has attained a remarkable record of service. Beginning operations in 1925, the station is currently operated by the Vistra Corporation, generating numerous megawatts of electricity for a large region; and WHEREAS, The success of the Miami Fort Power Plant is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the venture itself but also on its astute management and hard-working employees. Due to their vision and initiative, this respected enterprise has earned the gratitude and appreciation of many, and it is truly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Over the past century, the Miami Fort Power Plant has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area. We are certain that as this noteworthy establishment maintains its unfaltering dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Miami Fort Power Plant on its One Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Miami Fort Power Plant.

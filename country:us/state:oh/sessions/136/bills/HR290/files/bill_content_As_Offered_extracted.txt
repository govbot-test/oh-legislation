As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 290 2025-2026 Representative Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Avon High School girls volleyball team on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Avon High School girls volleyball team as the 2025 Division II State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Avon High School girls volleyball team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year’s Division II State Tournament, the Eagles won the state crown with a final result of 25-16, 18-25, 25-23, and 25-22, and the members amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional Avon High School girls volleyball team coaching staff, including Coach Kara Coffman, as well as the support of their Eagles community, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Avon High School girls volleyball team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in volleyball; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Avon High School girls volleyball team on its 2025 Division II State Championship title and count its players and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Avon High School girls volleyball team.

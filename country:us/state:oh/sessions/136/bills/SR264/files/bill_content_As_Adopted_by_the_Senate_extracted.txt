Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 264 2025-2026 Senator Roegner A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Cuyahoga Falls Lions Club on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Cuyahoga Falls Lions Club on the commemoration of its One Hundredth Anniversary, February 21, 2026; and WHEREAS, One hundred years of existence is a distinctive milestone for the Cuyahoga Falls Lions Club, and this occasion provides the chapter a unique opportunity to celebrate its numerous philanthropic, civic, and social activities. Founded on October 28, 1925, this iconic club has overcome challenges and persevered to become an invaluable resource to the community, and it has established a long and distinguished history through its thoughtful outreach to Cuyahoga Falls and the surrounding area; and WHEREAS, The State of Ohio continues to prosper thanks to the endeavors of organizations such as the Cuyahoga Falls Lions Club. Among its meaningful initiatives, the group has promoted vision care by sponsoring eyeglasses collection sites and addressing unmet health and education needs worldwide, and it has established a storied legacy of volunteerism; and WHEREAS, The members of the Cuyahoga Falls Lions Club can always be relied upon to favor a worthy cause, and these exceptional people have made a lasting impact on the community through their admirable resolve and vision. We look with optimism to a society that will continue to thrive as a result of the initiative of all those who have comprised the organization, both past and present; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Cuyahoga Falls Lions Club for one hundred years of exemplary presence in the community and salute its leadership and members as fine Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Cuyahoga Falls Lions Club.

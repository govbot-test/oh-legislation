As Passed by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 293 2025-2026 Representatives Rader, Mathews, T. Cosponsors: Representatives Piccolantonio, Brennan, Newman, Williams, Rogers, Russo, Sweeney, Jarrells, Lett, Grim, Somani, McNally, Brewer, Abdullahi, Synenberg, Miller, J., Cockley, Brownlee, Hall, D., White, E., Odioso, Richardson, Lawson-Rowe, Dovilla, Ghanbari, Lampton, Abrams, Barhorst, Brent, Claggett, Click, Daniels, Deeter, Demetriou, Fischer, Fowler Arthur, Glassburn, Hall, T., Hiner, Holmes, Hoops, Isaacsohn, John, Johnson, King, Kishman, Klopfenstein, LaRe, Lear, Lorenz, Manning, Mathews, A., Miller, M., Mohamed, Oelslager, Peterson, Plummer, Ray, Ritter, Robb Blasdel, Salvo, Santucci, Schmidt, Sigrist, Sims, Troy, Upchurch, White, A., Willis, Workman, Young A BILL To enact section 5.282 of the Revised Code to designate the fourth Thursday of January as "NASA Day of Remembrance." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.282 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.282. The fourth Thursday of January is designated as "NASA Day of Remembrance" to pay tribute to and honor members of the national aeronautics and space administration family who have lost their lives while furthering the cause of space exploration and discovery for the benefit of all, including the crews of Apollo 1 and space shuttles Challenger and Columbia.

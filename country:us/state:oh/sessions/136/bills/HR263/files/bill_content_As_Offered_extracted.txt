As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 263 2025-2026 Speaker Huffman, Representatives Isaacsohn, Abdullahi, Abrams, Baker, Barhorst, Bird, Brennan, Brent, Brewer, Brownlee, Bryant Bailey, Callender, Claggett, Click, Cockley, Craig, Creech, Daniels, Dean, Deeter, Demetriou, Dovilla, Ferguson, Fischer, Fowler Arthur, Ghanbari, Glassburn, Grim, Gross, Hall, D., Hall, T., Hiner, Holmes, Hoops, Humphrey, Jarrells, John, Johnson, King, Kishman, Klopfenstein, Lampton, LaRe, Lawson-Rowe, Lear, Lett, Lorenz, Manning, Mathews, A., Mathews, T., McClain, McNally, Miller, J., Miller, K., Miller, M., Mohamed, Moore, Mullins, Newman, Odioso, Oelslager, Peterson, Piccolantonio, Pizzulli, Plummer, Rader, Ray, Richardson, Ritter, Robb Blasdel, Robinson, Roemer, Rogers, Russo, Salvo, Santucci, Schmidt, Sigrist, Sims, Somani, Stephens, Stewart, Swearingen, Sweeney, Synenberg, Teska, Thomas, C., Thomas, D., Thomas, J., Tims, Troy, Upchurch, White, A., White, E., Williams, Willis, Workman, Young A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Wendy Zhan on her retirement as the director of the Ohio Legislative Service Commission. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Wendy Zhan on the occasion of her retirement from the Ohio Legislative Service Commission; and WHEREAS, Throughout her distinguished tenure with the Ohio Legislative Service Commission, Wendy Zhan has upheld the highest standards of integrity and dedication. Commencing her career with LSC as an intern, she joined the Legislative Budget Office as a budget analyst with the education group in 1995 and went on to serve as the division chief of the education group and the director of budget and fiscal analysis before being named the tenth director of the Legislative Service Commission in 2019. In the performance of her duties, she has demonstrated the utmost efficiency and competence, and she has displayed a genuine commitment to meeting the varying and complex needs of the legislature; and WHEREAS, The expertise that Wendy Zhan has shown, time and again, has gained her the esteem of all those with whom she has worked, the respect of legislators on both sides of the aisle, and the gratitude of the public at‑large. She has consistently brought the full measure of her enthusiasm and intelligence to her roles, and she has executed her responsibilities with a degree of precision and discretion that exceeded all expectations; and WHEREAS, In applauding Wendy Zhan for her steady leadership, we note with pride that it is through the exemplary efforts of such dedicated individuals that the Great State of Ohio remains responsive to its citizens. It is our sincere hope that her sterling example of analytical acumen and collaborative spirit will inspire future generations to devote themselves similarly; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend special recognition to Wendy Zhan for her exceptional service to the Ohio Legislative Service Commission and to the State of Ohio and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Wendy Zhan.

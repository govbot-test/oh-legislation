Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 84 2025-2026 Senators Brenner, Craig Cosponsors: Senators Ingram, Weinstein, Lang, Romanchuk, Liston, Blackshear, Wilson A BILL To enact section 5.57 of the Revised Code to designate September 19th as "Stillbirth Prevention Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.57 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.57. The nineteenth of September is designated as "Stillbirth Prevention Day."

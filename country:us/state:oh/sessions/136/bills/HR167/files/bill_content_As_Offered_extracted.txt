Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 167 2025-2026 Representative Lorenz A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring C.J. Sanna on winning a 2025 Division I State Track and Field Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud C.J. Sanna as the 2025 Ohio High School Athletic Association Division I State Track and Field Champion in the boys discus; and WHEREAS, C.J. Sanna’s outstanding ability and spirited drive, as well as his quickness and tenacity, have distinguished him as a superior athlete, and during the season, he has gained the respect of all those who have witnessed him in action. A junior at Olentangy High School, he has proven himself to be one of the finest athletes in Ohio, as evidenced by his first-place throw of 194 feet, 11 inches in the boys discus at this year’s Division I State Track and Field Meet; and WHEREAS, Few sports embody the spirit of individual accomplishment more than track and field, and athletes such as C.J. Sanna exemplify the qualities necessary for excellence in any sport, including discipline, agility, conditioning, and focus. Indeed, his exceptional prowess has won him numerous admirers; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, high school athletic competition has become a valuable tool. Through his participation in sports, C.J. Sanna has learned to meet challenges with enthusiasm and resolve; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize C.J. Sanna on his outstanding achievement at the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Meet and offer best wishes for the years ahead; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to C.J. Sanna.

Section 1:
sr398_00_IN As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 398 2025-2026 Senator Romanchuk A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Madison Henkel as the 2026 Division III State Champion in the long jump. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Madison Henkel on winning first place in the long jump at the 2026 Division III State Track and Field Meet; and WHEREAS, Madison Henkel is deserving of this prestigious title, a tribute to the remarkable athletic gifts and indomitable spirit that she has so amply displayed as a senior member of the Shelby High School girls track and field team and a two-time state champion. A tenacious competitor, she placed first in the long jump with a leap of 19 feet, 11.5 inches at this year's Division III State Track and Field Meet, and not only secured her second consecutive title but also broke the Jesse Owens Memorial Stadium record. Understandably, the strength, agility, stamina, and skill that she has exhibited have earned her many admirers; and WHEREAS, Madison Henkel has meticulously applied herself to the quest for excellence as an athlete, training diligently in preparation for her return to the state championship. Indeed, she has overcome every challenge she has faced, maintaining her morale, composure, and good sportsmanship, and she has gained the esteem of all those who have followed her athletic journey; and WHEREAS, Madison Henkel's enthusiastic involvement in track and field has enabled her to learn much about dedication, self-discipline, and leadership, and in the process, her exceptional prowess has set a fine example that is sure to inspire future generations of athletes. Throughout extensive practice and preparation, she has developed into a dynamic competitor, and it is our hope that she will take the lessons learned from these triumphs and apply them well as she pursues her goals in the years ahead; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute Madison Henkel on her tremendous athletic achievement and commend her on an outstanding season; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Madison Henkel.

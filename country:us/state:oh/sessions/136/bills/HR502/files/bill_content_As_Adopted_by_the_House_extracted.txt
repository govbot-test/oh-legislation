Section 1:
hr502_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 502 2025-2026 Representative Dovilla A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Riley Jones as a 2026 Division I State Track and Field Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Riley Jones on winning the 2026 Division I State Track and Field Championship in the high jump; and WHEREAS, A senior at Berea-Midpark High School, Riley Jones has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, he has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in track and field, and as a result, he won first place in the high jump with a leap of 6 feet, 9 inches at this year's Division I State Meet; and WHEREAS, Riley Jones' accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his coaches, his school, and his community. His unwavering commitment to excellence has earned him the respect and esteem of many, and he is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Riley Jones on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience he has gained through his participation in track and field will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Riley Jones as the 2026 Division I State Track and Field Champion in the high jump and offer best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Riley Jones.

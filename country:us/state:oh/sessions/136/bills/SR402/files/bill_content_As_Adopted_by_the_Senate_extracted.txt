sr402_01_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 402 2025-2026 Senator Blackshear A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Cameron Lindsey on winning the 2026 Division III State Championship in the 3,200-meter run. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Cameron Lindsey as the 2026 Division III State Champion in the 3,200-meter run; and WHEREAS, A member of the Oakwood High School boys track and field team, Cameron Lindsey has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional athlete. With admirable dedication, he has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in his sport, and as a result, he won first place in the 3,200-meter run at this year's Division III State Meet with a time of 9:11.8; and WHEREAS, Cameron Lindsey's accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his coaches, his school, and his community. His unwavering commitment to excellence has earned him the respect and esteem of many, and he is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to Cameron Lindsey on an exemplary season. Indeed, the maturity and experience he has gained as a member of the Oakwood High School boys track and field team will aid him in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize Cameron Lindsey on his Division III State Championship and salute him as one of Ohio's finest young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Cameron Lindsey.

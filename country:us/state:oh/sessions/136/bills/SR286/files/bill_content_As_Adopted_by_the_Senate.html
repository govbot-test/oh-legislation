A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Case Western Reserve University on its Two Hundredth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Case Western Reserve University on the celebration of its Bicentennial, February 7, 2026; and

WHEREAS, Congratulations are certainly in order as Case Western Reserve University marks this landmark anniversary. With an unwavering dedication to educational excellence, the institution has worked diligently to offer students a well-balanced curriculum which seeks to develop their academic and practical skills, and it has earned the gratitude and respect of many satisfied graduates; and

WHEREAS, As Case Western Reserve University continues to forge its path forward, its administration, faculty, staff, students, and alumni can reflect with pride on two hundred years of tradition and prominence. All those associated with this fine institution merit praise for their profound commitment to perpetuate the establishment’s stalwart reputation for excellence; and

WHEREAS, Since 1826, this leading scholastic community has championed ambitious standards and has encouraged the lofty goals of its members, and the remarkable people who currently direct and sustain Case Western Reserve University, not only caring for its physical structure but also initiating and supporting its many edifying works and programs, serve those who attend with vision and vigor. Indeed, they have proven daily that they understand the importance of perpetuating the virtues of the past, even while embracing the ideals of the future; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute Case Western Reserve University on its Two Hundredth Anniversary and look to a future that expands upon its record of exemplary education; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Case Western Reserve University.

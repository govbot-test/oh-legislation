Section 1:
As Passed by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 385 2025-2026 Representative Moore Cosponsors: Representatives Willis, Daniels, Grim, Lorenz, Miller, K., Rogers, Abrams, Barhorst, Bird, Brennan, Brownlee, Claggett, Deeter, Dovilla, Ghanbari, Gross, Hall, D., Hiner, Holmes, Hoops, John, Klopfenstein, LaRe, Lawson-Rowe, Mathews, T., Miller, J., Mohamed, Mullins, Newman, Peterson, Richardson, Ritter, Salvo, Schmidt, Swearingen, Synenberg, Thomas, C., Upchurch, White, A., Williams, Young To enact section 5534.668 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of U.S. Route 22 in Harrison County as the "PFC Thomas "Tommy" Pizzino Memorial Highway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.668 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.668. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the eastbound and westbound lanes of United States route number twenty-two between the eastern border of Harrison county and the intersection of state route number one hundred fifty-one, in Harrison county, shall be known as the "PFC Thomas "Tommy" Pizzino Memorial Highway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 259 2025-2026 Senator DeMora A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring The Ohio State University club football team as the 2025 NCFA Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate The Ohio State University club football team on winning the 2025 National Club Football Association Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of The Ohio State University club football team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the Buckeyes have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in football, and as a result, they defeated a tough Coppin State team by the score of 28-8 to win this year’s NCFA Championship, their second consecutive national crown and fourth such title in six years, and finished the season with a perfect undefeated record; and WHEREAS, The Buckeyes’ achievements and leadership are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the players, their families, their school, their community, and their coach, James Grega. In leading their team to victory, Coach Grega and his staff have not only helped the athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but also instilled in each a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coaches for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend The Ohio State University club football team on its tremendous season and salute the members and coaches as fine citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to The Ohio State University club football team.

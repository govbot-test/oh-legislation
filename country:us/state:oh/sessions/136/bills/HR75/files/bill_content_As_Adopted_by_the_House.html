A R E S O L U T I O N

Recognizing National Vietnam War Veterans Day, March 29, 2025.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize March 29, 2025, as National Vietnam War Veterans Day; and

WHEREAS, The United States was involved in the Vietnam War for a period of twenty years, and during that time, more than 8,744,000 Americans served in the armed forces, including more than 58,000 who were killed, more than 300,000 who were wounded, and approximately 600 who were held as prisoners of war. On March 30, 1973, U.S. combat troops were officially withdrawn from Vietnam, and in 2017, March 29 was designated National Vietnam War Veterans Day to honor of those who fought, died, or are still unaccounted for as a result of the Vietnam War; and

WHEREAS, This year marks fifty-two years after the end of America’s role in the Vietnam War, and the 2025 National Vietnam War Veterans Day underscores the importance of this period of history for all Americans. It is especially important to commemorate the service men and women who valiantly served our nation’s ideals and interests; and

WHEREAS, It is imperative that we openly acknowledge our debt to those who bore our country’s standard with courage, dignity, and decency through their military service. It is, indeed, fitting that National Vietnam War Veterans Day be observed to honor the selfless and courageous members of the U.S. Armed Forces who demonstrated a commendable adherence to the noble principles of democracy and love of country through their outstanding military and community service during the Vietnam War; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize March 29, 2025, as National Vietnam War Veterans Day; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit duly authenticated copies of this Resolution to the news media of Ohio.

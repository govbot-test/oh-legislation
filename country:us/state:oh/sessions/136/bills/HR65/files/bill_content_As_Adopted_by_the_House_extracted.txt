Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 65 2025-2026 Representative Roemer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring John Groce as the 2025 MAC Coach of the Year. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to John Groce on being selected as the 2025 Mid-American Conference Coach of the Year in men’s basketball; and WHEREAS, John Groce is deserving of commendation, for in receiving this richly deserved distinction, he joins the ranks of some of the most illustrious and talented sports figures in the history of collegiate men’s basketball. As head coach of The University of Akron men’s basketball team, he amassed a regular conference 17-1 record, just the second MAC team to accomplish such a feat, and led his squad to win this year’s Mid-American Conference Championship title, and it is fitting that he be recognized with MAC Coach of the Year honors; and WHEREAS, John Groce’s remarkable prowess has earned him the esteem of fans and opponents alike, and we are pleased to recognize such outstanding skill and sportsmanship. To his credit, he has guided the Zips with unwavering expertise, always encouraging his players to push forward in pursuit of victory, and his tremendous talent as a mentor is reflected in their success. He can be proud that the example he has set will long stand as a hallmark worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. With tremendous patience, John Groce has coaxed admirable performances out of his players, and the lessons he has imparted, both on and off the court, will undoubtedly be of great benefit to them throughout their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute John Groce on his recent honor and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to John Groce.

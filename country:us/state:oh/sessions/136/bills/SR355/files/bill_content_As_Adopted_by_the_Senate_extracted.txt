sr355_01_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 355 2025-2026 Senators Gavarone, Hicks-Hudson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Kaliah Ludlow as a 2026 USA Gymnastics Level 5 Ohio Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Kaliah Ludlow on winning the beam event at the 2026 USA Gymnastics Level 5 Ohio Championships; and WHEREAS, A member of Kids World Gymnastics, Kaliah Ludlow has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently to attain ultimate proficiency in gymnastics, and as a result, she won the beam event with a score of 9.45 and placed second in the floor and all-around events at this year's USA Gymnastics Level 5 Ohio Championships; and WHEREAS, Kaliah Ludlow's accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of athletic competition, are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Kaliah Ludlow on an exemplary performance. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in sports will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Kaliah Ludlow on her achievement and offer best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Kaliah Ludlow.

sr349_01_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 349 2025-2026 Senator DeMora A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Keana Hunter on winning a national title at the 2026 U.S. Collegiate Championships in synchronized swimming. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Keana Hunter as a 2026 U.S. Collegiate Champion in the synchronized swimming duet event; and WHEREAS, A member of The Ohio State University artistic swimming team, Keana Hunter is deserving of recognition for her exemplary accomplishments in her sport. At this year's U.S. Collegiate Championship Meet, she won first place in the duet event with her teammate, Ruby Remati, and she helped her team win third place overall. Her noteworthy record of personal achievement stands as a hallmark for others to emulate; and WHEREAS, Through her participation in sports, Keana Hunter has learned important lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and good sportsmanship that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to her throughout her life. Through her outstanding efforts, she has inspired countless people to excel in various areas of endeavor, and she has set an example worthy of the highest praise; and WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. With sports playing such an important part in the development of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to such a fine athlete as Keana Hunter; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Keana Hunter on her remarkable 2025-2026 synchronized swimming season and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Keana Hunter.

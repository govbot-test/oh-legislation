Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 802 2025-2026 Representatives Callender, Miller, K. To enact section 5534.456 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of U.S. Route 20 in Lake County as the "Patrolman Karl E. Bushong Memorial Highway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.456 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.456. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the eastbound and westbound lanes of United States route number twenty between Hubbard road and county line road, within Lake county, shall be known as the "Patrolman Karl E. Bushong Memorial Highway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

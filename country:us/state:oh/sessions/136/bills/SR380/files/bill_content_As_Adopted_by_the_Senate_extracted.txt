Section 1:
sr380_01_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 380 2025-2026 Senator Landis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Bradlee Keehn on winning a 2026 Division IV State Track and Field Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Bradlee Keehn as the 2026 Division IV State Track and Field Champion in the 800-meter run; and WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Bradlee Keehn is deserving of acknowledgement for his exemplary accomplishments as a senior member of the Fairless High School boys track and field team. This year, he secured the Division IV State Championship in the 800-meter run with a time of 1:55.77, and his noteworthy record of athletic achievement stands as a hallmark for others to emulate; and WHEREAS, Bradlee Keehn can always be counted on to shine, and his abilities and sportsmanship have been important factors in his success. A tenacious competitor, he has shown tremendous devotion to this demanding activity, and through his outstanding efforts, he has inspired countless people, including his peers. Indeed, he has set an example worthy of applause; and WHEREAS, The unparalleled commitment to excellence that Bradlee Keehn has displayed in his endeavors has earned him the respect and esteem of many. To his credit, he has demonstrated the skill, integrity, and dedication that are the hallmarks of a true champion, and he has certainly distinguished himself as one of the most talented high school runners in the State of Ohio; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, honor Bradlee Keehn on his state championship and wish him the best in the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Bradlee Keehn.

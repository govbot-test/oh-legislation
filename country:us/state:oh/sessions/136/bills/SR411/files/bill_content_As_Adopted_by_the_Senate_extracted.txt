Section 1:
sr411_01_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 411 2025-2026 Senator Blackshear A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Ethan Ireland as the 2026 Division I State Champion in the boys pole vault. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize Ethan Ireland on winning the 2026 Division I State Championship in the boys pole vault; and WHEREAS, A senior member of the Centerville High School track and field team, Ethan Ireland is deserving of praise for his remarkable athletic gifts and indomitable spirit. At this year's Division I State Meet, he put his talent on display by clearing 16 feet, 4 inches in the pole vault to win the title, and understandably, the strength, stamina, and skill that he has exhibited have earned him many admirers; and WHEREAS, Throughout the season, Ethan Ireland could always be counted on to shine, and by maintaining his morale, composure, and good sportsmanship, he has gained the respect of even his most determined rivals. Indeed, his involvement in sports has enabled him to learn much about dedication, self‑discipline, and leadership, and in the process, his exceptional prowess has made all those who have supported him very proud; and WHEREAS, We are pleased to pay tribute to Ethan Ireland on his stalwart accomplishment. Throughout his high school athletic career, he has prepared conscientiously in pursuit of excellence, and his unwavering commitment has established a notable legacy at Centerville High School; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Ethan Ireland on his state championship in the pole vault and look with optimism to his continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Ethan Ireland.

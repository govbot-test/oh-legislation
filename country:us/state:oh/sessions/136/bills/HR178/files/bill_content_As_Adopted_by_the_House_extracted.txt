Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 178 2025-2026 Representative Craig A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Kaitlyn Carr as a 2025 Division III State Champion in track and field. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Kaitlyn Carr on placing first in the 1,600- and 3,200-meter runs at the 2025 Division III State Track and Field Meet; and WHEREAS, Kaitlyn Carr’s accomplishments are truly noteworthy, for she has clearly demonstrated her talent and perseverance, as well as her mental and physical fortitude. A standout member of the Smithville High School girls track and field team, she has exemplified all of the characteristics which are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, integrity, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship, and at this year’s Division III State Track and Field Meet, she secured first place in the 1,600- and 3,200-meter runs with times of 4:49.44 and 10:48.87, respectively, winning the 1,600-meter title for a second consecutive year and setting a school record in that event; and WHEREAS, By developing such remarkable prowess as a runner, Kaitlyn Carr has earned the appreciation of her fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even her most determined rivals. Her outstanding record of achievement stands as a hallmark worthy of emulation, and she has inspired many individuals to excel in numerous areas of endeavor; and WHEREAS, Running is an event which requires exceptional stamina and natural ability, and Kaitlyn Carr is to be commended for her commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining her athletic goals, she has certainly distinguished herself as a true champion, and her contributions to the Smithville athletic program are sure to be fondly recalled for many years to come; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Kaitlyn Carr on her state championships and salute her as a fine Ohioan; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Kaitlyn Carr.

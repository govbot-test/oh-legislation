Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 342 2025-2026 Senator Romanchuk A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team as the 2026 Division II State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team on winning the 2026 Division II State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, Grantham Trumpower, Owen Walter, Jack Rietschlin, and Colten Montgomery have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in swimming, and as a result, they secured this year’s Division II State Championship crown with a new tournament record time of 1:24.32; and WHEREAS, The Warriors’ achievements and leadership are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the swimmers, their families, their school, their community, and their coach, Brooke Trumpower. In leading her team to victory, Coach Trumpower has not only helped the athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coach for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team on its tremendous season and salute the members and coach as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team.

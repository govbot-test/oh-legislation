Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 154 2025-2026 Representative Miller, M. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Ashland High School boys bowling team on winning the 2025 United States High School Bowling National Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Ashland High School boys bowling team as the 2025 United States High School Bowling National Champion; and WHEREAS, The Ashland High School boys bowling team has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for the spirited and highly motivated performance of its members. Throughout the season, the athletes worked diligently to improve their skills on the lanes, and as a result, the Arrows accomplished their goal of bringing home this year’s United States High School Bowling National Championship, their second consecutive title; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined team, the Ashland Arrows, including Landon Dreibelbis, Max Oeken, Luke Rhine, Mikey McKinney, Brantyn Long, and Adam Reynolds, have developed into a remarkably efficient group of bowlers whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their coach, Jim Brown; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of athletes and coaches alike. The members of the Ashland High School boys bowling team have exemplified the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Ashland High School boys bowling team on its 2025 United States High School Bowling National Championship and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Ashland High School boys bowling team.

As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 42 2025-2026 Senators Antonio, Craig Cosponsors: Senators Blackshear, Ingram, Hicks-Hudson, Liston, Smith, DeMora, Weinstein A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Scott W. Stockman for outstanding service to the State of Ohio. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Scott W. Stockman for nearly ten years of loyal service to the Ohio Senate; and WHEREAS, Scott Stockman is an esteemed individual, for he has combined civic concern and dedication with selfless initiative to become a dynamic member of the Ohio General Assembly staff. During his tenure, he has been a legislative assistant for Senator Thomas Sawyer and the constituents of Ohio’s 28th District and a senior policy advisor, deputy legal counsel, and legal counsel for the Senate Democratic Caucus, performing his duties with the utmost efficiency and competence; and WHEREAS, Deserving of high praise, Scott Stockman has given generously of his time, energy, and abilities to assist the Ohio General Assembly and has demonstrated a noteworthy commitment to the success of our state’s government. Indeed, he has provided a valuable service by helping to maintain the efficiency and effectiveness of the legislative process, and his work ethic has always reflected a genuine interest in benefiting his fellow Ohioans; and WHEREAS, Scott Stockman’s contributions to the State of Ohio have been greatly appreciated by all those who have had the pleasure of working with him. He has displayed exceptional insight, dependability, and integrity in his professional endeavors, and we offer best wishes for health and happiness in his future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Scott W. Stockman on his exemplary state service and salute him as a fine Ohioan; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Scott W. Stockman.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 294 2025-2026 Representative Deeter A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the St. Paul High School cheer team as the 2025 Division III OHSCCA State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the St. Paul High School cheer team on winning the 2025 Division III OHSCCA State Championship in the game day category; and WHEREAS, A standout group of young athletes, the members of the St. Paul High School cheer team have established themselves as truly exceptional competitors by combining talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship. With admirable dedication, they have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in their event, and as a result, they placed first in the Division III Best in the State game day category at this year’s OHSCCA State Championship; and WHEREAS, The achievements and leadership of the St. Paul High School cheer team are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the members, their families, their school community, and their coaches. Indeed, these athletes have developed their great talent and learned valuable lessons of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are pleased to pay tribute to the exemplary cheerleaders and the outstanding coaches of the St. Paul High School cheer team for a job well done. We look forward to seeing what all those associated with the team can accomplish in their bright futures; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the St. Paul High School cheer team on its tremendous season and praise the members and coaches as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the St. Paul High School cheer team.

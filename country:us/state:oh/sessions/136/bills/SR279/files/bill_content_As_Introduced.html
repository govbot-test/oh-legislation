A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Lakewood United Methodist Church on its Sesquicentennial.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Lakewood United Methodist Church on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, Recognition of this significant milestone is a fitting tribute to Lakewood United Methodist Church, for over the past century and a half, it has attained a praiseworthy record of service. The achievements of this fine house of worship are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on the church itself but also on its devoted pastor, the Reverend Robin Dillon, on its members, and on the Lakewood community; and

WHEREAS, The source of important social, religious, and humanitarian projects and programs, Lakewood United Methodist Church has enhanced the quality of life for many since its inception in 1876, and its generous contributions have earned it the gratitude and respect of the countless citizens it has so capably served. We are certain that in the years to come, the church will maintain the tradition of spiritual enlightenment that has long been its hallmark; and

WHEREAS, The members of the Lakewood United Methodist Church have long been actively involved in, and concerned about, area events, and this celebration offers them a unique opportunity to share their memories and to reflect on the benefits of life in their close-knit community. The congregation can, indeed, be proud of their church, for they have built on the best traditions of the past to ensure a peaceful and robust future; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, we commend Lakewood United Methodist Church on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary and extend best wishes for continued growth and prosperity; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Lakewood United Methodist Church.

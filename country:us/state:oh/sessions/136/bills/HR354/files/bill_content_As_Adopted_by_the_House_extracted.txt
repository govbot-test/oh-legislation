Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 354 2025-2026 Representative Callender A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Rotary Club of Painesville on its One Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Rotary Club of Painesville on the commemoration of its Centennial; and WHEREAS, One hundred years is a distinctive milestone on which the Rotary Club of Painesville can enthusiastically celebrate its mission, for through its philanthropic, civic, and social activities, it has enhanced the quality of life in the area. Founded in 1926, this iconic club has overcome challenges and persevered by remaining a relevant resource for the community, and it has paid homage to its long and distinguished history through its thoughtful outreach to the Painesville area; and WHEREAS, One of many reasons that the State of Ohio continues to prosper is because of organizations such as the Rotary Club of Painesville. For a century, this exemplary group has engaged in an array of worthwhile activities and projects that have met a number of local needs, and we are certain that it will continue to positively impact our state and society for generations to come; and WHEREAS, The members of the Rotary Club of Painesville can always be relied upon to favor a worthy cause, and these exceptional people have made a lasting impact on the community through their admirable resolve and vision. We look with optimism to a society that will thrive as a result of the initiatives of all those who have comprised the organization, both past and present; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Rotary Club of Painesville on its One Hundredth Anniversary and salute its leadership and members as fine Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Rotary Club of Painesville.

Representative Mathews, T.


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Kate Simmons on securing the 2025 Girls Wrestling State Championship in the 170-pound weight class.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Kate Simmons as a 2025 OHSAA State Wrestling Champion; and

WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication during the 2024‑2025 season, Kate Simmons has certainly distinguished herself as one of the most talented wrestlers in the state. A senior member of the Findlay High School girls wrestling team, she demonstrated her drive and skill by winning the state title this year, and her outstanding 37-7 record is praiseworthy; and

WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Kate Simmons has consistently exhibited the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. Indeed, she has inspired her teammates, thrilled her fans, gained the respect of her opponents, and made her family, her coaches, and her Trojan community very proud; and

WHEREAS, Participation in wrestling has enabled Kate Simmons to learn much about dedication, self‑discipline, and leadership. Commendably, she has revealed the skill, strength, and stamina that befit a standout athlete, and it is no surprise that she has won so many admirers. The remarkable skill and experience she has shown as a wrestler will be of great benefit as she forges a bright, promising future; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Kate Simmons as the 2025 State Champion in girls wrestling in the 170-pound weight class and look with optimism to her continued success; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Kate Simmons.

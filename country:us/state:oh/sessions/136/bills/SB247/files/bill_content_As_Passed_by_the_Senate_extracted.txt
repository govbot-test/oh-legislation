Section 1:
Am. S. B. No. 247 RS As Passed by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session	Am. S. B. No. 247 2025-2026 Senator DeMora Cosponsors: Senators Timken, Blessing, Blackshear, Smith, Liston, Weinstein, Wilkin, Brenner, Johnson, Antonio, Craig, Gavarone, Hicks-Hudson, Ingram, Manning, Patton, Reynolds, Roegner, Schaffer, Wilson To amend section 5.05 and to enact section 5.083 of the Revised Code to designate the buckeye as the state candy of Ohio, and to correct the Latin name of the Buckeye, the state tree. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.05 be amended and section 5.083 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.05. The tree, Aesculus globra glabra, commonly known as the "Buckeye" is hereby adopted as the official tree of the state. Sec. 5.083. The confection consisting of peanut butter fudge and chocolate, known as the buckeye, is hereby adopted as the official candy of the state. Section 2. That existing section 5.05 of the Revised Code is hereby repealed.

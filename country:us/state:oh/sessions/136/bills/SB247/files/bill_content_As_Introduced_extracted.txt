Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 247 2025-2026 Senator DeMora Cosponsors: Senators Timken, Blessing, Blackshear, Smith, Liston, Weinstein, Wilkin To enact section 5.083 of the Revised Code to designate the buckeye as the state candy of Ohio. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.083 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.083. The confection consisting of peanut butter fudge and chocolate, known as the buckeye, is hereby adopted as the official candy of the state.

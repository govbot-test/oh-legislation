As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 319 2025-2026 Representative Lampton A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Beavercreek High School boys cross country team on securing the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Beavercreek High School boys cross country team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Beavercreek High School boys cross country team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year’s Division I State Meet, the Beavers won the state crown, their first such title in program history, with a score of 107, and the members amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional Beavercreek High School boys cross country team coaching staff, including Head Coach Howard Russ, as well as the support of their Beavers community, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Beavercreek High School boys cross country team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in cross country; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Beavercreek High School boys cross country team on its 2025 Division I State Championship title and count its members and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Beavercreek High School boys cross country team.

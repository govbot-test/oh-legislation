Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 86 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Bloom-Carroll High School FFA ag sales team on placing first at the 2025 Ohio FFA Ag Sales Competition. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Bloom-Carroll High School FFA ag sales team as the 2025 Ohio FFA Ag Sales Champion; and WHEREAS, The members of the Bloom-Carroll High School FFA ag sales team are a dedicated and committed collection of students, and they are richly deserving of acknowledgment for the legacy they are leaving for future FFA teams by winning first place in their event at the 2025 State FFA Ag Sales Competition. Under the leadership of their advisors, members Sadie Moore, Henry Hoisington, Ryan Bender, and Reagan Bender and alternates Jackson Line and Peyton Courtright have displayed a degree of determination and motivation of which they can be proud, and they have set an example of excellence that is worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, FFA combines the wisdom and leadership of adulthood with the enthusiasm and optimism of youth so that our young people will be better prepared to assume meaningful, rewarding roles in society. It is certainly encouraging to know that the future of Ohio agriculture lies, to a large extent, in the capable hands of conscientious individuals such as the members of the Bloom-Carroll High School FFA ag sales team; and WHEREAS, All those associated with the Bloom-Carroll High School FFA ag sales team warrant the highest praise, for they have demonstrated exceptional diligence and enthusiasm in preparing for the rigors of competition, as well as an unwavering dedication to the goals and principles of the National FFA Organization. The maturity and experience these members have gained through their participation in FFA will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of tomorrow, and we are certain that their future holds great promise; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Bloom-Carroll High School FFA ag sales team on its success and salute those involved with the group as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Bloom-Carroll High School FFA ag sales team.

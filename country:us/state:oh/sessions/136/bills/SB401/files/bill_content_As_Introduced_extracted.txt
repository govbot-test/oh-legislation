Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 401 2025-2026 Senator DeMora To enact section 5.63 of the Revised Code to designate February 8th as "General William Tecumseh Sherman Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.63 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.63. The eighth day of February is designated as "General William Tecumseh Sherman Day" in recognition of William Tecumseh Sherman, the civil war general who was born in Lancaster, Ohio on that day in 1820.

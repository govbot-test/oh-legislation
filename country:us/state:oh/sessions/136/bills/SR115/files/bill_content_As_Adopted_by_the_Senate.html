A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Treu House of Munch, Inc., on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Treu House of Munch, Inc., on the memorable occasion of its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, Treu House of Munch is, indeed, deserving of high praise, for throughout fifteen decades of operation, this fine company has attained a noteworthy record of accomplishment. This leading distributor of domestic, imported, and craft beers as well as ready to drink wine mixers and energy drinks has earned the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied clients, and its success is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management, on its hard-working employees, and on the Toledo community; and

WHEREAS, Since its establishment in 1875, Treu House of Munch has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area, and its dedication to the best interests of the customer has been evident in all its transactions. We have no doubt that as this worthy enterprise maintains its dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to grow and prosper; and

WHEREAS, Over the years, the proprietors and managers of Treu House of Munch, including its current owners, Todd Esser and Tracey Perkins, have epitomized America’s entrepreneurial spirit, and it is through the contributions of business leaders such as these that our state has gained a reputation as a friendly, productive, and pleasant place in which to live. We are certain that in years to come, all those associated with this fine venture will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been the hallmark of the business; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Treu House of Munch, Inc., on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Treu House of Munch, Inc.

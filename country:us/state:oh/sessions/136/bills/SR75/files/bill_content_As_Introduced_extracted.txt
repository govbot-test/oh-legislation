Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 75 2025-2026 Senator Landis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Dover High School cheerleading team as a 2025 OASSA Division 3 State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Dover High School cheerleading team on securing the 2025 Ohio Association of Secondary School Administrators Division 3 Game Day Non-Building Large State Championship; and WHEREAS, The Dover High School cheerleading team is made up of tremendous athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year’s OASSA Division 3 State Tournament, the Crimson Tornadoes decisively won the crown in the game day non-building large category, and the members amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals and signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. The members of the Dover High School cheerleading team have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional coaching staff, as well as the support of the Crimson Tornadoes community, they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Dover High School cheerleading team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in cheerleading; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Dover High School cheerleading team on its 2025 OASSA Division 3 State Championship title and count its members and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Dover High School cheerleading team.

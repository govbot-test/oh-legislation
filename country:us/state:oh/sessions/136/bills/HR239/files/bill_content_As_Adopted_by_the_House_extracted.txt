Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 239 2025-2026 Representative Pizzulli A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Beau Hesler on winning a 2025 OHSAA Division III State Track and Field Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Beau Hesler as the 2025 Ohio High School Athletic Association Division III State Champion in the 800-meter run; and WHEREAS, This accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for Beau Hesler has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his mental and physical fortitude as a runner. As this year’s Division III State Champion in the 800-meter run with a personal-best and school-record time of 1:51.78, he has exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, determination, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; and WHEREAS, In displaying such outstanding prowess as a member of the North Adams High School track and field team, as well as the first state champion in the history of the school, Beau Hesler has earned the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. We take this opportunity to acknowledge his exemplary record of achievement; and WHEREAS, Running is an event that requires exceptional stamina and natural ability, and Beau Hesler is to be commended for his commitment to this demanding activity. In striving to attain his athletic goals, he has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity that are the hallmarks of a true champion; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Beau Hesler as the 2025 OHSAA Division III State Champion in the 800-meter run and salute him as a fine Ohioan; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Beau Hesler.

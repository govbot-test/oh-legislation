As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 172 2025-2026 Representative Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Relative to the election of the minority leader, the assistant minority leader, the minority whip, and the assistant minority whip for the 136th General Assembly of Ohio. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO: WHEREAS, C. Allison Russo of House District #7 has resigned as minority leader of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, effective June 23, 2025, thus creating a vacancy in the position of minority leader of the House of Representatives, Dontavius L. Jarrells of House District #1 has resigned as assistant minority leader of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, effective June 23, 2025, thus creating a vacancy in the position of assistant minority leader of the House of Representatives, Dani Isaacsohn of House District #24 has resigned as minority whip of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly, effective June 23, 2025, thus creating a vacancy in the position of minority whip of the House of Representatives; and Michele Grim of House District #43 has resigned as assistant minority whip of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly, effective June 23, 2025, thus creating a vacancy in the position of assistant minority whip of the House of Representatives; and WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly have been advised that the caucus of Democrat members has chosen Dani Isaacsohn of House District #24 as minority leader, Phillip M. Robinson, Jr. of House District #19 as assistant minority leader, Beryl Piccolantonio of House District #4 as minority whip; and Desiree Tims of House District #38 as assistant minority whip; therefore be it RESOLVED, By the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly that Dani Isaacsohn be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of minority leader, effective June 24, 2025; that Phillip M. Robinson, Jr. be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of assistant minority leader, effective June 24, 2025; that Beryl Piccolantonio be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of minority whip, effective June 24, 2025; and that Desiree Tims be, and upon adoption of this resolution is, hereby elected to the office of assistant minority whip, effective June 24, 2025.

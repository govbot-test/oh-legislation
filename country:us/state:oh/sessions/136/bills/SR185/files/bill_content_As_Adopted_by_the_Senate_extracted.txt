Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 185 2025-2026 Senator Chavez A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Barnesville State Theater on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Barnesville State Theater on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to the Barnesville State Theater, for it has become a beloved local landmark and an outstanding symbol of community pride. Established in 1925, the theater gained the appreciation of countless audiences before falling into disuse, and in 2022, a campaign was launched by local residents and volunteers to revive the structure and restore it as a multi-use venue for music, performances, and the advancement of the arts; and WHEREAS, The spirit of our nation is founded upon, and reflected in, its exciting and colorful past, and the memorializing of significant buildings, such as the Barnesville State Theater, is vital to the preservation of America’s unique heritage. Indeed, the One Hundredth Anniversary of this remarkable edifice is an outstanding reflection not only on the structure itself but also on the numerous community members and stakeholders who have contributed to its preservation; and WHEREAS, All those associated with the Barnesville State Theater have distinguished themselves as concerned and responsible citizens. With admirable dedication, they have helped to ensure that the important details of our past are transmitted to future generations for their appreciation, and we salute them for their work to sustain the history of the area; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Barnesville State Theater on its One Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Barnesville State Theater.

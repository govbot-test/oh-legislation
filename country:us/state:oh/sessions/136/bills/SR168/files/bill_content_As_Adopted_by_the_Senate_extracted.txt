Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 168 2025-2026 Senator Landis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Zoey Caldwell as a 2025 Division II State Track and Field Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Zoey Caldwell on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship in the 100-meter hurdles; and WHEREAS, A senior at Cambridge High School, Zoey Caldwell has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in track and field, and as a result, she placed first in the 100-meter hurdles with a time of 14.28 seconds at this year’s Division II State Meet; and WHEREAS, Zoey Caldwell’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to Zoey Caldwell on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in track and field will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Zoey Caldwell as the 2025 Division II State Champion in the 100‑meter hurdles and extend best wishes for continued success as she continues her athletic career at Ohio University; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Zoey Caldwell.

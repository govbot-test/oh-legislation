Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 494 2025-2026 Representatives Piccolantonio, Sigrist Cosponsors: Representatives Brennan, Brownlee, Click, Lett, McNally, Rader, Russo, Salvo, White, A., Williams A BILL To enact section 5.57 of the Revised Code to designate the third Sunday in November as "Road Traffic Victims Day of Remembrance." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.57 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.57. The third Sunday in November is designated as "Road Traffic Victims Day of Remembrance," to honor the people killed or seriously injured on the roads of this state and to encourage road safety and support for road traffic victims and their families.

Representatives Brennan, Hall, D.


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Nevaeh Hall on winning a 2026 Division I State Track and Field Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Nevaeh Hall as the 2026 Division I State Track and Field Champion in the girls long jump; and

WHEREAS, Nevaeh Hall's outstanding ability and spirited drive, as well as her quickness and tenacity, have distinguished her as a superior athlete and an exemplary member of the Valley Forge High School girls track and field team. At this year's Division I State Meet, she placed first in the long jump with a leap of 18 feet, 9 inches, and she has gained the respect and admiration of all those who have witnessed her in action; and

WHEREAS, Few events embody the spirit of individual accomplishment more than the long jump. Athletes such as Nevaeh Hall exemplify the qualities necessary for excellence in any sport, including discipline, conditioning, and focus. Indeed, throughout long hours of practice and competition, she has consistently demonstrated the talent and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true competitor; and

WHEREAS, Through her participation in sports, Nevaeh Hall has learned lessons of perseverance, hard work, and fair and honest competition that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to her throughout her lifetime. Time and again, she has risen to the occasion, maintaining her morale, composure, and good sportsmanship, and she has set a fine example that is sure to inspire future generations; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Nevaeh Hall on her outstanding performance at the Division I State Track and Field Championship and offer best wishes for success; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Nevaeh Hall.

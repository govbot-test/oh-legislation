Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 12 2025-2026 Senator Wilkin A R E S O L U T I O N In memory of Andrew Steven Lansing. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio wish to offer our deepest sympathy and heartfelt condolences to the family and many friends of Andrew Steven Lansing on the sorrowful occasion of his death; and WHEREAS, Andy Lansing left an indelible impression on the people whose lives he touched, and he will long be remembered as a spirited individual who contributed immeasurably to the world around him. A veteran of the U.S. Army, he worked at the Ohio Department of Rehabilitation and Correction for more than twenty-four years, most recently as an officer at Ross Correctional Institute, and he was widely respected for his willingness to serve in a variety of capacities, including as a member of the Special Response Team. Clearly, he demonstrated an unwavering commitment to his profession, and his absence will be keenly felt; and WHEREAS, Giving generously of his energy and abilities, Andy Lansing displayed exceptional concern and insight and was tireless in his work. Indeed, he always used his talents to the benefit of those around him, including his wife, Chun, his two children, Kimberly and Edward, and his many siblings, nieces, and nephews, and although the void his death has created can never be filled, the legacy of care and commitment he established will surely live on; and WHEREAS, Andy Lansing’s concern for improving the quality of life in our society was clearly evident in the contributions he made to his community. Among his endeavors, he was a member of Orient Lodge 321 of the Free and Accepted Masons and the Pike County Shrine Club, and the warmth and understanding that he so readily extended to others will stand as a monument to his personal character; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the memory of a truly honorable man, Andrew Steven Lansing; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the family of Andrew Steven Lansing.

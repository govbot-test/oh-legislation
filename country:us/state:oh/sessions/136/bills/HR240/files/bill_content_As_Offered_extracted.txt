As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 240 2025-2026 Representative Oelslager A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Louisville Rotary Club on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Louisville Rotary Club on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary, celebrated September 27, 2025; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to the Louisville Rotary Club, for it has attained a remarkable record of service to the area. Since its inception in 1925, this association has exemplified the values of volunteerism, service, and civic engagement, and its accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the group itself but also on its dedicated members, both past and present, on the community, and on its parent organization; and WHEREAS, Over the past century, the Louisville Rotary Club has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding area, and through its generous contributions, it has earned the respect and gratitude of generations of people. We are certain that as this noteworthy organization maintains its unfaltering commitment, it will continue in the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; and WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unceasing efforts of entities such as the Louisville Rotary Club that Ohio has gained a reputation as a state that is responsive to the needs of its citizens and has remained a pleasant place in which to live and work. This fine group is truly deserving of high praise; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Louisville Rotary Club on its One Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Louisville Rotary Club.

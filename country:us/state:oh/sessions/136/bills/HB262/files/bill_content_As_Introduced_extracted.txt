Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 262 2025-2026 Representatives Lear, Williams Cosponsors: Representatives Hall, T., Johnson, King, Click, McClain, Gross, Barhorst, Ritter, Thomas, D., Mullins, Hiner, Newman, Claggett, Miller, M., Salvo, Kishman, Mathews, T., Willis, Dean, Ferguson, Fowler Arthur, Klopfenstein, Bird, John, Workman, Fischer To enact section 5.2541 of the Revised Code to designate the weeks between Mother's Day and Father's Day as "Natural Family Month." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2541 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2541. The weeks from mother's day, which is the second Sunday in May, to father's day, which is the third Sunday in June, shall be designated as "Natural Family Month."

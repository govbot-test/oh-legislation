Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 27 2025-2026 Representatives Callender, Troy A BILL To enact section 5534.90 of the Revised Code to designate the bridge spanning the Grand River in Lake County as the "Specialist Jacob A. Ashton Memorial Bridge." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.90 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.90. The bridge spanning the grand river, located in Lake county and being part of the highway known as interstate route number ninety, shall be known as the "Specialist Jacob A. Ashton Memorial Bridge." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers upon the bridge or its approaches indicating its name.

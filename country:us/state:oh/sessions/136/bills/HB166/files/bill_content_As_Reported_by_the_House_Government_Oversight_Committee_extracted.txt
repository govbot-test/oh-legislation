Section 1:
As Reported by the House Government Oversight Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 166 2025-2026 Representatives Lawson-Rowe, Williams Cosponsors: Representatives Brennan, Brent, Piccolantonio, Brownlee, Click, Fowler Arthur, Jarrells, King, Lett, Miller, K., Rader, Sigrist, Sims, Sweeney, Synenberg, Thomas, D., Upchurch, Hall, T., Gross, Lear To enact section 5.2324 of the Revised Code to designate March 10 as "Harriet Tubman Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2324 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2324. The tenth day of March is designated as "Harriet Tubman Day."

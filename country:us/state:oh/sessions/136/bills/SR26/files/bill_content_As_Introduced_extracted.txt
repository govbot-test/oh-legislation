Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 26 2025-2026 Senator DeMora Cosponsors: Senators Antonio, Blackshear, Blessing, Brenner, Chavez, Cirino, Craig, Cutrona, Gavarone, Hicks-Hudson, Huffman, Ingram, Johnson, Koehler, Landis, Lang, Liston, Manchester, Manning, McColley, O'Brien, Patton, Reineke, Reynolds, Roegner, Romanchuk, Schaffer, Smith, Timken, Weinstein, Wilkin, Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Kathleen A. Luikart on her retirement from the Ohio Legislative Service Commission. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to a fine Ohioan, Kathleen A. Luikart, for more than forty years of stalwart service to the Ohio Legislative Service Commission; and WHEREAS, For more than four decades, Kathy Luikart has earnestly served the State of Ohio and the Ohio Legislative Service Commission. Starting her career in public service in 1979 as an LSC intern assigned to the Ohio Senate, she became a fiscal analyst for the Legislative Budget Office upon the completion of her internship in 1980. Shortly after, she moved with her family to Illinois but returned to Ohio in 1983. She rejoined LSC as a research analyst in June of that year, and over the course of her tenure at LSC, she has gained a reputation for unwavering dedication to public service; and WHEREAS, Kathy Luikart has been one of the Ohio Legislative Service Commission’s most capable ambassadors, not only as an expert in the area of utilities and energy law, but also as a teacher or mentor to countless employees in the legislative arena. By bringing her many talents and experiences to bear upon the ever-changing needs of the legislature, she has set an example worthy of emulation. She has gained esteem for her willingness to take on such responsibilities as training new legislative staff, maintaining A Guidebook for Ohio Legislators, and organizing the agency’s biennial New Member Orientation Program, and her knowledge, patience, and flexibility have clearly benefited the Ohio General Assembly and the State of Ohio; and WHEREAS, Kathy Luikart is deserving of tremendous respect for her dependability and engagement, and her professional acumen is a result of her collaboration with Ohio legislators and their offices as well as with her colleagues at the Ohio Legislative Service Commission. Her loyalty to LSC, along with her work ethic, is the measure of a true public servant; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute Kathleen A. Luikart for her meaningful and lasting contributions to the Ohio Legislative Service Commission and extend best wishes for a happy, healthy retirement; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Kathleen A. Luikart.

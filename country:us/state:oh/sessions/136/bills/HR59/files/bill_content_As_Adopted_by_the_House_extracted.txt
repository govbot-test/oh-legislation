Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 59 2025-2026 Representative Russo A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring The Ohio State University pistol team as the 2025 National Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud The Ohio State University pistol team on winning the 2025 Intercollegiate Pistol Championship; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise The Ohio State University pistol team have proven themselves to be talented and superbly conditioned, and they have gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for their spirited and highly motivated performance. Throughout the season, the expert shooters worked diligently to improve their skills, and as a result, the Buckeyes accomplished their goals, bringing home this year’s state championship, their fifth consecutive title and sixth crown since 2018; and WHEREAS, Known for their drive and determination, the Buckeyes have developed into a remarkably efficient group of sharpshooters whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of natural ability. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the esteemed leadership of their coach, Emil Milez; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of athletes and coaches alike. The members of The Ohio State University pistol team have exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, praise The Ohio State University pistol team on its 2025 Intercollegiate Pistol Championship and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to The Ohio State University pistol team.

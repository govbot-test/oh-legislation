Section 1:
As Passed by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 316 2025-2026 Representative LaRe Cosponsors: Representatives Willis, Lorenz, Miller, K., Newman, Rogers, Abrams, Brennan, Brownlee, Click, Cockley, Deeter, Demetriou, Dovilla, Fischer, Ghanbari, Grim, Hall, D., Hiner, Hoops, Humphrey, Jarrells, Lampton, Lawson-Rowe, Mathews, A., Mathews, T., McNally, Miller, J., Richardson, Robb Blasdel, Russo, Schmidt, Sigrist, Stewart, Thomas, C., Upchurch, White, A., White, E., Young To enact section 5534.927 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of Chillicothe Lancaster Road in Fairfield County as the "MSGT Norman Michael Kilbarger Memorial Highway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.927 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.927. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the northeastbound and southwestbound lanes of Chillicothe Lancaster road between Amanda Clearport road southwest and United States route number twenty-two, in Fairfield county, shall be known as the "MSGT Norman Michael Kilbarger Memorial Highway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

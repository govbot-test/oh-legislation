A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Paulding Knights of Columbus on its Centennial.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Paulding Knights of Columbus on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to the Paulding Knights of Columbus, for it has achieved a remarkable record of service to the surrounding area. The many accomplishments of the group are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the organization itself but also on its dedicated members, and it is deserving of high praise; and

WHEREAS, The Paulding Knights of Columbus has enhanced the quality of life in our society, and through its generous contributions, the group has earned the respect of many thankful citizens. We are certain that as this worthy organization maintains its dedication to service, it will continue in the tradition of excellence that has been the hallmark of the Knights of Columbus since its establishment; and

WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unceasing efforts of individuals such as all those associated with the Paulding Knights of Columbus that Ohio has gained a reputation as a state that is responsive to the needs of its citizens and has remained a pleasant place in which to live and work. Indeed, the group’s members have displayed exceptional initiative and compassion, and we applaud them on their commitment to bettering the world around them; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Paulding Knights of Columbus on its One Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Paulding Knights of Columbus.

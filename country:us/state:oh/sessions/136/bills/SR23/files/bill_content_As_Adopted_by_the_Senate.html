A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Urban Meyer on being inducted into the College Football Hall of Fame.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Urban Meyer on being selected as a 2025 inductee into the College Football Hall of Fame in South Bend, Indiana; and

WHEREAS, Urban Meyer is, indeed, deserving of this prestigious honor, which has been accorded to him in recognition of his numerous athletic and coaching accomplishments. Beginning his coaching career as a graduate assistant under Earle Bruce in 1986, he went on to lead Bowling Green University, the University of Utah, and the University of Florida to distinguished seasons before returning to The Ohio State University, finishing his career with an excellent 187-32 record and three national championships; and

WHEREAS, Urban Meyer’s exceptional record of personal and professional achievement stands as a hallmark worthy of emulation, and he has inspired countless individuals to excel in numerous areas of endeavor. As a coach, he has demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in leading his teams to victory, and in so doing, he not only has encouraged his players to develop their talents to the fullest but also instilled in each a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, camaraderie, and school spirit; and

WHEREAS, The respect and admiration which Urban Meyer has earned throughout his lifetime are evident in his induction into the College Football Hall of Fame. Truly, his achievements and leadership are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on him but also on his family, his players, and his community; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Urban Meyer on being inducted into the College Football Hall of Fame and salute him as one of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Urban Meyer.

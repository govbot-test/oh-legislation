Representative LaRe


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team on winning the 2026 Division III State Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team as the 2026 Division III State Champion; and

WHEREAS, The members of the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly exceptional competitors. The unwavering commitment of the players and coaches has earned them the esteem of many, and they are certainly deserving of high praise; and

WHEREAS, Coach Chris Lucas has demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team to victory, and his skill is reflected in the squad's outstanding accomplishments. Due largely to his tireless efforts, the members have distinguished themselves on the diamond, and he has received respect and admiration from all who know him; and

WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team as the 2026 Division III State Champion and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 204 2025-2026 Representative White, A. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Logan Wilkins on winning a 2025 Division I State Track and Field Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Logan Wilkins as the 2025 Division I State Champion in the boys 110-meter hurdles; and WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Logan Wilkins is deserving of recognition for his exemplary accomplishments as a member of the Fairmont High School track and field team. At the 2025 Division I State Meet, he won first place in the boys 110-meter hurdles with a time of 13.88 seconds, and his notable record of personal achievement stands as a hallmark for others; and WHEREAS, Through his participation in sports, Logan Wilkins has learned important lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and good sportsmanship that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to him throughout his life. Through his outstanding efforts, he has inspired many people to excel in various areas of endeavor, and he has set an example worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. With sports contributing to the development of countless youth, we are delighted to congratulate such a fine athlete as Logan Wilkins; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Logan Wilkins on winning a Division I State Championship and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Logan Wilkins.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 181 2025-2026 Senators Brenner, Landis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring The Killbuck Savings Bank on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend The Killbuck Savings Bank on the observance of its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, This auspicious milestone is an appropriate time to recognize The Killbuck Savings Bank for its many significant contributions to Ohio’s banking industry. Since its establishment in 1900, this fine institution has prospered with its reliable financial services for families, small businesses, and organizations and deep commitment to local development, and it now has twelve offices throughout the region; and WHEREAS, Over the years, the management and staff of The Killbuck Savings Bank have consistently demonstrated exemplary professionalism and expertise, as well as an unwavering commitment to maintaining the highest quality standards. The tremendous effort and initiative displayed by the employees have been instrumental in the success of the venture, and the dedication of each person is certainly worthy of acknowledgement; and WHEREAS, Ohio’s economic well-being depends on innovative, forward-thinking business entrepreneurs to provide goods and services to the citizens of our state. We are proud to note that it is through the diligence of individuals such as everyone associated with The Killbuck Savings Bank, who are willing to invest in the future of their community, that Ohio remains a pleasant place in which to live and work; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to The Killbuck Savings Bank on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary and look to its future with great optimism; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to The Killbuck Savings Bank.

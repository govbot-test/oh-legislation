As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 276 2025-2026 Senator Reineke A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team on earning the 2025 National FFA Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team on being named the 2025 National FFA Champion; and WHEREAS, Under the leadership of their advisor, Erin Wollett, the members of the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team became the first from the State of Ohio to win the National FFA Championship. Students Jaxon Ongalibang, Madison Garrabrant, Bristal Cain, Mason Newsome, Victoria Kovacs, Alayna Seavolt, and Ky Barlow worked diligently to achieve success, and being part of the top ranked chapter in the state for novice parliamentary procedure, as well as Jaxon Ongalibang and Victoria Kovacs receiving high honors for their respective roles in the event, is a testament to their commitment to this fine organization; and WHEREAS, Throughout the school year, the members of the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team have shown a sincere desire to fully develop their knowledge and skills so that they might fulfill their goals, and this, coupled with their maturity and initiative, illustrates their potential to become some of tomorrow’s leaders. Indeed, they have set an example of excellence that is worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, Through participation in FFA, the members of the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team will be better prepared to become productive members of our complex and ever-changing society. Their achievements in this program will not only enrich their educational careers but also enable them to make better-informed decisions as they assume the responsibilities of adult citizenship; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team on winning the 2025 National FFA Championship and extend best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Cardington FFA conduct of chapter meetings team.

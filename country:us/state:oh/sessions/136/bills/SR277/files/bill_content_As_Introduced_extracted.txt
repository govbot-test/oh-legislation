Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 277 2025-2026 Senator Hicks-Hudson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Stautzenberger College on its One Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Stautzenberger College on the celebration of its Centennial; and WHEREAS, Congratulations are certainly in order as Stautzenberger College marks this landmark anniversary. With an unwavering commitment to educational excellence, the institution has worked diligently to offer students a well-balanced curriculum which seeks to develop their academic and practical skills in the fields of healthcare, animal sciences, business, and skilled trades, and it has earned the gratitude and respect of many satisfied graduates; and WHEREAS, As Stautzenberger College continues to forge its path forward, its administration, faculty, staff, students, and alumni can reflect with pride on one hundred years of tradition and prominence. Indeed, all those associated with this fine institution merit praise for their profound commitment to perpetuate the establishment’s stalwart reputation for excellence; and WHEREAS, Since 1926, this closely knit scholastic community has championed ambitious standards and has encouraged the lofty goals of its members, and the remarkable people who currently direct and sustain Stautzenberger College, not only caring for its physical structure but also initiating and supporting its many edifying works and programs, serve those who attend with vision and vigor. Indeed, they have proven daily that they understand the importance of perpetuating the virtues of the past, even while embracing the ideals of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute Stautzenberger College on its One Hundredth Anniversary and look to a future that expands upon its record of exemplary education; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Stautzenberger College.

As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 307 2025-2026 Representative Hall, T. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Middletown Rotary Club on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Middletown Rotary Club on the celebration of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, This important milestone provides an ideal time to acknowledge the exceptional work done by the Middletown Rotary Club. Chartered in 1925 by a group of steadfast individuals who were eager to help their community develop and prosper, the organization has achieved a praiseworthy record of service to the area, and its impressive humanitarian endeavors readily explain why it is so widely held in high esteem; and WHEREAS, All those associated with the Middletown Rotary Club are to be commended for their foresight and dedication and for giving of their time, energy, and ability far beyond what was required or expected. These fine people have gained the appreciation and admiration of their community, for they have set an example of concerned and responsible citizenship that is worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, The State of Ohio continues to grow and prosper thanks to the efforts of organizations such as the Middletown Rotary Club. Over the past century, this fine group has engaged in an array of worthwhile projects and events, including participation in goodwill initiatives and establishing local partnerships to benefit the area, and we look with optimism to a future that will thrive as a result of its ongoing success; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Middletown Rotary Club on its One Hundredth Anniversary and salute its leadership and members as fine Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Middletown Rotary Club.

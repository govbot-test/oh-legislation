As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 374 2025-2026 Representative King A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Nelson’s Body Shop on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Nelson’s Body Shop on the memorable occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Nelson’s Body Shop is worthy of acknowledgement, for throughout a century of operation, it has attained a remarkable record of accomplishment. In all of its endeavors, the venture has continually demonstrated its exemplary commitment to the highest industry standards, quality workmanship, and competitive pricing, and this milestone is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on this family-owned business itself but also on its astute management, on its diligent employees, and on the legacy of its founder, Verling V. Nelson; and WHEREAS, Born in 1908, Verling Nelson was a remarkable individual who held a deep passion and interest in the field of auto body work, and in 1926, he launched his own garage, Nelson’s Body Shop. In the years that followed, he gained a fine reputation as an entrepreneur, operating Wayne Sporting Goods Store and Wayne Motor Sales as well as supporting his wife, Esther, in launching Nelson’s Restaurant, and he was always willing to invest in the future of his community. Clearly, he established a tradition of excellence that endures to this day and that remains the hallmark of his family’s business; and WHEREAS, The success of Nelson’s Body Shop can be attributed to the vision and foresight of a host of innovative individuals, and all those who have been involved with its growth over the years are to be applauded on their tremendous efforts. Indeed, they have convincingly demonstrated how very much can be accomplished by a group of conscientious people with clear objectives and firm resolve, and it is through the work of citizens such as these that Ohio remains a pleasant place in which to live; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Nelson’s Body Shop on its One Hundredth Anniversary and offer best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Nelson’s Body Shop.

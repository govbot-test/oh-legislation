A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Jonathan Thomas for outstanding service to the State of Ohio.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Jonathan Thomas for his loyal service to the State of Ohio; and

WHEREAS, Jonathan Thomas is, indeed, a remarkable individual, for he has combined civic concern and dedication with selfless initiative to become a dynamic asset to the Ohio Senate. Commencing his tenure as a sergeant-at-arms in 2016, he also served as a page coordinator from 2020 to 2025, and he has diligently provided an unparalleled wealth of knowledge and experience to numerous grateful and appreciative members of the General Assembly; and

WHEREAS, Throughout his career, Jonathan Thomas has clearly shown the potential of each person to have a positive effect on the quality of life in our society. Always sincere and energetic in his approach to his work, he has distinguished himself as a conscientious and responsible Ohioan, and he is truly deserving of high praise; and

WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unsurpassed efforts and deep commitment of diligent people such as Jonathan Thomas that the State of Ohio continues to grow and prosper. Over the years, he has striven to serve his fellow citizens to the best of his ability, and we applaud him on his fine work; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Jonathan Thomas on his exemplary state service and offer best wishes for health and happiness in the years to come; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Jonathan Thomas.

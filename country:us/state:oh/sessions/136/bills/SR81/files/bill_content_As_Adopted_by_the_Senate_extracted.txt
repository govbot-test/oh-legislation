Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 81 2025-2026 Senator Cutrona A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Austintown Fitch High School competition cheer team as the 2025 OASSA Division 2 Building State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Austintown Fitch High School competition cheer team on winning the Division 2 building category at the 2025 Ohio Association of Secondary School Administrators State Cheer Championships; and WHEREAS, The talented athletes of the Austintown Fitch High School competition cheer team are, indeed, to be commended for their remarkable fitness and competitive drive, qualities that have figured prominently in the squad’s impressive performance at this year’s OASSA State Cheer Championships, including placing first in the Division 2 building category to claim their second consecutive state title. To their credit, the members could always be counted on to execute even the most demanding routines with agility, precision, exuberance, and grace; and WHEREAS, Under the expert guidance of their coaches, Julie Williams, Julie Ciolino, and Hallie Marstellar, Austintown Fitch High School competition cheer team members Amadi Beardsley, Skylin Christopher, Brianna Gay, Kamryn Gizzi, An’Taya Kibble, Danielle Little, Ava Marstellar, Kaylyn Skowron, Mallory Whorten, and Eliana Williams have trained tirelessly in order to ensure peak physical and mental conditioning, and their exceptional prowess has earned them many admirers. Participation in this sport has enabled them to learn much about creative expression, discipline, and dedication, and clearly, the experience they have acquired is certain to benefit them in the years to come; and WHEREAS, Their enthusiastic spirit and love of sports has led all those who comprise the Austintown Fitch High School competition cheer team to take part in many activities, and they have served as an inspiration to their peers. Their accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on them but also on their families, their coaches, their school, and their community; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Austintown Fitch High School competition cheer team for exemplary achievement and extend best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Austintown Fitch High School competition cheer team.

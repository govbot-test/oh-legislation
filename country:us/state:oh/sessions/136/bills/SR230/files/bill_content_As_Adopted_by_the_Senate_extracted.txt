As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 230 2025-2026 Senator Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Avon High School girls volleyball team as the 2025 Division II State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Avon High School girls volleyball team on securing the 2025 Division II State Championship; and WHEREAS, The talented Avon High School girls volleyball team is, indeed, to be heartily commended for decisively earning this year’s Division II State Championship with a 25-16, 18-25, 25‑23, 25-22 win against Anthony Wayne High School. The squad’s performance reflects the outstanding collective effort by a gifted group of athletes, and without exception, the team has devoted countless hours to training and practice in order to ensure peak conditioning. Clearly, this demanding regimen has paid handsome dividends, and the team concluded the season with an impressive 27-1 record; and WHEREAS, The members of the Avon High School girls volleyball team have epitomized the power, poise, and precision that befit true competitors. Despite pressure, they have consistently maintained their composure and morale, exhibiting strength, speed, stamina, and agility in every challenging encounter. Time and again, the Eagles have risen to the occasion, amply demonstrating their unflagging determination, and their impressive maturity and finesse have earned them widespread respect, even among their most formidable rivals; and WHEREAS, By weathering a rigorous schedule and prevailing over a host of adversaries, the members of the Avon High School girls volleyball team have learned much about dedication, self‑discipline, leadership, and perseverance. Their first-place finish is a measure of their stalwart commitment to the sport, and their indomitable spirit has made their families, friends, and coach, Kara Coffman, incredibly proud; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Avon High School girls volleyball team on its brilliant 2025 season and wish its members continued success in all of their future endeavors; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Avon High School girls volleyball team.

Section 1:
As Passed by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 52 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer Cosponsors: Senators Wilson, Weinstein, Craig, Smith, Antonio, Brenner, Cirino, DeMora, Gavarone, Huffman, Ingram, Johnson, Koehler, Landis, Lang, O'Brien, Patton, Reineke, Roegner, Romanchuk, Timken, Wilkin A BILL To enact section 5534.59 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of U.S. Route 33 in Fairfield County and I-70 in Clark County as the "Congressman David Hobson Memorial Highway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.59 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.59. (A) In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the following portions of highway shall be known as the "Congressman David Hobson Memorial Highway": (1) The northbound and southbound lanes of United States route number thirty-three between Columbus-Lancaster road northwest and Logan-Lancaster road, in Fairfield county; (2) The eastbound and westbound lanes of interstate route number seventy between state route number forty-one and United States route number sixty-eight, in Clark county. (B) The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

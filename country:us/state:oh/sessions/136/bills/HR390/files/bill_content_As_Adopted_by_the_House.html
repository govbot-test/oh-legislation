A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Beavercreek High School Air Force JROTC as a 2026 U.S. Air Force Open Drill Nationals Champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Beavercreek High School Air Force Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps on winning the 2026 U.S. Air Force Open Drill Nationals title in the armed and unarmed divisions; and

WHEREAS, The Beavercreek High School Air Force JROTC is worthy of recognition for its outstanding performance in competition. Excelling in both the armed and unarmed competitions, the team won the national title for an unprecedented fourth consecutive year, and it has truly proven to be a credit to the school, the community, and Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps programs across all military service branches; and

WHEREAS, A talented group of individuals, the members of the Beavercreek High School Air Force JROTC have devoted countless hours to rigorous training and study in order to be in peak mental and physical condition. Under the direction of their instructors, who bring a wealth of experience to their posts as retired Air Force commissioned and noncommissioned officers, the students involved with the Beavercreek High School Air Force JROTC have confronted competition with confidence and enthusiasm, and they have distinguished themselves as worthy young Ohioans; and

WHEREAS, An accomplishment such as this demonstrates a strong sense of duty, love of country, and dedication to the ideals of our nation on the part of the members and leaders of the Beavercreek High School Air Force JROTC. It is through the hard work, skills, and sacrifices of Americans like them that the United States remains a proud, strong, and respected nation, and they are truly deserving of high praise; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, praise the Beavercreek High School Air Force Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps on its remarkable success and salute its members as exemplary citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Beavercreek High School Air Force Junior Reserve Officers’ Training Corps.

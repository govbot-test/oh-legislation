Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 386 2025-2026 Representative Stephens A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Budder Manley on securing a 2026 Division III State Wrestling Championship title. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Budder Manley as the 2026 Division III State Wrestling Champion in the 190-pound weight class; and WHEREAS, Budder Manley is deserving of this prestigious title, a tribute to the remarkable athletic gifts and indomitable spirit that he has so amply displayed as a sophomore member of the River Valley High School wrestling team. A tenacious competitor, he could always be counted on to shine, and understandably, the strength, agility, stamina, and skill that he has exhibited have earned him many admirers; and WHEREAS, Budder Manley has conscientiously applied himself to the quest for excellence as an athlete, training and practicing diligently in order to ensure peak conditioning. Time and again, he has risen to the occasion, maintaining his morale, composure, and good sportsmanship, and he has gained the respect of even his most determined rivals. Clearly, his enthusiastic involvement in athletics has enabled him to learn much about dedication, self-discipline, and leadership, and in the process, he has set a fine example that is sure to inspire future generations of wrestlers; and WHEREAS, Wrestling is a demanding sport that requires exceptional strength, stamina, and natural ability, and Budder Manley is to be commended for his exemplary performance at this year’s Division III State Meet, where he defeated a tough opponent from Graham Local 13-3 to secure the crown. Throughout extensive practice and preparation, he has developed into a dynamic competitor, and it is our hope that he will take the lessons learned from this triumph and apply them well as he pursues his goals in the years ahead; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to Budder Manley on his tremendous athletic achievement and honor him on an outstanding season; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Budder Manley.

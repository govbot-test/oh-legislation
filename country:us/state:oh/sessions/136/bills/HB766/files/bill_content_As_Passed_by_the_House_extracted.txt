hb766_02_PH As Passed by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 766 2025-2026 Representatives Lorenz, Salvo Cosponsors: Representatives Barhorst, Williams, Johnson, Abdullahi, Abrams, Brennan, Brent, Brewer, Brownlee, Bryant Bailey, Claggett, Cockley, Daniels, Deeter, Fowler Arthur, Glassburn, Grim, Hall, D., Hiner, John, Lawson-Rowe, Lett, Manning, Mathews, A., Mohamed, Mullins, Peterson, Piccolantonio, Plummer, Richardson, Robb Blasdel, Rogers, Russo, Sigrist, Sims, Somani, Stephens, Synenberg, Thomas, C., Tims, White, A., White, E., Willis, Workman, Young To enact section 5.63 of the Revised Code to designate October as "Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) Awareness Month." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.63 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.63. The month of October is designated as "Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) Awareness Month."

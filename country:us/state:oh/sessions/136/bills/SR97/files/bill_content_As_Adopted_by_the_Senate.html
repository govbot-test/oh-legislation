A R E S O L U T I O N

Observing April 2025 as the Month of the Military Child in Ohio.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Month of the Military Child in Ohio, April 2025; and

WHEREAS, In an effort to increase public knowledge and understanding of the issues surrounding children who have a parent in the military, the month of April was first designated by then U.S. Secretary of Defense Caspar Weinberger as the Month of the Military Child in 1986. The children of U.S. Armed Forces members are deserving of profound admiration for their strength of character; and

WHEREAS, As Americans enjoy the freedom and democracy that service men and women help to perpetuate, we feel the deepest respect for military families in their own right as individuals who have imparted a stalwart sense of patriotic duty and who have accepted the risks of service with dignity and courage. There are those who take for granted our precious rights and privileges, never adequately considering the enormous personal costs so frequently exacted in conjunction with the fulfillment of military obligations. We owe it to military families, especially their children, who may move many times throughout their lives, bear the burden of a parent being in harm’s way, and endure long separations from their parent, to remain keenly aware of how dearly our nation has always paid for its liberty and its ideals; and

WHEREAS, Childhood is a formative time, and family members, together with communities, educators, public officials, and faith-based organizations, play important roles in helping to ensure that children grow up in a safe and stable environment, one that provides nurturing love and encouragement. All Ohioans share the responsibility of supporting our youth, and during the month of April, we renew our commitment to military children, offering understanding and empathy, and urge all citizens to mark this observance by advocating for those who are serving our country and their families; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, acknowledge the Month of the Military Child in Ohio, April 2025, and urge all citizens to participate in appropriate programs and activities associated with it; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit duly authenticated copies of this Resolution to the news media of Ohio.

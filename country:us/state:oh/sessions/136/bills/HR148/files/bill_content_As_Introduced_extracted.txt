Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. R. No. 148 2025-2026 Representatives Brewer, Deeter Cosponsors: Representatives Rader, Piccolantonio, Synenberg, Lawson-Rowe, Upchurch, Grim, McNally, Rogers, Mathews, T., Sweeney, Mohamed, Robinson, Brennan A R E S O L U T I O N To urge Ohioans to safely store and secure firearms. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO: WHEREAS, The Second Amendment to the United States Constitution protects the individual right to keep and bear arms; and WHEREAS, With this right comes the responsibility to ensure that firearms are stored and secured in a manner that prevents unauthorized access and reduces the risk of accidental injury or death; and WHEREAS, Unintentional shootings, particularly those involving children, are a tragic and preventable public health issue; and WHEREAS, Safe storage practices, such as using gun safes, lock boxes, and trigger locks, and storing ammunition separately, can significantly reduce the risk of unintentional shootings and firearm theft; and WHEREAS, Public education and awareness about the importance of safe firearm storage are essential components in promoting responsible gun ownership; now therefore be it RESOLVED, That we urge all Ohioans who own firearms to take appropriate measures to safely store and secure their firearms when not in use, to prevent unauthorized access, and to protect the safety of all citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That we encourage state and local agencies, community organizations, and other stakeholders to promote public awareness and education on the importance of safe firearm storage; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Governor of Ohio, the Director of the Department of Public Safety, the Director of Children and Youth, and the Director of the Department of Health.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 13 2025-2026 Representative Pizzulli Cosponsors: Representatives Schmidt, Click, Fischer, Brennan, Bird, Russo, Klopfenstein, Stephens A BILL To enact section 5.267 of the Revised Code to designate November 5th as "Roy Rogers Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.267 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.267. The fifth day of November is designated as "Roy Rogers Day," in honor of Roy Rogers, also known as "King of the Cowboys," who was a singer and actor from Portsmouth, Ohio.

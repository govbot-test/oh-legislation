As Reported by the Senate Government Oversight and Reform Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 13 2025-2026 Representative Pizzulli Cosponsors: Representatives Schmidt, Click, Fischer, Brennan, Bird, Russo, Klopfenstein, Stephens, Abdullahi, Abrams, Barhorst, Brent, Brewer, Brownlee, Callender, Creech, Deeter, Denson, Ghanbari, Grim, Hall, D., Jones, Lear, Manning, Mathews, A., Miller, J., Newman, Oelslager, Peterson, Plummer, Rader, Ray, Ritter, Robinson, Sigrist, Sims, Synenberg, Thomas, C., Upchurch, White, A., White, E., Williams, Young Senators Brenner, Johnson A BILL To enact section 5.267 of the Revised Code to designate November 5th as "Roy Rogers Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.267 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.267. The fifth day of November is designated as "Roy Rogers Day," in honor of Roy Rogers, also known as "King of the Cowboys," who was a singer and actor from Portsmouth, Ohio.

hr480_00_IN As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 480 2025-2026 Representative White, A. A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Cameron Lindsey on winning a 2026 Division III State Track and Field Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Cameron Lindsey as the 2026 Division III State Track and Field Champion in the 3,200-meter run; and WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Cameron Lindsey is deserving of acknowledgement for his exemplary accomplishments as a senior member of the Oakwood High School track and field team. At the 2026 Division III State Meet, he won first place in the 3,200-meter run with a time of 9:11.8, and his notable record of personal achievement stands as a hallmark for others; and WHEREAS, Through his participation in sports, Cameron Lindsey has learned important lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and good sportsmanship that will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the future. Through his outstanding efforts, he has inspired many people to excel in various areas of endeavor, and he has set an example worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. With sports contributing to the development of countless youth, we are happy to congratulate such a fine athlete as Cameron Lindsey; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Cameron Lindsey on winning a Division III State Championship and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Cameron Lindsey.

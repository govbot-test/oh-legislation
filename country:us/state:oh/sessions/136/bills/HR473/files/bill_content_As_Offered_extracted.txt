hr473_00_IN As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 473 2025-2026 Representative Plummer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Key’Shawn Garrett as the 2026 Division I State Champion in the 100- and 200-meter dashes. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Key'Shawn Garrett on winning first place in the 100- and 200-meter dashes at the 2026 Division I State Track and Field Meet; and WHEREAS, These accomplishments are truly noteworthy, for Key'Shawn Garrett has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his mental and physical fortitude as an athlete. At this year's Division I State Track and Field Meet, he secured first place in the 100-meter dash with a time of 10.49 and the 200‑meter dash with a time of 21.10, helping his team win the meet, and he is deserving of the highest praise for his efforts; and WHEREAS, In displaying such exceptional prowess as a senior member of the Wayne High School boys track and field team, Key'Shawn Garrett has earned the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. Competition in athletics has enabled him to learn much about dedication and self-discipline, and we take this opportunity to acknowledge his exceptional record of achievement; and WHEREAS, Running is an event that requires exceptional stamina and natural ability, and Key'Shawn Garrett is to be commended for his commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining his athletic goals, he has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity that are the hallmarks of a true champion, and he has exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, determination, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Key'Shawn Garrett on his tremendous performance at the 2026 Division I State Track and Field Meet and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Key'Shawn Garrett.

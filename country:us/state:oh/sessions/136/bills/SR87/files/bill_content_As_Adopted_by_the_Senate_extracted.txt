Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 87 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team on securing the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to salute the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team on placing first in the 2025 Division I State Championship tournament; and WHEREAS, Establishing a reputation for its skill and tenacity, the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team is an impressive group of athletes whose talent and teamwork are unmatched. At the 2025 Division I State Championship, the Tigers defeated the team from Princeton High School by a score of 47-44, securing the title, and this victory will be celebrated for many years to come; and WHEREAS, The members of the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team rose to the occasion and proved themselves to be a dynamic force in high school sports. Truly, these outstanding athletes can be proud of the accomplishments that were hard-won and deserved throughout the season; and WHEREAS, By remaining faithful to the highest standards of sportsmanship and teamwork, the members of the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team primed themselves under the guidance of Coach Johnathan Hedgepeth to secure this year’s state crown and reach their full potential. Indeed, the Tigers have shown superb skill, enthusiasm, and commitment in all of their endeavors; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team on its success and honor the athletes and coaches for their tremendous prowess; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Pickerington High School Central girls basketball team.

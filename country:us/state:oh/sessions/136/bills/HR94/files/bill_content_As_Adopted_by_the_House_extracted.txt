Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 94 2025-2026 Representative Young A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Centerville High School junior varsity dance team on winning the 2025 OASSA Jazz State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Centerville High School junior varsity dance team as the 2025 Ohio Association of Secondary School Administrators Jazz State Champion; and WHEREAS, At this year’s Ohio Association of Secondary School Administrators State Dance Championships, the members of the Centerville High School junior varsity dance team rose to the occasion and proved themselves to be a dynamic force in high school sports. In addition to winning first place in the jazz category, they secured third place in the pom and hip hop categories, and truly, these outstanding athletes can be proud of the accomplishments that were hard-won and well-deserved; and WHEREAS, With great admiration, we congratulate the athletes who comprise the Centerville High School junior varsity dance team, for they have overcome many obstacles and displayed tremendous prowess. Indeed, we are confident that as they meet the challenges ahead, they will take with them the lessons of comradery, diligence, and discipline; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in this exciting activity, the members of the Centerville High School junior varsity dance team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Centerville High School junior varsity dance team on its state championship title and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Centerville High School junior varsity dance team.

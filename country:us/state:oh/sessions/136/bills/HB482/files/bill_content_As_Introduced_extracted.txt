Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 482 2025-2026 Representatives Kishman, Oelslager To enact section 5534.972 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of U.S. Route 30 in Stark County as the "Bill Powell Memorial Highway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.972 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.972. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the eastbound and westbound lanes of United States route number thirty between evening star avenue and Neimans avenue, in Stark county, shall be known as the "Bill Powell Memorial Highway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

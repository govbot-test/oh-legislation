Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 393 2025-2026 Representative Hoops A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Gwen Huddle as a 2026 OATCCC Division III State Indoor Track and Field Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Gwen Huddle on winning Ohio Association of Track and Cross Country Coaches Division III State Indoor Track and Field championships in the 1,600- and 3,200-meter runs; and WHEREAS, A student at Napoleon High School, Gwen Huddle has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in distance running, and as a result, she placed first in the 1,600-meter run with a time of 4:53.50 and the 3,200-meter run with a time of 10:47.71 at this year’s OATCCC Division III State Indoor Track And Field Meet; and WHEREAS, Gwen Huddle’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to Gwen Huddle on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in track and field will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Gwen Huddle on her state championships and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Gwen Huddle.

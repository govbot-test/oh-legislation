As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 41 2025-2026 Senator Antonio Cosponsors: Senators Brenner, Cirino, Craig, DeMora, Gavarone, Hicks-Hudson, Johnson, Patton, Reineke, Reynolds, Roegner, Smith, Weinstein, Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Authorizing Cleveland State University to use the Ohio Senate Chamber on March 13, 2025. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF OHIO: WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to have learned that Cleveland State University has requested permission to use the Ohio Senate Chamber for its Columbus Seminar, March 13, 2025; and WHEREAS, Cleveland State University's Columbus Seminar will provide students with the opportunity to better appreciate and understand government and its components by simulating a Senatorial session. Indeed, the knowledge gained through events such as this will be of great value to participants in the years to come; and WHEREAS, By utilizing the facilities that accommodate those involved in the state legislative process, Cleveland State University's Columbus Seminar will offer students an accurate depiction of government and the manner in which legislation is created and processed. This is a prime example of how various state bodies collaborate to help tomorrow's leaders shape a more promising future for themselves and their communities; and WHEREAS, Through participation in Cleveland State University's Columbus Seminar, students will be better prepared to become productive members of our complex and ever-changing society. The maturity and insight participants will gain through their involvement in this program will not only enrich their total educational experience but also enable them to make better-informed decisions as they assume the responsibilities of adult citizenship; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, permit Cleveland State University to use the Ohio Senate Chamber for its Columbus Seminar, contingent upon the availability of the facility, and salute the participants as some of Ohio's finest young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Cleveland State University.

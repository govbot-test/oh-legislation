As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 4 2025-2026 Representative Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Relative to employment and compensation of employees of the House of Representatives preparatory to and during House organization. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO: BE IT RESOLVED, By the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly, that the Chief Administrative Officer of the House of Representatives is hereby authorized to pay all employees actually employed preparatory to House organization of the 136th General Assembly from December 30, 2024 to January 6, 2025, the date of the convening of the 136th General Assembly, at the same salary that was paid them during the 135th General Assembly.

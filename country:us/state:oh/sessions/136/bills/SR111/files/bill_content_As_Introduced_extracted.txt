Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 111 2025-2026 Senator Chavez A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Italian American Cultural Club on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Italian American Cultural Club on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary, celebrated May 18, 2025; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to the Italian American Cultural Club, for since its inception a century ago, it has attained a remarkable record of service to the Steubenville area. Founded in 1925 as the Steubenville Dramatic Club, the organization staged various Italian performances such as plays, dramas, movies, and operas to connect Italian immigrants to their cultural roots while helping them become proud Americans, and it gradually transitioned into its current iteration as a social club that helps citizens of Italian descent celebrate their heritage. The club’s accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the group itself but also on its dedicated members, both past and present, and on the community; and WHEREAS, Over the past century, the Italian American Cultural Club has enhanced the quality of life within Steubenville and the surrounding area, and through its generous contributions, it has earned the respect and gratitude of generations of people. We are certain that as this noteworthy organization maintains its unfaltering commitment to service, it will continue in the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; and WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unceasing efforts of entities such as the Italian American Cultural Club that Ohio has gained a reputation as a state that is responsive to the needs of its citizens and has remained a pleasant place in which to live and work. This fine group is truly deserving of high praise; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Italian American Cultural Club on its One Hundredth Anniversary and extend best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Italian American Cultural Club.

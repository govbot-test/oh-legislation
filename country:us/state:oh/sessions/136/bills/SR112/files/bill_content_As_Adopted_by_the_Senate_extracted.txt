Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 112 2025-2026 Senator Brenner A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Olentangy Orange High School girls wrestling team on winning the 2025 State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Olentangy Orange High School girls wrestling team as the 2025 State Champion; and WHEREAS, The talented Olentangy Orange High School girls wrestling team is, indeed, to be heartily commended for decisively earning this year’s State Championship, the second consecutive state title for the program. The squad’s performances throughout the season reflect the outstanding collective effort by a gifted group of athletes, and without exception, the team has devoted countless hours to training and practice in order to ensure peak conditioning. Clearly, this demanding regimen has paid handsome dividends; and WHEREAS, The members of the Olentangy Orange High School girls wrestling team have epitomized the power, poise, and precision that befit true champions. Despite sometimes grueling pressure, they have consistently maintained their composure and morale, exhibiting strength, speed, stamina, and agility in every challenging encounter. Time and again, they have risen to the occasion, amply demonstrating their unflagging determination, and their impressive maturity and finesse have earned them widespread respect, even among their most formidable rivals; and WHEREAS, By weathering a rigorous schedule and prevailing over a host of adversaries, the members of the Olentangy Orange High School girls wrestling team have learned much about dedication, self-discipline, leadership, and perseverance. Their first-place finish is a measure of their stalwart commitment to the sport, and their indomitable spirit has made their families, friends, and coaches incredibly proud; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Olentangy Orange High School girls wrestling team on its brilliant season and wish its members continued success in all of their future endeavors; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Olentangy Orange High School girls wrestling team.

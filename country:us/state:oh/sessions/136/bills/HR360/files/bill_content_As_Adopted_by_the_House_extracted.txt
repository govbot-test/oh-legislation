Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 360 2025-2026 Representative John A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team on winning the 2026 Division II State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team as the 2026 Division II State Champion; and WHEREAS, Under the capable leadership of Coach Brooke Trumpower, the members of the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team have proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and have gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for their spirited and highly motivated performance. Throughout the season, the swimmers worked diligently to improve their skills in the pool, and as a result, the Warriors accomplished their goals, bringing home this year’s Division II State Championship title with a state tournament record time of 1:24.32; and WHEREAS, Members Owen Walter, Jack Rietschlin, Colten Montgomery, and Grantham Trumpower have developed into a remarkably efficient group of athletes whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork and sportsmanship. Indeed, they have exemplified the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts are worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, The road to victory and a championship title is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, teamwork, and a positive attitude on the part of students and coaches alike. The outstanding athletes of the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team have earned the admiration of all those who have witnessed them in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team on securing the 2026 Division II State Championship and commend everyone associated with this exceptional squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Ontario High School boys 200-yard freestyle relay team.

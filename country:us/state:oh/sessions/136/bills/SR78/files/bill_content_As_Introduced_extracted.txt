Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 78 2025-2026 Senator Romanchuk A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Ashland High School boys bowling team as the 2025 Division I State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Ashland High School boys bowling team on securing the 2025 Division I State Championship; and WHEREAS, The talented Ashland High School boys bowling team is, indeed, to be heartily commended for decisively earning this year’s Division I State Championship, its first-ever state championship crown. The squad’s performance reflects the outstanding collective effort by a gifted group of athletes, and without exception, the team has devoted countless hours to training and practice in order to ensure peak conditioning. Clearly, this demanding regimen has paid handsome dividends; and WHEREAS, The members of the Ashland High School boys bowling team, including Adam Reynolds, Luke Rhine, Max Oeken, Brantyn Long, Mikey McKinney, and Landon Dreibelbis, have epitomized the power, poise, and precision that befit true champions. Despite sometimes grueling pressure, they have consistently maintained their composure and morale, exhibiting strength, stamina, and agility in every challenging encounter. Time and again, they have risen to the occasion, amply demonstrating their unflagging determination, and their impressive maturity and finesse have earned them widespread respect, even among their most formidable rivals; and WHEREAS, By weathering a rigorous schedule and prevailing over a host of adversaries, the members of the Ashland High School boys bowling team have learned much about dedication, self‑discipline, leadership, and perseverance. Their first-place finish is a measure of their stalwart commitment to the sport, and their indomitable spirit has made their families, friends, coach, Jim Brown, and assistant coach, Chris Rhine, incredibly proud; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Ashland High School boys bowling team on its brilliant 2024‑2025 season and wish its members continued success in the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Ashland High School boys bowling team.

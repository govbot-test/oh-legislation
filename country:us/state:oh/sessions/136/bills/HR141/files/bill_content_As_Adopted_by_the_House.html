Representative Synenberg


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Caleb Greenwood on winning a 2025 Division III State Wrestling Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Caleb Greenwood as the 2025 Division III State Wrestling Champion in the 150-pound weight class; and

WHEREAS, This accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for Caleb Greenwood has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his mental and physical fortitude. As this year’s Division III State Champion in the 150-pound weight class, he has exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, determination, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; and

WHEREAS, In displaying such outstanding prowess as a member of the Beachwood High School wrestling team, Caleb Greenwood has earned the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. Competition in athletics has enabled him to learn much about dedication and self-discipline, and we take this opportunity to acknowledge his exemplary record of achievement; and

WHEREAS, Wrestling is a sport that requires exceptional strength, stamina, and natural ability, and Caleb Greenwood is to be commended for his commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining his athletic goals, he has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity that are the hallmarks of a true champion; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Caleb Greenwood on his personal success and salute him as one of Ohio’s finest young citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Caleb Greenwood.

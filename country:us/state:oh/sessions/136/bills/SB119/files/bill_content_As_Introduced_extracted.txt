Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 119 2025-2026 Senator Brenner A BILL To enact section 5534.967 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of U.S. Route 36 in Delaware County as the "Freedom Parkway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.967 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.967. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the eastbound and westbound lanes of United States route number thirty-six between interstate seventy-one and South Galena road, in Delaware county, shall be known as the "Freedom Parkway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

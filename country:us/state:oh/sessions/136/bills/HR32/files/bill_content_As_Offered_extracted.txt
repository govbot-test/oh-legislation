Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 32 2025-2026 Representative Peterson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Amberly Szczerbiak as the 2025 Ohio Fairs’ Queen. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Amberly Szczerbiak on being crowned the 2025 Ohio Fairs’ Queen; and WHEREAS, An intelligent, poised, and motivated young woman, Amberly Szczerbiak has developed her knowledge and skills and has labored diligently in pursuit of her objectives. A student at Miami University, where she is studying strategic communication and biology, she was named the 2024 Fayette County Fair Queen, and she has further distinguished herself through her activity with such organizations as the Fayette County Junior Fair Board, 4-H, and the Miami Trace/Great Oaks FFA Chapter. Without question, she has displayed an exceptional willingness to work hard to attain superior results, and through her unfaltering devotion, she has been selected as this year’s Ohio Fairs’ Queen; and WHEREAS, Amberly Szczerbiak has derived extra energy from her many activities and accomplishments, and her integrity and steadfast determination are truly admirable. Indeed, her amiable style and unquenchable enthusiasm will be of great value to her in the years to come; and WHEREAS, The achievements of Amberly Szczerbiak are a justifiable source of pride not only for her family and friends but also for the entire Faytette County community. She has shown a sincere desire to fully develop her talents, enthusiasm, and joy with others, and these, coupled with her maturity and initiative, show her ability to excel in all of her endeavors; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Amberly Szczerbiak on this special accolade and extend best wishes for continued happiness and fulfillment; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Amberly Szczerbiak.

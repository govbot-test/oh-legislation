A R E S O L U T I O N

Authorizing Ohio TeenPact Leadership School to use the Ohio House Chambers to conduct its graduation ceremony.

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO:

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio have learned that TeenPact Leadership School has requested permission to use the Ohio House Chambers for its graduation ceremony, April 16, 2026; and

WHEREAS, TeenPact Leadership School provides students with the opportunity to better appreciate and understand government and its components; and

WHEREAS, By utilizing the facilities that accommodate those involved in the state legislative process, TeenPact Leadership School will offer students an accurate depiction of state government and the manner in which legislation is created and processed; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, permit TeenPact Leadership School to use the Ohio House Chambers for its graduation ceremony, April 16, 2026, contingent upon the availability of those facilities, and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to TeenPact Leadership School.

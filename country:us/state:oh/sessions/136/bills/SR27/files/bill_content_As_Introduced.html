A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Maggie Purdy for outstanding service to the State of Ohio.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Maggie Purdy for her loyal service to the Ohio Senate; and

WHEREAS, Throughout Maggie Purdy’s distinguished tenure, she has demonstrated an unwavering dedication to performing her duties and fulfilling her responsibilities with the utmost diligence and competence. Starting as a fellow with the Ohio Legislative Service Commission, she went on to serve as a senior legislative aide for Senator Stephanie Kunze before being named the deputy press secretary for the Senate Majority Caucus. In her work, she has been instrumental in maintaining the efficiency and effectiveness of the legislative process and in ensuring that our state continues to uphold the ideals that are embodied in our constitution and in the laws enacted under it; and

WHEREAS, The expertise and professionalism Maggie Purdy has shown in her endeavors have gained her the admiration and esteem of all those with whom she has been associated. As she reflects on her service with the Ohio General Assembly staff, she has the satisfaction of knowing that she has provided a valuable service to the state, and her record of achievement will stand as a hallmark for others to emulate; and

WHEREAS, Maggie Purdy’s contributions to the State of Ohio have been greatly appreciated by everyone who has had the pleasure of working with her. She has displayed exceptional devotion, responsibility, and enthusiasm over the years, and we offer best wishes as she begins a new chapter in her estimable career; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Maggie Purdy for her exemplary public service and applaud her as a fine Ohioan; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Maggie Purdy.

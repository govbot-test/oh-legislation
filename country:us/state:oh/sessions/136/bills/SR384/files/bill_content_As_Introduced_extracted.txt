sr384_00_IN As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 384 2025-2026 Senator Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Oberlin High School girls 4x100-meter relay team as the 2026 Division V State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Oberlin High School girls 4x100-meter relay team on placing first at the 2026 Division V State Track and Field Meet; and WHEREAS, Throughout the season, the members of the Oberlin High School girls 4x100-meter relay team have proven to be talented and superbly conditioned individuals, and their spirited and highly motivated efforts have gained them the respect of fans and opponents alike. At this year's Division V State Track and Field Meet, the Phoenix accomplished their goal and decisively won the 2026 title with an impressive time of 48.76, and they are certainly deserving of commendation; and WHEREAS, Under the leadership of their coaches, Oberlin High School girls 4x100-meter relay team members De'Shyana Freeman, Cayman Noble, Aubrey Abeyta, and Alyssa Webb have developed into remarkably efficient runners whose skill on the track is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork and sportsmanship. They are not only a credit to their families and their school but also representative of the Phoenix's proud athletic tradition; and WHEREAS, The road to victory and a championship title is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of athletes and coaches alike. The members of the Oberlin High School girls 4x100-meter relay team have demonstrated all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, and integrity. Indeed, they have earned the admiration of all those who have witnessed them in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Oberlin High School girls 4x100-meter relay team on winning the 2026 Division V State Track and Field Championship and wish the runners and coaches great success in all their future endeavors; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Oberlin High School girls 4x100-meter relay team.

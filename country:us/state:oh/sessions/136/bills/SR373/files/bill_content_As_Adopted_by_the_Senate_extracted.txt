sr373_01_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 373 2025-2026 Senator Landis A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Wyatt Lendon as a 2026 Division II State Track and Field Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Wyatt Lendon on winning the 2026 Division II State Track and Field Championship in the 1,600-meter run; and WHEREAS, A student at New Philadelphia High School, Wyatt Lendon has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish himself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, he has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in track and field, and as a result, he won first place in the 1,600-meter run with a time of 4:11.56 at this year's Division II State Meet; and WHEREAS, Wyatt Lendon's accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his coaches, his school, and his community. His unwavering commitment to excellence has earned him the respect and esteem of many, and he is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Wyatt Lendon on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience he has gained through his participation in track and field will greatly aid him in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Wyatt Lendon as the 2026 Division II State Track and Field Champion in the 1,600-meter run and offer best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Wyatt Lendon.

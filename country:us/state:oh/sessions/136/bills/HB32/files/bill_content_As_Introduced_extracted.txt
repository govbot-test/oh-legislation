Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 32 2025-2026 Representatives Jarrells, Cockley Cosponsors: Representatives Piccolantonio, McNally, Brownlee, Lett, Rader, Somani, Rogers, Williams, Abdullahi, Mohamed, Brennan, Synenberg, Russo, Hall, D., Grim A BILL To enact section 5.2322 of the Revised Code to designate the month of July as Celebrating Disabilities Month. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2322 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2322. The month of July is designated as "Celebrating Disabilities Month."

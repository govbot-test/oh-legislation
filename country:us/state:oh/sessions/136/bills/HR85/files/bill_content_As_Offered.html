Representative Russo


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Jesse Mendez as the 2025 NCAA Division I Wrestling Champion in the 141-pound weight class.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize Jesse Mendez on securing the 2025 National Collegiate Athletic Association Division I Wrestling Championship in the 141-pound weight class; and

WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication during the 2024-25 season, Jesse Mendez has certainly distinguished himself as one of the most talented wrestlers in the country. After winning a national championship last year, he defeated a tough opponent from the University of Nebraska this year by a score of 12-9 in the 141-pound weight class to earn his second consecutive national title; and

WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Jesse Mendez has consistently demonstrated the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. To his credit, he could always be counted on not only to shine individually but also to contribute significantly to his teammates’ collective effort; and

WHEREAS, A junior at The Ohio State University, Jesse Mendez is truly worthy of acknowledgement for the formidable athletic gifts and the competitive spirit that he has so amply revealed throughout this past season. He has inspired his teammates, thrilled his fans, gained the respect of his opponents, and made his coaches and family very proud; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Jesse Mendez on his impressive wrestling accomplishment and salute him as a fine young athlete; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Jesse Mendez.

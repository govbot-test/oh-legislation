Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 4 2025-2026 Representatives Holmes, Thomas, C. Cosponsors: Representatives Click, Johnson, Fowler Arthur, White, A., Brennan, Klopfenstein, Miller, M., Schmidt A BILL To enact section 5.2471 of the Revised Code to designate September 19 as "Ohio Stillbirth Prevention Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2471 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2471. The nineteenth day of September is designated as "Ohio Stillbirth Prevention Day."

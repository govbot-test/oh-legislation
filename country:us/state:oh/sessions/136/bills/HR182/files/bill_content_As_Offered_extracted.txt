As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 182 2025-2026 Representative Lorenz A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Blake Eichhorn on placing first in his competition at the 2025 SCTP Ohio Championships. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Blake Eichhorn as a 2025 Scholastic Clay Target Program Ohio singles champion; and WHEREAS, A dedicated member of his youth trap team, Blake Eichhorn is deserving of recognition for his exemplary accomplishments in the sport of trap shooting. A diligent and capable individual, he shot a personal best 250 of 250 clay pigeons to win the title for his competition at this year’s SCTP Ohio Championships. Indeed, his noteworthy record of personal achievement stands as a hallmark for others to emulate; and WHEREAS, Through his participation in sports, Blake Eichhorn has learned the important lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and good sportsmanship that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to him throughout his life. Through his outstanding efforts, he has inspired countless individuals to excel in various areas of endeavor, and he has set an example worthy of the highest praise; and WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. With sports playing such an important part in the development of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to such an impressive marksman as Blake Eichhorn; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Blake Eichhorn on his state championship and salute him as a fine Ohioan; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Blake Eichhorn.

Section 1:
(136th General Assembly) (House Bill Number 38) AN ACT To enact section 5534.924 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of State Route 87 in Geauga County as the "Dr. Steven Takacs Veteran Memorial Highway." Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Ohio: Section 1. That section 5534.924 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.924. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the portion of the road known as state route number eighty-seven, running in an easterly and westerly direction, between the intersection of that route and state route number forty-four and the intersection of that route and Sperry road, in Geauga county only, shall be known as the "Dr. Steven Takacs Veteran Memorial Highway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name. Speaker ___________________ of the House of Representatives.

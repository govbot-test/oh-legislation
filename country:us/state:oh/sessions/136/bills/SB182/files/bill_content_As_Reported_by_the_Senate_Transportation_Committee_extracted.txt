Section 1:
As Reported by the Senate Transportation Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 182 2025-2026 Senator Patton To enact section 5534.926 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of State Route 10 in Cleveland as "John E. Gallagher Way." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.926 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.926. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the northeastbound and southwestbound lanes of state route number ten between west forty-seventh street and west fiftieth street, in the municipal corporation of Cleveland in Cuyahoga county, shall be known as the "John E. Gallagher Way." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

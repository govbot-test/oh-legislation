Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 178 2025-2026 Representatives Troy, Williams Cosponsors: Representatives Abdullahi, Brennan, Brent, Brewer, Brownlee, Denson, Grim, Jarrells, Lett, Miller, J., Mohamed, Piccolantonio, Rader, Russo, Sigrist, Sweeney, Synenberg, Upchurch, White, E. A BILL To enact section 5101.546 of the Revised Code to establish a supplemental benefit allotment for certain recipients of supplemental nutrition assistance program benefits and to make an appropriation. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5101.546 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5101.546. (A) The department of job and family services shall provide a supplemental benefit allotment to households participating in the supplemental nutrition assistance program in accordance with this section. (B) To be eligible to receive a supplemental benefit allotment under this section, a participating household shall meet both of the following requirements: (1) Include an individual who is sixty years of age or older; (2) Receive a monthly supplemental nutrition assistance program benefit allotment that is less than fifty dollars. (C) The department shall provide eligible households with a supplemental benefit allotment in an amount that equals the amount necessary to provide the household with a total monthly benefit amount of fifty dollars. Section 2. All items in this act are hereby appropriated as designated out of any moneys in the state treasury to the credit of the designated fund. For all operating appropriations made in this act, those in the first column are for fiscal year 2026 and those in the second column are for fiscal year 2027. The operating appropriations made in this act are in addition to any other operating appropriations made for these fiscal years. Section 3. 1 2 3 4 5 A JFS DEPARTMENT OF JOB AND FAMILY SERVICES B General Revenue Fund C GRF 600564 SNAP State Supplement $12,495,000 $21,415,000 D TOTAL GRF General Revenue Fund $12,495,000 $21,415,000 E TOTAL ALL BUDGET FUND GROUPS $12,495,000 $21,415,000 SNAP STATE SUPPLEMENTAL The foregoing appropriation item 600564, SNAP State Supplement, shall be used to provide a supplemental benefit allotment for eligible households participating in the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program in accordance with section 5101.546 of the Revised Code. Section 4. Within the limits set forth in this act, the Director of Budget and Management shall establish accounts indicating the source and amount of funds for each appropriation made in this act, and shall determine the manner in which appropriation accounts shall be maintained. Expenditures from operating appropriations contained in this act shall be accounted for as though made in, and are subject to all applicable provisions of, the main operating appropriations act of the 136th General Assembly.

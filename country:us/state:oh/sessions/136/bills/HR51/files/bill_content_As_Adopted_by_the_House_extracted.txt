Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 51 2025-2026 Representative Stephens A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Jackson High School competition cheerleading team as the 2025 OAC and OHSCCA State Champions. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to the Jackson High School competition cheerleading team on winning first place in its division at the 2025 Ohio Athletic Conference State Championship and the 2024 Ohio High School Cheerleading Coaches Association competitions; and WHEREAS, The talented athletes of the Jackson High School competition cheerleading team are, indeed, to be commended for their remarkable fitness and competitive drive, qualities that have figured prominently in their impressive performance this season. To their credit, members Abi Baker, Abby Barnett, Addyson Dailey, Alana Stockmeister, Alexus Helman, Bralee Lyons, Breleigh Sturgill, Bryn Denney, Bryn McGhee, Bryn Tripp, Brynlee Smalley, Cannon Stiffler, Daycee Howard, Emily Wiggins, Hannah Pennington, Izzy Swisher, Jada Montgomery, Keirsten Snyder, Kinslee Leach, Lexi Butcher, Madi McElfresh, Mattison Robbins, Mckinlee Lizon, McKinley Riley, Olivia Rogers, Addy Scherer, Olivia Taylor, Preslee Leach, Sophie Williams, and Tayler Williams could always be counted on to execute even the most demanding routines with agility, precision, exuberance, and grace; and WHEREAS, Under the expert guidance of their coaches, Cortney Butcher and Tiffany Boggs, the members of the Jackson High School competition cheerleading team have trained tirelessly in order to ensure peak physical and mental conditioning, and their exceptional prowess has earned them many admirers. Participation in this sport has enabled them to learn much about creative expression, discipline, and dedication, and clearly, the experience they have acquired is certain to benefit them in the years to come; and WHEREAS, Their enthusiastic spirit and love of sports has led all those who comprise the Jackson High School competition cheerleading team to take part in many activities, and they have served as an inspiration to their peers. Their accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on them but also on their families, their coaches, their school, and their community; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Jackson High School competition cheerleading team for exemplary achievement and extend best wishes for ongoing success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Jackson High School competition cheerleading team.

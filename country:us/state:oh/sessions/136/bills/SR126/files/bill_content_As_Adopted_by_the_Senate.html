2025-2026


Senator Manning


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the St. Paul High School cheer team on winning two categories at the 2025 OASSA State Championships.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the St. Paul High School cheer team as the State Champion in the Division 6 building and game day building categories at the 2025 Ohio Association of Secondary School Administrators State Cheer Championships; and

WHEREAS, The St. Paul High School cheer team has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for the spirited and highly motivated performance of its members. Throughout the season, the athletes worked diligently to improve their skills, and as a result, the Flyers accomplished their goals, bringing home this year’s state championship in the Division 6 building and game day building categories; and

WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined team, the St. Paul High School Flyers have developed into a remarkably efficient group of cheerleaders whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their head coach, Katie Weisenberger; and

WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of members and coaches alike. The members of the St. Paul High School cheer team have exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the St. Paul High School cheer team on its 2025 OASSA State Championships and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the St. Paul High School cheer team.

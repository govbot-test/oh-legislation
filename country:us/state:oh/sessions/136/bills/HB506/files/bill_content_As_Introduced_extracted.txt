Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 506 2025-2026 Representative Mohamed Cosponsors: Representatives Brennan, Lett, Sims To enact section 5534.914 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of I-71 in Franklin County as the "George Walker Memorial Highway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.914 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.914. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the northbound and southbound lanes of interstate route number seventy-one between east fifth avenue and Cooke road, in the municipal corporation of Columbus in Franklin county, shall be known as the "George Walker Memorial Highway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

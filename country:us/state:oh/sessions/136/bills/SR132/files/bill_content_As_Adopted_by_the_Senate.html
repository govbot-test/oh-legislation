Senator Liston


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring KyLee Tibbs on winning a 2025 State Wrestling Championship.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud KyLee Tibbs as the 2025 State Wrestling Champion in the 140-pound weight class; and

WHEREAS, This accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for KyLee Tibbs has clearly demonstrated her talent and perseverance, as well as her mental and physical fortitude. As this year’s State Champion in the 140-pound weight class, she holds the special distinction of being the first champion in program history, and she has exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, determination, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; and

WHEREAS, In displaying such outstanding prowess as a member of the Gahanna-Lincoln High School wrestling team, KyLee Tibbs has earned the appreciation of her fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even her most determined rivals. Competition in athletics has enabled her to learn much about dedication and self-discipline, and we take this opportunity to acknowledge her exemplary record of achievement; and

WHEREAS, Wrestling is a sport that requires exceptional strength, stamina, and natural ability, and KyLee Tibbs is to be commended for her commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining her athletic goals, she has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity that are the hallmarks of a true champion; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate KyLee Tibbs on her personal success and salute her as one of Ohio’s finest young citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to KyLee Tibbs.

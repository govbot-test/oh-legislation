Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 218 2025-2026 Representative Oelslager A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Daniela Scheffler on securing first place at the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Daniela Scheffler on her performance at the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Meet; and WHEREAS, A senior at Lake Middle High School, Daniela Scheffler has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional athlete. A strong competitor, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in her events, and as a result, she won first place in the 3,200-meter run with a time of 10 minutes, 21.82 seconds and second place in the 1,600-meter run with a time of 4 minutes, 47.08 seconds at this year’s Division I State Meet; and WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Daniela Scheffler has consistently demonstrated the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Daniela Scheffler’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in track and field will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Daniela Scheffler on her personal success and salute her as one of Ohio’s finest young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Daniela Scheffler.

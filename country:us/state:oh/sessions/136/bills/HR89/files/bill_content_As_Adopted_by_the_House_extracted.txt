Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 89 2025-2026 Representative Barhorst A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Ronnie Thomas as the 2025 Division III State Wrestling Champion in the 190-pound weight class. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Ronnie Thomas on winning the 2025 Division III State Wrestling Championship crown in the 190-pound weight class; and WHEREAS, Ronnie Thomas’ accomplishments are truly noteworthy, for he has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his mental and physical fortitude as an athlete. As a member of the Mechanicsburg High School wrestling team, he has exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, integrity, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; and WHEREAS, By developing such remarkable prowess in wrestling, Ronnie Thomas has earned the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. Indeed, he has decisively earned this year’s Division III State Championship title in the 190-pound weight class, and his outstanding record of achievement is sure to inspire future generations; and WHEREAS, Wrestling is a sport which requires exceptional stamina and natural ability, and Ronnie Thomas is to be commended for his commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining his athletic goals, he has certainly distinguished himself as a true champion, and his accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on him but also on his family, his teammates, his coaches, his school, and his community; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize Ronnie Thomas on his state wrestling championship and honor him as a fine Ohioan; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Ronnie Thomas.

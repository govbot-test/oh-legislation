hr466_01_AH As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 466 2025-2026 Speaker Huffman A R E S O L U T I O N Authorizing Forge Leadership Network to use the Ohio House Chambers to conduct its 2026 model legislature. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO: WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio have learned that Forge Leadership Network has requested permission to use the Ohio House Chambers for the legislative portion of the Forge Leadership Network Summit, July 23, 2026; and WHEREAS, Forge Leadership Network provides students with the opportunity to better appreciate and understand government and its components; and WHEREAS, By utilizing the facilities that accommodate those involved in the state legislative process, Forge Leadership Network will offer students an accurate depiction of state government and the manner in which legislation is created and processed; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, permit Forge Leadership Network to use the Ohio House Chambers for its legislative simulation, contingent upon the availability of those facilities; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Forge Leadership Network.

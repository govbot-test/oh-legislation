Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 139 2025-2026 Senator Ingram Cosponsors: Senators Lang, DeMora, Craig, Smith A BILL To enact section 5.295 of the Revised Code to designate May 13 as "Judge Nathaniel R. Jones Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.295 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.295. The thirteenth day of May is designated as "Judge Nathaniel R. Jones Day" in honor of the attorney, judge, and professor of law who was born on that day in Youngstown, Ohio, in 1928, and who committed his life to the advancement of civil rights.

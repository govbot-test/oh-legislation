Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 35 2025-2026 Representatives Kishman, Robb Blasdel, Oelslager A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Minerva High School girls cross country team as the 2024 Division II State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Minerva High School girls cross country team on winning the 2024 Division II State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Minerva High School girls cross country team have established themselves as truly exceptional competitors by combining talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship. With admirable dedication, Annabelle Crissman, Kayla Crissman, Hannah Ison, Katie Ison, Makenzie Beavers, Gabby Lupshu, and Tateum Richard have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in cross country, and as a result, the Lions won the Division II state crown for the second consecutive year and the third time in four years; and WHEREAS, The achievements and leadership of the Minerva High School girls cross country team are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on these long-distance runners, their families, their school community, and their head coach, Olivia Scott. Indeed, in leading the team to victory, Coach Scott has helped these athletes to develop great talent by instilling the valuable lessons of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are pleased to pay tribute to the exemplary athletes and the outstanding coaches of the Minerva High School girls cross country team for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Minerva High School girls cross country team on its tremendous 2024 season and salute the members and coaches as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Minerva High School girls cross country team.

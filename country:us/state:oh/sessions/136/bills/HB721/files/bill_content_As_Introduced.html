To enact section 122.381 of the Revised Code to establish the Veterans Organization Grant Program and to make an appropriation.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO:

Section 1. That section 122.381 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows:

Sec. 122.381. (A) The veterans organization grant program is created in the department of development. Under the program, the director of development, upon request from an organization eligible to receive money under division (B) of section 307.66 of the Revised Code, may annually provide a one thousand dollar grant to the organization for the purpose of financing celebrations honoring veterans or for providing veterans with basic necessities, including food.

(B) The director of development shall establish the procedures by which eligible organizations may apply for assistance under this section and shall adopt rules for the administration of this section in accordance with Chapter 119. of the Revised Code. The rules shall require that each organization that receives a grant shall, until the funds have been exhausted, annually file a sworn statement with the director of development accounting for the complete expenditures of the funds.

Section 2. All items in this act are hereby appropriated as designated out of any moneys in the state treasury to the credit of the designated fund. For all operating appropriations made in this act, those in the first column are for fiscal year 2026 and those in the second column are for fiscal year 2027. The operating appropriations made in this act are in addition to any other operating appropriations made for these fiscal years.

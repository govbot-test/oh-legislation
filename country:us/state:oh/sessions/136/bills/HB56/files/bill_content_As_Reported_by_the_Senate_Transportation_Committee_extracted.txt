Section 1:
As Reported by the Senate Transportation Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 56 2025-2026 Representative Ray Cosponsors: Representatives Lorenz, Robb Blasdel, Rogers, Abrams, Barhorst, Brennan, Brent, Brownlee, Callender, Click, Cockley, Creech, Deeter, Dovilla, Ghanbari, Grim, Hall, D., Holmes, Hoops, Isaacsohn, Jarrells, Jones, Lawson-Rowe, Manning, Mathews, T., Miller, M., Mohamed, Newman, Piccolantonio, Plummer, Rader, Richardson, Russo, Stephens, Thomas, C., Williams A BILL To enact section 5534.013 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of State Route 421 in Medina County as the "CPL David James Amheiser Memorial Highway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.013 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.013. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, the eastbound and westbound lanes of state route number four hundred twenty-one between United States route forty-two and Lodi road, in Medina county, shall be known as the "CPL David James Amheiser Memorial Highway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

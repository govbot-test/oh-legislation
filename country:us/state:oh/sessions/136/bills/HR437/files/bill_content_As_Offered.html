A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Iron City Hardware on its One Hundredth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Iron City Hardware on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is, indeed, a fitting tribute to Iron City Hardware, for over the past one hundred years, this fine business has attained a remarkable record of service to the area. Since its founding by Herman and Henry Hacker, this family-owned company has continually demonstrated its exemplary commitment to the highest industry standards and competitive pricing, and its dedication to the best interests of its clientele has been evident in its efforts to remain current for generations of customers; and

WHEREAS, The success of Iron City Hardware is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management, including its third-generation-owner, Jim Hacker, and its hard-working employees. Their vision and initiative have earned this respected institution the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied customers, and they are truly deserving of high praise; and

WHEREAS, Over the years, Iron City Hardware has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding community and contributed greatly to the area's economic well-being. We are certain that as this worthy enterprise maintains its dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to prosper and will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Iron City Hardware on its Centennial and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Iron City Hardware.

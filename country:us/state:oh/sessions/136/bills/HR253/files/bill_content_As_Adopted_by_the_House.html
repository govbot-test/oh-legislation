A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Mahle Behr, LLC on the Centennial of its Dayton Plant.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Mahle Behr, LLC, on the auspicious occasion of the One Hundredth Anniversary of its Dayton Plant; and

WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to Mahle Behr, for throughout the past century, this fine business’ Dayton plant has attained a remarkable record of service to the area. One of Dayton’s oldest employers and one of the city’s largest manufacturers, the site previously served such companies as the Maxwell Motor Company and the Chrysler Group, and today, the plant makes thermal components for automotive manufacturers for Mahle Behr; and

WHEREAS, The success of Mahle Behr is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and on its hard-working employees. This respected enterprise has earned the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied customers due to the vision and initiative of its staff, and it is truly deserving of high praise; and

WHEREAS, Over the years, Mahle Behr has enhanced the quality of life within Dayton and the surrounding area. We are certain that as this worthy establishment maintains its unwavering dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to prosper and will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Mahle Behr, LLC, on the One Hundredth Anniversary of its Dayton Plant and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Mahle Behr, LLC.

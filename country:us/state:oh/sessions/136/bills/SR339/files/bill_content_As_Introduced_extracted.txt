Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 339 2025-2026 Senator Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Avon High School winter guard on winning a 2026 OIPA State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Avon High School winter guard on being named the 2026 Scholastic A Class Champion at the 2026 Ohio Indoor Performance Association State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of performers, the members of the Avon High School winter guard have established themselves as truly exceptional competitors by combining talent and spirit with the highest ideals of teamwork. With admirable dedication, the Crimson Tornadoes have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency, and as a result, they placed first at the Ohio Indoor Performance Association State Championship; and WHEREAS, The achievements and leadership of the Avon High School winter guard are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the members of the squad, their families, their school community, and their coach, Laurel Mitchell. Indeed, the members have developed determination, perseverance, and camaraderie that they have displayed proudly in all of their competitive endeavors; and WHEREAS, With competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are pleased to pay tribute to the members of the Avon High School winter guard for a job well done. The maturity and experience that these exemplary young Ohioans have gained will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Avon High School winter guard as a 2026 OIPA State Champion and honor its members and coach on this outstanding accomplishment; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Avon High School winter guard.

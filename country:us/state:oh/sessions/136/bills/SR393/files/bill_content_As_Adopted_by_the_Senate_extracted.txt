Section 1:
sr393_01_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 393 2025-2026 Senator Manchester A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Hazel Francis as a 2026 Division V State Track and Field Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Hazel Francis on winning the 2026 Division V State Championship in the high jump; and WHEREAS, A junior at Russia High School, Hazel Francis has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional young athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in track and field, and as a result, she placed first in the high jump with a leap of 5 feet, 6 inches at this year's Division V State Meet; and WHEREAS, Hazel Francis's accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Hazel Francis on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in track and field will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Hazel Francis as the 2026 Division V State Track and Field Champion in the high jump and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Hazel Francis.

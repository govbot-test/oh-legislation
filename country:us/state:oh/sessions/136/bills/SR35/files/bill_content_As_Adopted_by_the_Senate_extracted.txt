Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 35 2025-2026 Senator McColley Cosponsors: Senators Blessing, Brenner, Chavez, Cirino, Cutrona, Gavarone, Huffman, Johnson, Koehler, Landis, Lang, Manchester, Manning, O'Brien, Patton, Reineke, Reynolds, Roegner, Romanchuk, Schaffer, Timken, Wilkin, Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring David Reedy for outstanding service to the State of Ohio. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to David Reedy for his loyal service to the Ohio Senate; and WHEREAS, Throughout David Reedy’s distinguished tenure, he has demonstrated an unwavering dedication to performing his duties and fulfilling his responsibilities with the utmost diligence and competence. Starting as a legislative assistant for Senator Bill Coley in 2011, he went on to serve as a policy advisor and senior policy advisor before being named a deputy finance director in 2017. In his work, he has been instrumental in maintaining the efficiency and effectiveness of the legislative process and in ensuring that our state continues to uphold the ideals that are embodied in our constitution and in the laws enacted under it; and WHEREAS, The expertise and professionalism David Reedy has shown in his endeavors have gained him the admiration and esteem of all those with whom he has been associated. As he reflects on his service with the Ohio General Assembly staff, he has the satisfaction of knowing that he has provided a valuable service to the state, and his record of achievement will stand as a hallmark for others to emulate; and WHEREAS, David Reedy’s contributions to the State of Ohio have been greatly appreciated by everyone who has had the pleasure of working with him. He has displayed exceptional devotion, responsibility, and enthusiasm over the years, and we offer best wishes as he begins a new chapter in his estimable career; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend David Reedy for his exemplary public service and applaud him as a fine Ohioan; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to David Reedy.

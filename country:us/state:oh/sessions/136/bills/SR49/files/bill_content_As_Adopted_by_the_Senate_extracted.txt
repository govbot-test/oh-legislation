Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 49 2025-2026 Senator Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Julia Shafer on winning three 2025 Division II State Championship titles in swimming. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Julia Shafer as a 2025 Division II State Swimming Champion; and WHEREAS, A remarkable athlete, Julia Shafer has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a standout member of the Cincinnati Hills Christian Academy girls swim team. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in her sport, and as a result, she won first place in the 100-yard freestyle with a time of 50.77 and the 200-yard individual medley with a time of 2:01.71 and helped the 400-yard freestyle relay team win first place with a time of 3:28.38 and the 200-yard medley relay team in securing second place with a time of 1:45.95 at this year’s Division II State Championship meet; and WHEREAS, Julia Shafer is a truly exceptional swimmer, and her accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and a fine reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coach, her school, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly worthy of commendation; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are incredibly proud to applaud Julia Shafer on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in swimming will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Julia Shafer on her personal success and salute her as one of Ohio’s finest young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Julia Shafer.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 99 2025-2026 Senator Manchester A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Graham High School wrestling team on securing the 2025 Division III State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Graham High School wrestling team as the 2025 Division III State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Graham High School wrestling team is made up of remarkable athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year’s Division III State Meet, the Falcons won the state crown, the twenty-fourth consecutive title and twenty‑sixth overall title in program history, and the members amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional Graham High School wrestling team coaching staff, including coaches Jason Knott and Jack Peura, as well as the support of their Falcons community, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Graham High School wrestling team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in wrestling; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Graham High School wrestling team on its 2025 Division III State Championship title and count its members and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Graham High School wrestling team.

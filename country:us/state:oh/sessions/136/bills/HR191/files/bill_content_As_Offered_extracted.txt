Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 191 2025-2026 Representative King A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Coldwater High School girls track and field team on winning the 2025 Division III State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Coldwater High School girls track and field team as the 2025 Division III State Champion; and WHEREAS, The members of the Coldwater High School girls track and field team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly exceptional competitors, earning the esteem of many. At this year’s Division III State Championship meet, the Cavaliers finished first in four events and compiled a total of 65.5 points to win the overall title, and they are certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Head Coach Mark Bruns and his assistant coaches, Marty Schoenherr, Carlee Goodwin, and Ava Giere, have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the Coldwater High School girls track and field team to victory, and their skill is reflected in the squad’s outstanding accomplishments. Indeed, they have received respect and admiration from all who know them; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Coldwater High School girls track and field team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Coldwater High School girls track and field team as the 2025 Division III State Champion and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Coldwater High School girls track and field team.

Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 161 2025-2026 Representative Hiner A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Village of Millersburg on its Bicentennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Village of Millersburg on the jubilant occasion of its Two Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, This is, indeed, an auspicious milestone, and celebration is certainly in order. Since its inception two hundred years ago, the Village of Millersburg has experienced dramatic growth and change, and yet, commendably, even as its residents have exuberantly embraced the promise of a bright future, they have never relinquished their appreciation for the worthiest virtues of the past. Without sacrificing their sense of tradition in the name of progress, the people of Millersburg have managed to nurture a fresh perspective and a positive outlook that endear this distinctive place to all who know it well; and WHEREAS, Two centuries after it was founded, the Village of Millersburg still radiates vitality, optimism, and a charm all its own. Clearly, it is a place of many engaging qualities, and its schools, businesses, and civic organizations both embody and thrive upon the values of which our flag is an emblem. A vigorous commitment to public service and an earnest devotion to lofty ideals have long been second nature to many of Millersburg’s residents, and vigilance, industriousness, and generosity have come to typify their collective character; and WHEREAS, Every state depends upon such vibrant, durable communities as Millersburg for its own survival and stability. Although it has known adversity and tribulation, the village also has been favored with its share of blessings. In the years to come, Millersburg is sure to remain on its admirable course and to continue to transform challenges into opportunities; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend our congratulations to the Village of Millersburg on the observance of its Bicentennial and offer our best wishes for its perennial good fortune; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Village of Millersburg.

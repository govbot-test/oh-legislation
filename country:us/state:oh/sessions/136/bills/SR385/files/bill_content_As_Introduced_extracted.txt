sr385_00_IN As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 385 2025-2026 Senator Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Oberlin High School girls 4x200-meter relay team on placing first at the 2026 Division V State Track and Field Tournament. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Oberlin High School girls 4x200-meter relay team on securing the 2026 Division V State Championship title; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Oberlin High School girls 4x200-meter relay team have combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship in all of their endeavors. With admirable dedication, Alyssa Webb, Aubrey Abeyta, Cayman Noble, and Olivia Hudson have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in their sport, and as a result, they won this year's state title with an impressive time of 1:42.08; and WHEREAS, The achievements and leadership of the Phoenix are an excellent reflection on them and on their coaches. The success of any athletic endeavor depends largely on the ability of coaches to provide both guidance and inspiration, and in leading their team to victory, they has not only helped their runners develop their talents to the fullest but have also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to recognize these fine athletes and their outstanding coaches for a job well done. The maturity and experience the Phoenix have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Oberlin High School girls 4x200-meter relay team on its outstanding success this season and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Oberlin High School girls 4x200-meter relay team.

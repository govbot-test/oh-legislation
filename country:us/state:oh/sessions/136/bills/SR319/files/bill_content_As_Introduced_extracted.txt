Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 319 2025-2026 Senator Romanchuk A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Anthony Oscar on securing the 2026 Division II State Wrestling Championship in the 144-pound weight class. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Anthony Oscar as the 2026 Division II State Wrestling Champion in the 144-pound weight class; and WHEREAS, Anthony Oscar’s accomplishments are truly noteworthy, for he has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his mental and physical fortitude as a wrestler. As a member of the Clear Fork High School wrestling team, he has exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, integrity, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; and WHEREAS, By developing such remarkable prowess in wrestling, Anthony Oscar has earned the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. Indeed, his outstanding record of achievement as the Colts’ first state wrestling champion is sure to inspire future generations; and WHEREAS, Wrestling is a sport which requires exceptional stamina and natural ability, and Anthony Oscar is to be commended for his commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining his athletic goals, he has certainly distinguished himself as a true champion; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize Anthony Oscar on his state wrestling championship and honor him as a fine Ohioan; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Anthony Oscar.

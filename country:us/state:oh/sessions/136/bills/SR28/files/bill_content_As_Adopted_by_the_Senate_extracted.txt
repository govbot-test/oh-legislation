As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 28 2025-2026 Senator McColley Cosponsors: Senators Blessing, Brenner, Chavez, Cirino, Cutrona, Gavarone, Huffman, Johnson, Koehler, Landis, Lang, Manchester, Manning, O'Brien, Patton, Reineke, Reynolds, Roegner, Romanchuk, Schaffer, Timken, Wilkin, Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Theresea Varrasso for outstanding service to the Ohio Senate. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Theresea Varrasso for her loyal service to the Ohio Senate; and WHEREAS, Theresea Varrasso is a remarkable individual, for she has combined civic concern and dedication with selfless initiative to become a dynamic member of the Ohio Senate staff. Commencing her service as a page in 2017, she served as a legislative aide for four senators, a senate journal clerk, and the senior legislative aide for Senator Bill Reineke before being named the deputy press secretary for the Senate Majority Caucus in 2023, and she has brought passion to her work. She has clearly shown the potential of each person to have a positive effect on the quality of life in our society; and WHEREAS, Over the years, Theresea Varrasso has given generously of her time, energy, and abilities to assist the Ohio Senate and has demonstrated a praiseworthy commitment to the success of state government. She has played an important role in maintaining the efficiency and effectiveness of the legislative process, and her efforts have reinforced many services that are currently available to the constituents of our state; and WHEREAS, Theresea Varrasso’s contributions to the Ohio Senate have been greatly appreciated by all those who have had the pleasure of working with her. She has displayed exceptional devotion, responsibility, and enthusiasm in her endeavors, and we extend best wishes for health and happiness in the years to come; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Theresea Varrasso on her exemplary state service and salute her as one of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Theresea Varrasso.

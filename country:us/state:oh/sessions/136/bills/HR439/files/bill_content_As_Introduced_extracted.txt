hr439_00_IN As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. R. No. 439 2025-2026 Representative Lorenz A R E S O L U T I O N Recognizing Water Safety Month. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO: WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize May as Water Safety Month 2026; and WHEREAS, Swimming and other water-based exercises are some of the most popular activities in the United States and these activities contribute to improved physical and mental health and overall quality of life; and WHEREAS, There are approximately 4,000 unintentional drownings and 8,000 nonfatal drownings annually in the United States, accounting for an average of 11 drowning deaths per day and 22 nonfatal drownings per day; and WHEREAS, Drowning is a leading cause of death for children between the ages of 1 to 4 and a second leading cause of unintentional injury death for children between the ages of 5 to 14 in the United States; and WHEREAS, Drowning is preventable through education, supervision, and preparedness; and WHEREAS, Swimming is a vital life-saving skill rather than a luxury. Early childhood instruction can promote water awareness, water competency, safe swimming practices in different aquatic environments, and significantly reduce drowning risks and protect Ohio families and communities; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize May 2026 as Water Safety Month; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit duly authenticated copies of this Resolution to the news media of Ohio.

As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 300 2025-2026 Representative Swearingen A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the St. Mary Central Catholic High School football team as the 2025 OHSFCA 8-Man State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the St. Mary Central Catholic High School football team on winning the 2025 Ohio High School Football Coaches Association 8-Man State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the St. Mary Central Catholic High School football team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the Panthers have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in football, and as a result, they defeated a tough Chalker team by the score of 34-20 to win this year’s OHSFCA 8-Man State Championship and finished the season with an impressive 11‑1 record; and WHEREAS, The Panthers’ achievements and leadership are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the players, their families, their school, their community, and their coach, Toby Hammond. In leading his team to victory, Coach Hammond has not only helped the athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coach for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the St. Mary Central Catholic High School football team on its tremendous season and salute the members and coach as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the St. Mary Central Catholic High School football team.

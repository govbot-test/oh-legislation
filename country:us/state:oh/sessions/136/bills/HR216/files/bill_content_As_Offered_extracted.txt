Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 216 2025-2026 Representative Piccolantonio A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the New Albany High School boys doubles tennis team on winning the 2025 Division I State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the New Albany High School boys doubles tennis team as the 2025 Division I State Champion; and WHEREAS, The members of the New Albany High School boys doubles tennis team have proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned duo, and Jackson Clements and Abhinav Dandu have gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for their spirited and highly motivated performance. Throughout the season, each player worked diligently to improve his skills on the court, and as a result, they accomplished their goals, overcoming a tough pair from Sylvania Southview High School by a score of 6-4, 6-4 to bring home this year’s Division I State Championship; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined doubles tennis team, Jackson Clements and Abhinav Dandu have developed into a remarkably efficient pair whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their coaches; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of players and coaches alike. The members of the New Albany High School boys doubles tennis team have exemplified the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the New Albany High School boys doubles tennis team on its 2025 Division I State Championship and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the New Albany High School boys doubles tennis team.

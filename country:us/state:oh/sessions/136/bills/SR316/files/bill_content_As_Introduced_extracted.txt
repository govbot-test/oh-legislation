Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 316 2025-2026 Senator Blackshear A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Chaminade Julienne High School girls basketball team as the 2026 Division III State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Chaminade Julienne High School girls basketball team on winning the 2026 Division III State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Chaminade Julienne High School girls basketball team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the Eagles have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in basketball, and as a result, they defeated a tough St. Vincent-St. Mary team by the score of 49-40 to win this year’s Division III State Championship, their fourth such title in program history; and WHEREAS, The Eagles’ achievements and leadership are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the players, their families, their school, the Dayton community, and their coaches. In leading their team to victory, Coach Logan Allen and his assistants have not only helped the athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but have also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coaches for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Chaminade Julienne High School girls basketball team on its tremendous season and salute the members and coaches as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Chaminade Julienne High School girls basketball team.

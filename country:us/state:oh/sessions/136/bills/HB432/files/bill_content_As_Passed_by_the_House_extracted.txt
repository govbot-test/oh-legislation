As Passed by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 432 2025-2026 Representatives Brownlee, Miller, K. Cosponsors: Representatives Willis, Daniels, Grim, Rogers, Abrams, Barhorst, Brennan, Brent, Claggett, Click, Cockley, Creech, Deeter, Dovilla, Ghanbari, Gross, Hall, D., Hall, T., Holmes, Hoops, Humphrey, Isaacsohn, Jarrells, John, Johnson, LaRe, Lawson-Rowe, Lear, Lett, Lorenz, Mathews, A., Mathews, T., Miller, J., Mohamed, Odioso, Peterson, Ray, Richardson, Robb Blasdel, Russo, Schmidt, Sigrist, Sims, Stephens, Sweeney, Synenberg, Thomas, C., Tims, Troy, Upchurch, White, A., White, E., Williams, Workman, Young To enact section 5534.482 of the Revised Code to designate a portion of East Kemper Road in Hamilton County as the "Patrolman Earl W. Casterline Memorial Highway." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5534.482 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5534.482. In addition to any other name prescribed in the Revised Code or otherwise, that portion of east Kemper road, between tri county parkway and Lebanon road, in Hamilton county, shall be known as the "Patrolman Earl W. Casterline Memorial Highway." The director of transportation may erect suitable markers along the highway indicating its name.

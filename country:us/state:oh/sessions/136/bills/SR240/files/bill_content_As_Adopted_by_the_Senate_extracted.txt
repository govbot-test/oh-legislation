Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 240 2025-2026 Senator Blackshear A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Oakwood High School girls cross country team on winning the 2025 Division III State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize the Oakwood High School girls cross country team as the 2025 Division III State Champion; and WHEREAS, The members of the Oakwood High School girls cross country team are exemplary athletes, and they have shown themselves to be truly exceptional competitors. At the 2025 Division III State Championship meet, the Jills placed first with a total of 71 points. The unwavering commitment of the runners and coaches has earned them the esteem of many, and they are certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Coach Kurt Sanford has demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in guiding the Oakwood High School girls cross country team to victory, and his skill is reflected in the squad’s outstanding accomplishments. Due largely to his tireless efforts, the members have distinguished themselves on the course, and he has received respect and admiration from all who know him; and WHEREAS, In an educational system that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual and group effort, athletic competition has become a valuable tool. By taking part in sports, the members of the Oakwood High School girls cross country team have gained discipline and perseverance that will help them in all aspects of their lives; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to the Oakwood High School girls cross country team as the 2025 Division III State Champion and honor all those who contributed to the success of this fine squad; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Oakwood High School girls cross country team.

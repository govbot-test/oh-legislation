As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 15 2025-2026 Representative Roemer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Barberton Laundry, Inc., on its One Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Barberton Laundry, Inc., on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is, indeed, a fitting tribute to Barberton Laundry, for over the past one hundred years, this fine business has attained a remarkable record of service to the area. Since its founding on April 7, 1924, the company has continually demonstrated its exemplary commitment to the highest industry standards, customer satisfaction, and competitive pricing, and its dedication to the best interests of its clientele has been evident in all its transactions; and WHEREAS, The success of Barberton Laundry is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and hard-working employees. Their vision and initiative have earned this respected institution the gratitude and appreciation of many satisfied customers, and they are truly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Over the years, Barberton Laundry has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding community and contributed greatly to the area’s economic well-being. We are certain that as this worthy enterprise maintains its dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to prosper and will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Barberton Laundry, Inc., on its Centennial and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Barberton Laundry, Inc.

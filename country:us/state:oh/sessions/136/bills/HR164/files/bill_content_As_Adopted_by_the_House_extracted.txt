Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 164 2025-2026 Representative Lorenz A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Delaware Area Career Center video production team as a 2025 Business Professionals of America National Leadership Conference Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Delaware Area Career Center video production team on winning the 2025 Business Professionals of America National Leadership Conference Championship; and WHEREAS, The members of the Delaware Area Career Center video production team are a remarkable group of young academicians, combining talent and competitive spirit with exceptional knowledge to establish themselves as truly exemplary students. With commendable dedication, they performed admirably against teams from across the United States, and as a result, their project won the national championship at this year’s Business Professionals of America National Leadership Conference. Throughout long hours of practice and preparation, members Abigail Camealy, Nicholas DeVault, Brandon Fisher, and Michael Kromhout have displayed a degree of determination and motivation of which they can be proud; and WHEREAS, In preparing themselves for this important contest, the members of the Delaware Area Career Center video production team have had the opportunity to express their individual abilities and to acquire knowledge and information that will be of great benefit to them throughout their lives. Having worked diligently to sharpen their skills, they confronted competition with confidence and enthusiasm and distinguished themselves as worthy representatives of their school, community, and state; and WHEREAS, The success of the Delaware Area Career Center video production team is a justifiable source of pride not only for the members but also for their advisors, their families, their school, and their community. The maturity and experience the students have gained through their participation in this exciting activity will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Delaware Area Career Center video production team for outstanding achievement and salute the members and advisors as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Delaware Area Career Center video production team.

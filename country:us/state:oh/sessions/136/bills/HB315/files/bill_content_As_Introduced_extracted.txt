As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 315 2025-2026 Representatives Sims, Williams Cosponsors: Representatives Lawson-Rowe, Brennan, Synenberg, Robinson, Jarrells, Miller, J., Grim, Somani, Brownlee, Brent, Upchurch, White, E., Sigrist, Lett, Isaacsohn, Cockley, Piccolantonio, Thomas, C., Rader, Hall, D., Rogers, Baker, Brewer, Mohamed, Tims, McNally, Russo, Abdullahi, Daniels To enact section 5.282 of the Revised Code to designate May 29 as "Sojourner Truth Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.282 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.282. The twenty-ninth day of May is designated as "Sojourner Truth Day."

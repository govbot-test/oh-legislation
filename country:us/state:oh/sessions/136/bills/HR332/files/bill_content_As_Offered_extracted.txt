Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 332 2025-2026 Representative Deeter A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Hartland Township on its Two Hundredth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize Hartland Township on the jubilant occasion of its Bicentennial; and WHEREAS, Since the inception of Hartland Township two hundred years ago, its residents have experienced dramatic growth and change, and yet, commendably, even as they have exuberantly embraced the promise of a bright future, they have never relinquished their appreciation for the worthiest virtues of the past. Without sacrificing their sense of tradition in the name of progress, the people of Hartland have managed to nurture a fresh perspective and a positive outlook that endear this distinctive place to all who know it well; and WHEREAS, Throughout its existence, Hartland Township has enjoyed the benefits of a generous and civic-minded population, and those who currently make it their home take great pride in their community and its rich heritage. Indeed, a vigorous commitment to public service and an earnest devotion to lofty ideals have long been second nature to most of Hartland’s residents, and vigilance, industriousness, and generosity have come to typify their collective character. Clearly, their efforts have certainly made the area a pleasant and friendly place in which to live and work; and WHEREAS, Every state depends upon such vibrant, durable communities as Hartland Township for its own survival and stability. Although it has known adversity and tribulation, it also has been favored with its share of blessings. In the years to come, the township is sure to remain on its admirable course and to continue to transform challenges into opportunities; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend our congratulations to Hartland Township as it commemorates its Bicentennial and offer our best wishes for its perennial good fortune; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Hartland Township.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 329 2025-2026 Representative Abdullahi Cosponsors: Representatives Brent, Rader, Sims, McNally, Russo, Jarrells, Humphrey, Sweeney, Synenberg, White, E., Isaacsohn, Lett, Lawson-Rowe, Mohamed, Robinson, Grim, Brewer, Piccolantonio, Hall, D. A BILL To enact section 5.2323 of the Revised Code to designate February 1st as Ohio World Hijab Day. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2323 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2323. The first day of February is designated as "Ohio World Hijab Day," in honor of "World Hijab Day."

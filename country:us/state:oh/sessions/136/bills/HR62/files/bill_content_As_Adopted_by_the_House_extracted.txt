Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 62 2025-2026 Speaker Huffman A R E S O L U T I O N Authorizing the Ohio Youth Leadership Association to use the Ohio House Chambers to conduct its 2025 model legislature. BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE STATE OF OHIO: WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio have learned that the Ohio Youth Leadership Association has requested permission to use the Ohio House Chambers for its model legislature event, April 10-12, 2025; and WHEREAS, A beneficial educational organization, the Ohio Youth Leadership Association provides students with the opportunity to better appreciate and understand government and its components, and the knowledge gained through this event will certainly be of value to all participants throughout their lifetimes; and WHEREAS, By utilizing the facilities that accommodate those involved in the state legislative process, the Ohio Youth Leadership Association will offer students an accurate depiction of state government and the manner in which legislation is created and processed; and WHEREAS, Through participation in the Ohio Youth Leadership Association, students will be better prepared to become productive members of our complex and ever-changing society. The maturity and experience the participants will gain through their involvement in this program will not only enrich their total educational experience but also enable them to make better-informed decisions as they assume the responsibilities of adult citizenship; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, permit the Ohio Youth Leadership Association to use the Ohio House Chambers for its 2025 Ohio Youth in Government event, April 10-12, 2025, contingent upon the availability of those facilities, and salute its participants as conscientious young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Ohio Youth Leadership Association.

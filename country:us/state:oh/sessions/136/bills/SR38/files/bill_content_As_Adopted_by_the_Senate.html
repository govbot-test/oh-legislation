A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Annice Carter on her years of service to the Ohio Legislative Service Commission.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Annice Carter on the memorable occasion of her retirement from the Ohio Legislative Service Commission, March 3, 2025; and

WHEREAS, Annice Carter has earnestly served the State of Ohio for three decades, and throughout the entirety of her tenure, she has been celebrated for her dedication to public service and her sterling reputation. Commencing her career with the Ohio Legislative Budget Office on December 5, 1994, she continued to work as a member of the LSC support staff team after LBO merged with LSC, and she joined Resolution Typing in 2008, where she has set herself apart as a consummate professional and an asset to the department; and

WHEREAS, Annice Carter has been one of the Ohio Legislative Service Commission’s most capable ambassadors, giving graciously of her time and expertise and demonstrating a praiseworthy commitment to the success of state government. Time and again, she has ensured the estimable representation of the Ohio General Assembly and its elected members, and her valuable service has provided the constituents of our state with the commitment and effectiveness required by the legislative branch; and

WHEREAS, Annice Carter’s contributions to the Ohio Legislative Service Commission, to the Ohio General Assembly, and to the public at large have been greatly admired by all those who have had the pleasure of working with her, and her professional endeavors have embodied the tenets of dependability, sincerity, and integrity. Indeed, her distinguished service has benefited her colleagues, her fellow citizens, and the State of Ohio, and her exemplary work ethic has become her enduring legacy; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, extend a heartfelt salute to Annice Carter, a fine Ohioan, and offer best wishes for a happy, healthy retirement; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Annice Carter.

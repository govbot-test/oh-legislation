As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 197 2025-2026 Representative McNally A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring St. Mary’s Byzantine Catholic Church on its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to St. Mary’s Byzantine Catholic Church on the hallowed occasion of its One Hundred Twenty-fifth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, Since its inception, St. Mary’s Byzantine Catholic Church has offered its congregation the pathway to a more meaningful life by illuminating and demonstrating the precepts on which the church was founded. The spirit of love and Christian fellowship that exists among the parishioners not only prevails within the church edifice itself but also extends beyond those sacred walls to touch the lives of everyone encountered by these devout disciples; and WHEREAS, Under the capable leadership of its pastors, both past and present, St. Mary’s Byzantine Catholic Church has experienced continual growth and has provided a source of moral guidance and spiritual motivation for each of its members. Over the years, the church has enhanced the quality of life within Youngstown and the surrounding area, and through its generous efforts, it has earned the gratitude and respect of the many citizens it has so capably served; and WHEREAS, An occasion such as this is an important milestone which represents dedication and commitment. For a century and a quarter, the members of St. Mary’s Byzantine Catholic Church have kept their faith alive in the community, and on this special anniversary, the congregation has accepted the challenge of carrying its beliefs into the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate St. Mary’s Byzantine Catholic Church on its One Hundred Twenty‑fifth Anniversary and pay tribute to all those joining in the celebration of this joyous event; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to St. Mary’s Byzantine Catholic Church.

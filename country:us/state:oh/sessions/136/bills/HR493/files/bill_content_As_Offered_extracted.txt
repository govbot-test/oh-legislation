hr493_00_IN As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 493 2025-2026 Representative Rogers A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Scott High School boys 4x200-meter relay team on securing the 2026 Division II State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud the Scott High School boys 4x200-meter relay team as the 2026 Division II State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Scott High School boys 4x200-meter relay team is made up of outstanding athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year's Division II State Tournament, the Bulldogs won the state crown with a time of 1:26.38, and members Aidan Miller, Christian Bourn, Ta'Ron Williams, and W.T. Young III amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional coaching staff as well as the support of the Bulldogs community, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Scott High School boys 4x200-meter relay team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in track and field; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate the Scott High School boys 4x200-meter relay team on its 2026 Division II State Championship title and count its members and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Scott High School boys 4x200-meter relay team.

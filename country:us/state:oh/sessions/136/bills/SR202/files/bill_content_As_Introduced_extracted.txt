Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 202 2025-2026 Senator Brenner A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Lehner’s Pumpkin Farm on being voted the Best Pumpkin Patch in the United States. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Lehner’s Pumpkin Farm on being named the Best Pumpkin Patch in the United States by the 2025 USA Today Readers’ Choice Awards; and WHEREAS, This is a remarkable honor, and congratulations are in order. Lehner’s Pumpkin Farm has become a beloved part of the Radnor community, and after a rigorous selection and screening process, it was voted the 2025 Best Pumpkin Patch in the United States. Considering the farm’s positive impact on Central Ohio, it is no surprise that this fine endeavor is so widely held in high regard; and WHEREAS, The noteworthy success of Lehner’s Pumpkin Farm is attributable largely to the loyal service of its committed staff, as well as to the leadership of its management. Their combined efforts have earned the endeavor a well-deserved reputation for achievement and have distinguished the business as a worthy enterprise that has contributed greatly to the well-being of the area; and WHEREAS, Since its establishment, Lehner’s Pumpkin Farm has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding region, and it is truly deserving of high praise. Indeed, this family-owned operation has gained the appreciation of many satisfied guests with its wide range of attractions, including a pick-your-own pumpkin patch, barrel rides, a corn maze, a play village, a petting zoo, climbing walls, and tractor races. We are certain that as this noteworthy endeavor maintains its unwavering dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to grow and prosper and will follow in the tradition of excellence that has become its hallmark; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize Lehner’s Pumpkin Farm for outstanding attainment and look with optimism to its ongoing contributions to the community; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Lehner’s Pumpkin Farm.

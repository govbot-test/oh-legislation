A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Krill Funeral Service on its Sesquicentennial.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Krill Funeral Service on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to Krill Funeral Service, for throughout the past century and a half, it has attained a remarkable record of professionalism. Founded by the Krill family in 1875 as an offshoot of a furniture business, this fine venture is now the largest funeral provider in Williams County, with locations operating in Edgerton, Edon, and Bryan, and over the years, it has expanded on its support for all forms of family loss; and

WHEREAS, The success of Krill Funeral Service is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and hard‑working employees. Offering support, comfort, and dignified ceremonies for its clients, this outstanding establishment has helped countless people cope with one of the most difficult parts of their lives, the death of a loved one; and

WHEREAS, Time and again, Krill Funeral Service has earned a well-deserved reputation for compassion by providing assistance and concern to individuals and families in their time of bereavement. This noteworthy enterprise has maintained an unfaltering investment in the community for one hundred fifty years, and we are certain that it will continue to uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Krill Funeral Service on its One Hundred Fiftieth Anniversary and salute it as a fine Ohio endeavor; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Krill Funeral Service.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 248 2025-2026 Senator Ingram A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Summit Country Day High School girls soccer team as the 2025 Division IV State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Summit Country Day High School girls soccer team on winning the 2025 Division IV State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Summit Country Day High School girls soccer team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the Silver Knights have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in soccer, and as a result, they defeated a tough Columbus Academy team by the score of 4-0 to win this year’s Division IV State Championship, their third consecutive such crown, and finished the season with an impressive 22-0-2 record; and WHEREAS, The Silver Knights’ achievements and leadership are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the players, their families, their school, their community, and their coach, Mike Fee. In leading the team to victory, Coach Fee has not only helped the athletes to develop their talents to the fullest but also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to the exemplary athletes and outstanding coach who comprise the Summit Country Day High School girls soccer team. The maturity and experience they have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Summit Country Day High School girls soccer team on a tremendous 2025 season and salute the members and coach as some of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Summit Country Day High School girls soccer team.

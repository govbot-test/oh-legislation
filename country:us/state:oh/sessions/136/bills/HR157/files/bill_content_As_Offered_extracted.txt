As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 157 2025-2026 Representative Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Rania Brown as the 2025 Division I State Discus Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize Rania Brown, a recent graduate of Avon High School, on winning the 2025 Division I State Track and Field Championship in the discus; and WHEREAS, Displaying exceptional form, ability, leadership, and dedication during the 2025 season, Rania Brown certainly distinguished herself as one of the most talented throwers in Ohio. After winning the 2024 state championship in the discus, she once again prevailed in this event at this year’s Division I State Meet with a mark of 153 feet, 4 inches, and she is deserving of the highest praise; and WHEREAS, Throughout intense training and preparation, Rania Brown has consistently demonstrated the talent, integrity, and motivation that are the hallmarks of a true champion. She has inspired her teammates, thrilled her fans, gained the respect of her opponents, and made her coaches, family, and community proud; and WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. Clearly, Rania Brown has learned much about excellence as an athlete, and she has made a significant impact on high school track and field in our state; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, pay tribute to Rania Brown on her success and extend best wishes for the years ahead; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Rania Brown.

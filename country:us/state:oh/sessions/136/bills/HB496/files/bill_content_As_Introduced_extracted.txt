Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session	H. B. No. 496 2025-2026 Representatives Brewer, Williams Cosponsors: Representatives Brennan, Rogers, Brent, Click, Salvo, McNally, Lett, McClain, Russo To enact section 5.621 of the Revised Code to designate "Fathers Walk Week." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.621 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.621. The third week of September is designated as "Fathers Walk Week" to encourage fathers to walk their children to school.

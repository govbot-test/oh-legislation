Representative Miller, J.


A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Marion L. Steele High School cheer team as a 2025 OASSA Division 2 State Champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Marion L. Steele High School cheer team on earning the 2025 Ohio Association of Secondary School Administrators Division 2 Non-Building Game Day State Championship; and

WHEREAS, The Marion L. Steele High School cheer team is made up of tremendous athletes who have worked diligently to showcase the collective talents of their exceptional squad. At this year’s OASSA Division 2 State Tournament, the Comets decisively won the state crown in the non-building game day category, and the members amply demonstrated impressive determination, maturity, and finesse in pursuit of this achievement; and

WHEREAS, This triumph reflects the staunch resolve of these young individuals, and it signifies the acquisition of valuable experience that is sure to benefit them in later life. Thanks to the tireless efforts of an exceptional Marion L. Steele High School cheer team coaching staff, as well as the support of their Comets community, these athletes have met the demands of an extensive training regimen, and they have just cause for celebration; and

WHEREAS, All those who comprise the Marion L. Steele High School cheer team have set an excellent example of what a dedicated group of athletes can achieve when guided by a committed coaching staff and surrounded by loyal fans, sports families, and boosters. These young competitors have certainly proven themselves to be dynamic athletic leaders, and we are confident that as they meet future challenges, they will take with them the valuable lessons they have gained through their participation in cheerleading; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Marion L. Steele High School cheer team on its 2025 OASSA Division 2 State Championship title and count its members and coaches among the ranks of outstanding Ohioans; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Marion L. Steele High School cheer team.

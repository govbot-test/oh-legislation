Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 121 2025-2026 Senator Blessing A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Isaac Rogers for outstanding service to the Ohio Senate. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Isaac Rogers for his loyal service to the Ohio Senate; and WHEREAS, Isaac Rogers is a remarkable individual, for he has combined civic concern and dedication with selfless initiative to become a dynamic member of the Ohio Senate staff. Commencing his service in 2020, he has distinguished himself throughout his time as a senior legislative aide to Senator Louis W. Blessing, and he has brought tremendous passion to his important work. He has clearly shown the potential of each person to have a positive effect on the quality of life in our society; and WHEREAS, Over the years, Isaac Rogers has given generously of his time, energy, and abilities to assist the Ohio Senate and has demonstrated a praiseworthy commitment to the success of state government. He has played an important role in maintaining the efficiency and effectiveness of the legislative process, and his efforts have reinforced many services that are currently available to the constituents of our state; and WHEREAS, Isaac Rogers’ contributions to the Ohio Senate have been greatly appreciated by all those who have had the pleasure of working with him. He has displayed exceptional devotion, responsibility, and enthusiasm in his endeavors, and we extend best wishes for health and happiness in the years to come; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Isaac Rogers on his exemplary state service and salute him as one of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Isaac Rogers.

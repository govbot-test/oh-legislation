A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Marcus Benjamin for outstanding service to the State of Ohio.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Marcus Benjamin for his loyal service to the Ohio Senate; and

WHEREAS, Throughout Marcus Benjamin’s distinguished tenure, he has demonstrated an unwavering dedication to performing his duties and fulfilling his responsibilities with the utmost diligence and competence. Starting as a legislative assistant for Senator Thomas Patton in 2012, he went on to serve as a policy advisor and senior policy advisor before being named a deputy policy director. In his work, he has been instrumental in maintaining the efficiency and effectiveness of the legislative process and in ensuring that our state continues to uphold the ideals that are embodied in our constitution and in the laws enacted under it; and

WHEREAS, The expertise and professionalism Marcus Benjamin has shown in his endeavors have gained him the admiration and esteem of all those with whom he has been associated. As he reflects on his service with the Ohio General Assembly staff, he has the satisfaction of knowing that he has provided a valuable service to the state, and his record of achievement will stand as a hallmark for others to emulate; and

WHEREAS, Marcus Benjamin’s contributions to the State of Ohio have been greatly appreciated by everyone who has had the pleasure of working with him. He has displayed exceptional devotion, responsibility, and enthusiasm over the years, and we offer best wishes as he begins a new chapter in his estimable career; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Marcus Benjamin for his exemplary public service and applaud him as a fine Ohioan; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Marcus Benjamin.

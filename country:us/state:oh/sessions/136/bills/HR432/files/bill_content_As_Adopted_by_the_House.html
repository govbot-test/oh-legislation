A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Laibe Electric Technology on its One Hundredth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Laibe Electric Technology on its Centennial; and

WHEREAS, Since its establishment in 1926, Laibe Electric Technology has experienced tremendous prosperity by providing a variety of technology solutions to its clients. The company has continually demonstrated its exemplary commitment to the highest industry standards, quality workmanship, and competitive pricing, and its dedication to the best interests of its customers has been evident in all its transactions; and

WHEREAS, The notable success of Laibe Electric Technology is due largely to the loyal service of its qualified and committed staff, as well as to the innovation and foresight of its management. Their combined efforts have earned the venture a well-deserved reputation for excellence and have distinguished the organization as a worthy enterprise that has contributed greatly to the area's economic well-being; and

WHEREAS, The achievements of Laibe Electric Technology over the past one hundred years can be attributed to the vision and foresight of a host of innovative individuals, and all those who have been associated with the company, past and present, are to be applauded on their endeavors. Indeed, they have convincingly demonstrated how very much can be accomplished by a group of conscientious people with clear objectives and firm resolve, and it is through the work of citizens such as these that Ohio continues to be a pleasant place in which to live; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Laibe Electric Technology on its Centennial and salute it as an industry leader; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Laibe Electric Technology.

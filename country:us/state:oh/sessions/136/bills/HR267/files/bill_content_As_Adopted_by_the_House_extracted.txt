Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 267 2025-2026 Representative McNally A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Unitarian Universalist Church of Youngstown on the Centennial of its building. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Unitarian Universalist Church of Youngstown on the auspicious occasion of the One Hundredth Anniversary of its building, celebrated October 18, 2025; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this significant milestone is a fitting tribute to Unitarian Universalist Church of Youngstown, for it has attained a praiseworthy record of service over the years. Founded as First Unitarian Church of Youngstown in 1892, this fine house of worship constructed its current building in 1925, adding an education wing in 1969, and its achievements are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on the church itself but also on its devoted leaders and members and on the Youngstown community; and WHEREAS, The source of many social, religious, and humanitarian projects and programs, Unitarian Universalist Church of Youngstown has enhanced the quality of life in the area since its inception, and its generous contributions have earned it the gratitude and respect of the many citizens it has so capably served. We are certain that in the years to come, the church will maintain the tradition of spiritual enlightenment that has long been its hallmark; and WHEREAS, The members of Unitarian Universalist Church of Youngstown have long been actively involved in, and concerned about, the events in their community, and this celebration offers them a unique opportunity to share their memories and to reflect on the benefits of life in their close-knit community. The congregation can, indeed, be proud of their church, for they have built on the best traditions of the past to ensure a peaceful and robust future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Unitarian Universalist Church of Youngstown on the One Hundredth Anniversary of its building and extend best wishes for continued growth and prosperity; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Unitarian Universalist Church of Youngstown.

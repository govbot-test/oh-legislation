As Reported by the Senate Government Oversight and Reform Committee 136th General Assembly Regular Session	S. B. No. 21 2025-2026 Senators Brenner, Reineke Cosponsor: Senator Johnson A BILL To enact section 5.2322 of the Revised Code to designate October 4 as "Rutherford B. Hayes Day." BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF OHIO: Section 1. That section 5.2322 of the Revised Code be enacted to read as follows: Sec. 5.2322. The fourth day of October is designated as "Rutherford B. Hayes Day."

Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 215 2025-2026 Representative Ghanbari A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Marcus Blaze on winning a 2025 Division I State Wrestling Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Marcus Blaze as the 2025 Division I State Wrestling Champion in the 150-pound weight class; and WHEREAS, This accomplishment is truly noteworthy, for Marcus Blaze has clearly demonstrated his talent and perseverance, as well as his mental and physical fortitude. As this year’s Division I State Wrestling Champion in the 150-pound weight class, he holds the special distinction of becoming one of our state’s few four-time champions, and in winning this crown, he has exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of the sports world, including courage, determination, and an innate sense of the principles of good sportsmanship; and WHEREAS, In displaying such outstanding prowess as a senior member of the Perrysburg High School wrestling team, Marcus Blaze has earned the appreciation of his fellow athletes, the devotion of countless fans, and the respect of even his most determined rivals. Competition in athletics has enabled him to learn much about dedication and self‑discipline, and we take this opportunity to acknowledge his exemplary record of achievement; and WHEREAS, Wrestling is a sport that requires exceptional strength, stamina, and natural ability, and Marcus Blaze is to be commended for his commitment to this demanding activity. In attaining his athletic goals, he has consistently exhibited the skill and integrity that are the hallmarks of a true champion; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Marcus Blaze on his personal success and salute him as one of Ohio’s finest young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Marcus Blaze.

A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring Crown Battery Manufacturing on its One Hundredth Anniversary.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Crown Battery Manufacturing on the auspicious occasion of its One Hundredth Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is, indeed, a fitting tribute to Crown Battery Manufacturing, for over the past one hundred years, this fine business has attained a remarkable record of service by powering equipment and systems around the world. Since its founding in 1926 by William J. Koenig, the company has continually demonstrated its exemplary commitment to the highest industry standards, customer satisfaction, and competitive pricing, and its dedication to the best interests of its clientele has been evident in all its transactions; and

WHEREAS, The success of Crown Battery Manufacturing is a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on the business itself but also on its astute management and hard-working employees. Their vision and initiative have earned this respected institution the gratitude and respect of many appreciative customers, and they are truly deserving of high praise; and

WHEREAS, Over the years, Crown Battery Manufacturing has enhanced the quality of life within the surrounding community and has contributed greatly to the area’s economic well-being. We are certain that as this worthy enterprise maintains its dedication to service and achievement, it will continue to prosper and will uphold the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Crown Battery Manufacturing on its Centennial and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Crown Battery Manufacturing.

Section 1:
sr376_02_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 376 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team as the 2026 Division III State Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team on winning the 2026 Division III State Championship; and WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the team members have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in softball, and as a result, they defeated a tough Louisville team by a score of 10-1 to win this year's Division III State Championship; and WHEREAS, The achievements and leadership of the members of the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the players, their families, their school, their community, and their coach. These athletes have not only developed their talents to the fullest but also learned valuable lessons of determination, perseverance, and camaraderie; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coach for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team on its tremendous season and salute the members and coach as some of Ohio's finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Bloom-Carroll High School softball team.

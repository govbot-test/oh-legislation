Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 336 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Joe Curry on winning a 2026 Division II State Wrestling Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Joe Curry on securing first place in the 126-pound weight class at the 2026 Division II State Wrestling Meet; and WHEREAS, Joe Curry has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned athlete who has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for his spirited and highly motivated performance in wrestling. Throughout the season, he has practiced diligently to improve his skills on the mat, and as a result, he accomplished his goals, including winning this year’s Division II State Championship in the 126-pound weight class and finishing the season with a 47-4 record; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined individual, Joe Curry has developed into a remarkably dedicated and efficient wrestler whose skill is equaled only by his exemplary display of sportsmanship. Indeed, he is a credit not only to his family and Licking Valley High School but also to the superb leadership of his coaches; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of athletes and coaches alike. In all of his sports endeavors, Joe Curry has overcome a variety of challenges, and his tremendous efforts are worthy of emulation; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute Joe Curry on his brilliant wrestling season and wish him the best in the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Joe Curry.

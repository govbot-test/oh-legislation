Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 48 2025-2026 Representative Russo A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Hayden Hollingsworth as a 2025 OHSAA Division I Swimming Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Hayden Hollingsworth on winning the 2025 OHSAA Division I Championship in the 50-yard freestyle event; and WHEREAS, A student at Upper Arlington High School, Hayden Hollingsworth has combined talent and competitive spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish herself as a truly exceptional athlete. With admirable dedication, she has worked diligently to attain ultimate proficiency in swimming, and as a result, she won the Division I State Championship title in the 50-yard freestyle event for the third straight year, finished second in the 100-yard butterfly event, and was a member of the championship-winning 200-yard medley relay and 200-yard freestyle relay teams; and WHEREAS, Hayden Hollingsworth’s accomplishments and leadership, both in and out of athletic competition, are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on her but also on her family, her teammates, her coaches, her school, and her community. Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect and esteem of many, and she is certainly deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are overjoyed to pay tribute to Hayden Hollingsworth on an exemplary season. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has gained through her participation in swimming will greatly aid her in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Hayden Hollingsworth on her personal success and salute her as one of Ohio’s finest young citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Hayden Hollingsworth.

Section 1:
As Introduced 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 166 2025-2026 Senator Brenner Cosponsors: Senators Antonio, Blackshear, Blessing, Chavez, Cirino, Craig, Cutrona, DeMora, Gavarone, Hicks-Hudson, Huffman, Ingram, Johnson, Koehler, Landis, Lang, Liston, Manchester, Manning, McColley, O'Brien, Patton, Reineke, Reynolds, Roegner, Romanchuk, Schaffer, Smith, Timken, Weinstein, Wilkin, Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Randy Queen for outstanding service to the State of Ohio. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Randy Queen for his loyal service to the Capitol Square Review and Advisory Board; and WHEREAS, Randy Queen is a remarkable individual, for he has combined civic concern and dedication with selfless initiative to become a dynamic member of the General Assembly staff. Contributing significantly to the restoration of the Ohio Statehouse through his work with various contractors, he commenced his service with CSRAB as a stone mason in 1994, and he advanced to be named its maintenance manager in 2018. He certainly has performed his duties and fulfilled his responsibilities with the utmost efficiency and competence during his thirty-year tenure. Indeed, he is deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, Over the past three decades, Randy Queen has given generously of his time, energy, and abilities to facilitate the operation of state government while preserving the historical integrity of the Capitol Square Complex. He has provided a valuable service by helping to ensure the health, safety, and convenience of those who work in and visit the Ohio Statehouse, and without the efforts of individuals such as him, the services that are currently available to the constituents of our state would cease to exist; and WHEREAS, Randy Queen’s contributions to the State of Ohio have been greatly appreciated by all those who have had the pleasure of working with him. He has displayed exceptional devotion, responsibility, and enthusiasm in his endeavors, and we offer best wishes for health and happiness in the years to come; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Randy Queen on his exemplary state service and salute him as one of Ohio’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Randy Queen.

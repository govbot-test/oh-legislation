Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 335 2025-2026 Senator Manning A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Lorain High School Valorant esports team on winning the 2026 OHSAA State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to salute the Lorain High School Valorant esports team as the 2026 OHSAA State Champion; and WHEREAS, Establishing a reputation for its skill and tenacity, the Lorain High School Valorant esports team is an impressive group of gamers whose talent and teamwork are unmatched. At the 2026 OHSAA State Championship, the squad seized the title, and this victory will be celebrated for many years to come; and WHEREAS, The members of the Lorain High School Valorant esports team rose to the occasion and proved themselves to be dynamic and driven individuals. Truly, these outstanding students can be proud of the accomplishments that were hard-won and deserved throughout the season; and WHEREAS, By remaining faithful to the highest standards of teamwork, Lorain High School Valorant esports team members Julian Montero, Ramon Quintero, Gage Reinhart, Tristan Rose, Isaiah Vergara, Angel Olalde, and Riley Frey primed themselves to reach their full potential under the guidance of Coach Seth Litman. Indeed, they have demonstrated remarkable energy, enthusiasm, and dedication in all of their endeavors; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize the Lorain High School Valorant esports team on its success and honor all those associated with the team for their tremendous prowess; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Lorain High School Valorant esports team.

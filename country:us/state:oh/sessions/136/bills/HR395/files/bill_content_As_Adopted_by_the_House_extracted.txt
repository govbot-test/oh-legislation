Section 1:
As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 395 2025-2026 Representative Hoops A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Tyler Barnes as a 2026 Division III State Wrestling Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to applaud Tyler Barnes on winning first place in the 126-pound weight class at the 2026 Division III State Wrestling Meet; and WHEREAS, Tyler Barnes has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned athlete who has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for his spirited and highly motivated performance in wrestling. Throughout the season, he has worked diligently to improve his skills on the mat, and as a result, he accomplished his goal of winning this year’s Division III State Championship in the 126-pound weight class and finished with a 44-3 overall record; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined individual, Tyler Barnes has developed into a remarkably dedicated and efficient wrestler whose skill is equaled only by his exemplary display of sportsmanship. Indeed, he is a credit not only to his family and Pike-Delta-York High School but also to the superb leadership of his coaches, Marc Nagel and Drew Mattin; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of athletes and coaches alike. In all of his sports endeavors, Tyler Barnes has overcome a variety of challenges, and his tremendous efforts have earned him the admiration of all those who have witnessed him in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute Tyler Barnes on his brilliant wrestling season and wish him the best in the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Tyler Barnes.

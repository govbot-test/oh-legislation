A R E S O L U T I O N

Recognizing Ohio Child Abuse Prevention Month.

WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to recognize April as Ohio Child Abuse Prevention Month 2025; and

WHEREAS, The incidents and prevalence of child abuse and neglect have reached alarming proportions, and in an effort to address this issue, Governor Mike DeWine designated April 2025 as Ohio Child Abuse Prevention Month. During the month of April each year, various educational activities and events are coordinated in an effort to seek and apply solutions to this escalating problem; and

WHEREAS, Childhood is a formative time, and communities, family members, educators, public officials, and faith-based organizations all play important roles in helping to ensure that our children are safe and can grow surrounded by love and stability. Every Ohioan has a responsibility to support children and promote stable families across our state; and

WHEREAS, Child abuse prevention is one of the most critical issues we can address for the long-term prosperity of our country. Effective child development is hindered by toxic stress resulting from abuse and neglect, yet we can strive for a more promising future for this next generation of leaders by encouraging parental resiliency, social connection, concrete support, and knowledge of the social and emotional aspects of parenting. We hold sacred the trust of Ohio’s most valuable resource, our children, and the future of our country depends on the opportunities we provide them today; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, recognize April 2025 as Ohio Child Abuse Prevention Month; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit duly authenticated copies of this Resolution to the news media of Ohio.

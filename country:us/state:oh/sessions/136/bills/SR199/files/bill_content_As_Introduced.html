A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Fairfield County Fair on its one hundred seventy-fifth season.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to the Fairfield County Fair as it celebrates its one hundred seventy-fifth season; and

WHEREAS, Since the establishment of the Fairfield County Fair, all those involved with this iconic gathering have demonstrated exemplary commitment in their support of this event. Generations of hard-working people have seen to it that the fair has endured, and it is through the efforts of such individuals that Fairfield County and the State of Ohio continue to grow and prosper; and

WHEREAS, For the past seventeen and a half decades, the Fairfield County Fair has afforded local citizens the opportunity to gather together, to enjoy a range of diverse and exciting activities for all ages and interests, and to showcase their pride in their community. The friendliness and neighborly atmosphere of the fair have certainly distinguished it as one of Ohio’s finest celebrations; and

WHEREAS, Over the years, Fairfield County has benefited from a generous and civic-minded population, and the many individuals who have worked so diligently to ensure the success of the Fairfield County Fair have displayed a commendable spirit of cooperation and commitment. The area’s citizens are deserving of high praise for their optimism and determination; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud the Fairfield County Fair on its one hundred seventy-fifth season and extend best wishes for the future; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Fairfield County Fair.

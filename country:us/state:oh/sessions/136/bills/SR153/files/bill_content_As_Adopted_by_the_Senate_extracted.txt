Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 153 2025-2026 Senator Liston Cosponsors: Senators Antonio, Blackshear, Blessing, Brenner, Chavez, Cirino, Craig, Cutrona, DeMora, Gavarone, Hicks-Hudson, Huffman, Ingram, Johnson, Koehler, Landis, Lang, Manchester, Manning, McColley, O'Brien, Patton, Reineke, Reynolds, Roegner, Romanchuk, Schaffer, Smith, Timken, Weinstein, Wilkin, Wilson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Ricardo DeLaSerda, Jr. on his retirement from Ohio Legislative Information Systems. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Ricardo DeLaSerda, Jr., on retiring from Ohio Legislative Information Systems; and WHEREAS, Rick DeLaSerda is a truly remarkable individual, for he has combined civic concern and diligence with selfless initiative to become an invaluable asset to Ohio Legislative Information Systems. Joining the agency in 2000, he has brought to LIS a wealth of experience gained as a computer engineer with Texas Instruments for twenty-four years. Indeed, he has performed his duties and fulfilled his responsibilities with the utmost efficiency and competence across his two and a half decades of distinguished and outstanding public service; and WHEREAS, Throughout his career, Rick DeLaSerda has given generously of his time, energy, and abilities to assist the Ohio General Assembly, and he has displayed a noteworthy dedication to the success of state government. He has provided a valuable service by helping to maintain the efficiency and effectiveness of the legislative process, and he is deserving of high praise; and WHEREAS, We are proud to note that it is through the unsurpassed efforts and deep commitment of conscientious people such as Rick DeLaSerda that the State of Ohio continues to prosper. Over the years, he has striven to serve his fellow citizens to the best of his ability, and we applaud him on his fine work; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Rick DeLaSerda, Jr., on the occasion of his retirement and offer best wishes for peace and prosperity in the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Ricardo DeLaSerda, Jr.

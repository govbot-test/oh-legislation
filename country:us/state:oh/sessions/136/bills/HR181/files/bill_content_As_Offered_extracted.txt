Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 181 2025-2026 Representative Johnson A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Hocking County Courthouse on its Centennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Hocking County Courthouse on its One Hundredth Anniversary, celebrated at a re‑dedication event, June 23, 2025; and WHEREAS, Recognition of this prestigious milestone is a fitting tribute to the Hocking County Courthouse, for it has long stood as a piece of the area’s unique story. Dedicated in 1925, the courthouse has become a beloved pillar of its community and the centerpiece of the City of Logan and Hocking County; and WHEREAS, The Hocking County Courthouse has justly served as the physical embodiment of the area’s commitment to law and order. Its anniversary is a tremendous source of pride and an excellent reflection not only on the building itself but also on all those who have sustained it and who have given tirelessly of their time, energy, and support to this part of Ohio’s history; and WHEREAS, The Hocking County Courthouse has certainly become an emblem of the area’s rich heritage. In this modern era, in which the durability of goods and traditions is often measured in months and days rather than in years and decades, it is enlightening to discover that this venerable structure still stands for future generations as a testament to past achievements; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Hocking County Courthouse on its One Hundredth Anniversary and look to its future with optimism; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Hocking County Courthouse.

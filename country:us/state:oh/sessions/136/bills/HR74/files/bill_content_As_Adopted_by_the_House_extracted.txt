As Adopted by the House 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 74 2025-2026 Representative John A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Heritage Hill Farm on its Bicentennial. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to pay tribute to Heritage Hill Farm on the celebration of its Two Hundredth Anniversary; and WHEREAS, This is an important occasion, and it provides an ideal time to acknowledge the exceptional success and longevity of Heritage Hill Farm. Purchased in 1825 by John E. Brubaker and currently in its sixth generation of family ownership, it has grown to include dairy, corn, soy beans, wheat, oats, rye, and alfalfa, and in an effort to elevate the quality of its land, Heritage Hill Farm underwent a reforestation project in 2002, which involved the planting of 30,000 native hardwood trees. In addition, the operation has worked with regional state foresters to improve the surrounding forests, and clearly, it has grown into a truly noteworthy and exemplary business; and WHEREAS, All those associated with Heritage Hill Farm, including its owner, David Charles Winger, are to be commended for their adherence to the highest ideals and standards of the agricultural industry, and we are truly encouraged to recognize that our state’s future lies in the capable hands of conscientious Ohioans such as those who are involved with Heritage Hill Farm. Indeed, we are certain that they will not only continue to display the same vision and commitment for which they have become known but also that their efforts will advance the venture’s impressive contributions to the community; and WHEREAS, On this milestone, we praise Heritage Hill Farm for enhancing the quality of farming throughout Mansfield. As this esteemed operation maintains its unwavering dedication to service and achievement, it is certain to continue to grow, prosper, and follow in the tradition of excellence that has long been its hallmark, and we look with optimism to its promising future; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, congratulate Heritage Hill Farm on its Two Hundredth Anniversary and salute it as a fine Ohio endeavor; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Heritage Hill Farm.

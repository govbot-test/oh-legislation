sr395_01_AS As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 395 2025-2026 Senator Romanchuk A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring the Shelby High School boys 4x100-meter relay team on winning the 2026 Division III State Championship. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend the Shelby High School boys 4x100-meter relay team as the 2026 Division III State Champion; and WHEREAS, The Shelby High School boys 4x100-meter relay team has proven to be a talented and superbly conditioned squad and has gained the respect of fans and opponents alike for the spirited and highly motivated performance of its members. Throughout the season, team members Brady Bowman, Brayden DeVito, Renner Gano, and Gavin Baker worked diligently to improve their skills on the track, and as a result, the Whippets accomplished their goals, bringing home this year's state championship with a record-setting time of 41.30; and WHEREAS, Known as a driven and determined team, the members of the Shelby High School boys 4x100-meter relay team have developed into a remarkably efficient group of runners whose skill is equaled only by their exemplary display of teamwork. They are a credit not only to their families and their school but also to the superb leadership of their coaches; and WHEREAS, The road to victory is a difficult one, requiring ability, endurance, and a positive attitude on the part of runners and coaches alike. The members of the Shelby High School boys 4x100‑meter relay team have exemplified all of the characteristics that are essential for success both in and out of competition, including courage, discipline, integrity, and good sportsmanship, and their efforts have earned them the admiration of all those who have witnessed these outstanding athletes in action; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, salute the Shelby High School boys 4x100-meter relay team on its 2026 Division III State Championship and extend best wishes for the years to come; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Shelby High School boys 4x100-meter relay team.

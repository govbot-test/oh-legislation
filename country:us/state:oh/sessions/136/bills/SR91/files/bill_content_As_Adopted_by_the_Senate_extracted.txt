Section 1:
As Adopted by the Senate 136th General Assembly Regular Session S. R. No. 91 2025-2026 Senator Schaffer A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Maddox Laymon as a 2025 Division II State Wrestling Champion. WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to commend Maddox Laymon on winning the 2025 Division II State Championship in boys wrestling in the 120-pound weight class; and WHEREAS, A diligent and capable individual, Maddox Laymon is deserving of recognition for his exemplary accomplishments as a standout member of the Sheridan High School boys wrestling team. An exceptional athlete, he defeated a reigning state champion from Bexley with a pin in the third period to win first place in the 120-pound weight class at this year’s Division II State Tournament, becoming the first freshman in Muskingum Valley League history not only to go to the state championship but also to win it, and his noteworthy record of personal achievement stands as a testament to his dedication; and WHEREAS, Through his participation in wrestling, Maddox Laymon has learned the important lessons of discipline, perseverance, hard work, and good sportsmanship that will undoubtedly be of great benefit to him throughout his life. Indeed, he has inspired countless people to excel in various areas of endeavor, and his outstanding efforts serve as an example worthy of emulation; and WHEREAS, In a society that seeks to teach the future leaders of our state and nation the fundamentals of self-reliance and the rewards of individual effort, athletic competition has become a valuable educational tool. With sports contributing to the development of countless youth, we are happy to congratulate such a fine wrestler as Maddox Laymon; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, applaud Maddox Laymon on his Division II State Wrestling Championship and extend best wishes for continued success; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Maddox Laymon.

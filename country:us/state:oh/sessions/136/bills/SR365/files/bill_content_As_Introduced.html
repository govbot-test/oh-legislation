A R E S O L U T I O N

Honoring the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team as the 2026 NCSAA Division 5C champion.

WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team on winning the 2026 National Christian Schools Athletic Association Division 5C Championship; and

WHEREAS, A remarkable group of young athletes, the members of the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team have combined talent and spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish themselves as truly exceptional competitors. With admirable dedication, the Eagles have worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in basketball, and as a result, they defeated a tough Barry County Christian team by the score of 47-43 to win this year's NCSAA Division 5C Championship and finished the season with an impressive 23-7 record; and

WHEREAS, The Eagles' achievements and leadership are a justifiable source of pride and an excellent reflection on the players, their families, their school, their community, and their coach, Nick Hinkle. In leading his team to victory, Coach Hinkle has not only helped the athletes develop their talents to the fullest but also instilled in them a valuable sense of determination, perseverance, camaraderie, and Christian values; and

WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important role in the total education of our young people, we are extremely pleased to pay tribute to these exemplary athletes and their outstanding coach for a job well done. The maturity and experience the team members have gained through their participation in sports will greatly aid them in meeting the challenges of the future; therefore be it

RESOLVED, That we, the members of the Senate of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team on its tremendous season and salute the members and coach as some of Ohio's finest citizens; and be it further

RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the Senate transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to the Wooster Christian School boys basketball team.

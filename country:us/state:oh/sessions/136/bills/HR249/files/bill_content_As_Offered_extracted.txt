Section 1:
As Offered 136th General Assembly Regular Session H. R. No. 249 2025-2026 Representatives Brownlee, Baker A R E S O L U T I O N Honoring Norman G. Boyd as the 2025 National Big Brother of the Year. WHEREAS, The members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio are pleased to extend special recognition to Norman G. Boyd on being named the 2025 National Big Brother of the Year by Big Brothers Big Sisters of America; and WHEREAS, The National Big Brother of the Year designation is given to an individual who embodies the spirit of volunteerism and mentoring through the Big Brothers Big Sisters program, and this prestigious honor is a fitting tribute to Norm Boyd for his exemplary work to champion his little, Tyjon, for more than nine years. The respect and admiration he has earned are clearly evident, and his accomplishments are a justifiable source of pride and an outstanding reflection not only on him but also on his family and his community; and WHEREAS, A retired engineer and a former youth soccer coach for the Greater Sycamore Soccer Association, Norm Boyd is truly a remarkable individual, for he has combined civic concern and dedication with selfless initiative to become a dynamic leader throughout his community. His unfaltering devotion to service and achievement have distinguished him as a conscientious and hard-working Ohioan, and we are certain that he will continue to display the same unwavering commitment to excellence for which he has become known; and WHEREAS, Willingly giving of his time, energy, and abilities, Norm Boyd has striven to better the world around him, and through his generous contributions, he has gained the gratitude and esteem of many. We are proud to note that it is through the unceasing efforts of people such as he that the State of Ohio remains effective in meeting the needs of its residents; therefore be it RESOLVED, That we, the members of the House of Representatives of the 136th General Assembly of Ohio, in adopting this Resolution, commend Norman G. Boyd as the 2025 National Big Brother of the Year and salute him as one of our state’s finest citizens; and be it further RESOLVED, That the Clerk of the House of Representatives transmit a duly authenticated copy of this Resolution to Norman G. Boyd.
